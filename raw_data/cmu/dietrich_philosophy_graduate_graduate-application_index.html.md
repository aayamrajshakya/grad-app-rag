# Graduate Application
Applications for [doctorate](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/philosophy/graduate/phd/index.html) (Logic, Computation, & Methodology; Pure & Applied Logic; or Philosophy), [Master of Science](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/philosophy/graduate/masters/index.html), and [Master of Arts](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/philosophy/graduate/masters/index.html) degrees must include:  

  * Graduate application ([online](https://apply-dc-mcs.mcs.cmu.edu/apply/))
  * A sample of original written work, such as a paper or scientific report
  * Personal research statement/statement of purpose
  * Three (or more) letters of recommendation
  * Transcripts from all previously attended colleges or universities
  * GRE General test scores. We accept scanned copies of unofficial score reports. We recognize that for some applicants the GRE imposes a significant hardship. If you have concerns, please reach out to the Director of Graduate Studies to request a GRE waiver. 
  * For non-native English speaking applicants, we require scores from one of the following tests: TOEFL, IELTS, or the [Duolingo English Language Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/). 
  * There is no application fee for applying to any of the graduate programs in the department of philosophy.


Scanned versions of official transcripts, GRE score reports, and TOEFL/IELTS scores can be uploaded in lieu of sending paper copies. Students are encouraged to determine if they qualify for a [GRE fee waiver](https://www.ets.org/gre/score-users/reducing-barriers/fee-reductions.html).
