# Tuition Rates
Tuition rates vary between Masters and PhD programs.
# Please Note:
  * Technology Fee, Student Activities Fee, and Transportation Fee are standard required fees.
  * Graduate tuition rates for summer session are set by each college/school.
  * The above tuition rate is for reference only. The actual tuition rate may vary depending upon the department or program of study. For the actual tuition rate for your department or program of study, please reference your student account on [SIO](https://s3.andrew.cmu.edu/sio/index.html#home) or follow up with your departmental administrator.
