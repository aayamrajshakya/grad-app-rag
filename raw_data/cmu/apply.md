# ETC On-Line Application Information
Thank you for your interest in applying to Carnegie Mellon University's Entertainment Technology Center (ETC)! The application process is completed online and submitted electronically for your convenience. You do not have to complete the online application in one sitting.   
  
As you complete each section of your application, please keep in mind that the majority of the application items are submitted online, while others must be submitted directly to the Entertainment Technology Center. You will be required to submit both your essay question responses and a current resume online. If you are submitting a portfolio, the link/url to your original work must also be submitted online through the application.   
  
We must receive your official test scores directly from the testing agency, and we must receive official transcripts directly from each institution listed on your application. 
For official transcripts, we strongly prefer an electronic submissionAgain, all official documents must arrive directly from the university or testing agency.
The individuals you designated on your application to write letters of recommendation on your behalf will be notified directly by email. They will receive instructions on how to submit their recommendation letter electronically to our office, and you will receive a confirmation once it is received. You are required to have three letters of recommendation, and you must enter the names and contact information for each provider who can attest to your academic and/or professional qualifications.   
  
When ready to submit your application, you will be notified of “missing information” on the application review page. Please note, you may complete that information by clicking on the section where it resides in the application. You do not have to click through the entire application. Additionally, test scores must be OFFICIAL, so those will be updated when we receive scores from the testing agencies  
  
We wish you luck with the process and look forward to reviewing your application!
