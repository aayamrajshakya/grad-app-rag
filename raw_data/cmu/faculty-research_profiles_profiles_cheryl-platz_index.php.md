# Cheryl Platz
* * *
## Director of Experience Design, Marvel Strike Force (Scopely, Inc.)
Cheryl is a product designer, game developer, author, and actress best known for her work on Amazon's Alexa, the Echo Look, Cortana, the Sims family of games, the Riot Games + Xbox Game Pass partnership, and Microsoft's Azure platform. Prior to her current role as Director of XD for Marvel Strike Force at Scopely, Inc, Cheryl was Director of User Experience for the Player Platform at Riot Games. Her book, "Design Beyond Devices: Creating Multimodal, Cross-Device Experiences," (Rosenfeld Media, 2020) is a “manual for those who aspire to design the starship bridge of their dreams."
