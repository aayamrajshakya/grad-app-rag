# Pittsburgh Campus
As an MSCF student at our Pittsburgh location, you will enjoy all of the amenities of a world-class research university. Carnegie Mellon's library, gym, eateries and other facilities are a short walk from your classrooms. You will also have direct access to most of the professors teaching in the program and greater access to non-MSCF courses at CMU compared to the NYC location. 
Nestled in the heart of Oakland, Carnegie Mellon University sits adjacent to Schenley Park, with its more than 4,450 acres of trails and access to outdoor activities. If city life is more your style, downtown Pittsburgh is just a few miles away. Recognized as one of the [most livable cities in America](http://tepper.cmu.edu/prospective-students/think-pittsburgh/pittsburgh-rocks), Pittsburgh is known for it’s [great food](http://tepper.cmu.edu/prospective-students/think-pittsburgh/places-to-eat), [outdoor adventure](http://tepper.cmu.edu/prospective-students/think-pittsburgh/things-to-do), history and entertainment along its 35 miles of urban waterfront. 
Enjoy [discounted tickets](http://culturaldistrict.org/group-sales/cmu/) to performances, free access to the museums and free Port Authority bus and subway transportation with your [Carnegie Mellon ID](https://www.cmu.edu/idplus/services/artspass.html).
With 90 [neighborhoods](http://tepper.cmu.edu/prospective-students/think-pittsburgh/where-to-live) from which to choose, you will find many affordable living options. [Schedule your on-site visit to Pittsburgh now!](https://www.cmu.edu/mscf/_archive/visit-us/index.html)

# Things to do:
  * Walk the [trails](http://friendsoftheriverfront.org/trails/three-rivers-heritage-trail/)
  * Watch the [Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra](http://www.pittsburghsymphony.org/pso_home), [Pittsburgh Ballet Theatre](http://pbt.org/) and [Pittsburgh Opera](http://www.pittsburghopera.org/)
  * Explore the [Pittsburgh Cultural District](http://culturaldistrict.org/)
  * Stop by the [Carnegie Museum of Natural History](http://www.carnegiemnh.org/),
  * Experience [Phipps Conservatory](https://phipps.conservatory.org/) and the [Pittsburgh Zoo ](http://www.pittsburghzoo.org/)
  * Quarterly [Gallery Crawl](http://trustarts.culturaldistrict.org/event/2522/gallery-crawl-in-the-cultural-district) in the Cultural District
  * Ice skate in PPG Plaza
  * Ski at [Seven Springs](http://www.7springs.com/) or [Hidden Valley](http://www.hiddenvalleyresort.com/)
  * Visit the [Carnegie Museum of Art](http://www.cmoa.org/), the [Frick Pittsburgh](http://www.thefrickpittsburgh.org/index.php). the [Andy Warhol Museum](http://www.warhol.org/) and the [Mattress Factory](http://www.mattress.org/)
  * Attend a local sporting event
