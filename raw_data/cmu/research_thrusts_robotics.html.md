From stretchable electronics to miniature mobile robots, CMU engineers are collaborating across disciplines to create the next generation of robotics for human interaction. Our researchers are working to develop and improve soft and wearable robotics, legged and aerial robots, medical robotics, micro/nano-robotics, underwater robots, and IoT robots.
