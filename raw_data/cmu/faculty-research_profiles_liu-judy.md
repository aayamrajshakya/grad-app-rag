# Judy Liu
* * *
## Director of Business Solutions, DTC at NBCUniversal
Judy Liu is the Director of Business Solutions, DTC at NBCUniversal
Judy oversees the strategic alignment and execution of large-scale technical programs across various streaming platforms, including Peacock and SkyShowtime. Before this role, she worked at various technology startups. While at WAV Media, the live-streaming destination for the music community, she developed programming strategy and coordinated content launches with partners like Mixmag and 88 Rising. She’s launched several original series through WAV, including the livestream music show Kickback, highlighting emerging artists in the L.A. area. Judy was previously the Music Partnerships Manager at Flipagram, a digital video creator’s network, a precursor to TikTok. Her role involved managing relations with record labels, management companies, and social media agencies to implement new release marketing campaigns. In this position, she worked with many artists, including BIA, Carly Rae Jepsen, and Pharrell Williams.
