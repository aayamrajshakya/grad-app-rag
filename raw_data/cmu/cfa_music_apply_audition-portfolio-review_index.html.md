# Pre-screen, Audition & Portfolio Information
An audition, interview or portfolio review is required for admission to the School of Music. Students will submit their materials through the School of Music [Acceptd application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic). 
If you have any questions please feel free to contact the Office of Recruitment & Enrollment.
