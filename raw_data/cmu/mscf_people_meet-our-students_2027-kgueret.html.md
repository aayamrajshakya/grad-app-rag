## Bio
Kaloyan Gueret graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, with a Bachelor of Science in Data Science in 2025. His academic experience as an advanced undergraduate in Applied Statistics, along with exposure to C++/Python programming, complements his interest in Quantitative Research and Trading. Kaloyan held internships across Asset Management at a Real Estate Investment Trust, Quantitative Research in Investments, and Sales & Trading at a Fixed-Income Dealer. These diverse experiences, along with extracurricular projects in Machine Learning, Swing Trading, and Strategic Analysis, allowed him to strengthen his technical acumen, team cohesiveness, and discipline. Kaloyan joined MSCF to collaborate with like-minded peers, deepen his computational skill set, and prepare for a career in quantitative finance.
