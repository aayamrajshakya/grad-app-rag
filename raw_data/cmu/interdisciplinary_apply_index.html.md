# Admission
Students may enter the BCSA, BESA, BHA or BSA program in one of two ways: as an incoming first-year student or as an internal transfer student. Over half of the students enter the programs as internal transfer candidates. Internal transfer students have the same admission requirements as incoming freshmen.
EA additional major candidates in the College of Engineering may seek admission after beginning their major.
