# Meet Our Students
MSCF students are bright, passionate professionals—top-notch performers who come from a variety of educational and professional backgrounds. We invite you to learn about who they are; the schools they attended, the degrees they earned and how they’re using the MSCF program to prepare for rewarding careers in quantitative finance.
If you have a question after reading a bio, feel free to reach out to a student via email. Every one of our students is open to sharing their insights and answering your questions.
