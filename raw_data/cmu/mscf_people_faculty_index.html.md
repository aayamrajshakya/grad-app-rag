# Meet Our Faculty
MSCF faculty are more than exceptional scholars — they are dedicated mentors, passionate educators, and active researchers. Alongside our full-time, tenure-track professors, you’ll learn from accomplished adjunct instructors and industry guest lecturers who bring fresh perspectives and current market insights directly into the classroom. Together, they ensure you master both the rigorous theory and the practical skills needed to excel in your career.

##  Fikret Kalagoglu 
### Bio
Fikret Kalagoglu joined BAM in January 2023 as a Macro Portfolio Manager, in our New York office. Prior to BAM, Fikret was with Moore Capital Management from 2004 to 2022. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics from SUNY at Stony Brook, as well as a Master's in Computational Finance from Carnegie Mellon University. He has been a CFA charterholder since 2003.
