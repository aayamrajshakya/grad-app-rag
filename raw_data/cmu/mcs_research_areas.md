# Areas of Research at Mellon College of Science
Powerful cross-disciplinary teams focus on emerging areas with the potential for world-changing breakthroughs for profound public benefit. Our faculty experts and thought leaders are not only contributing to the future — they are building it, with unrivaled insight and impact.
## Breakthroughs That Matter
Carnegie Mellon University research is redefining scientific disciplines and making the kinds of discoveries that change how we live, connect and protect the world around us. And at every level — undergraduate, graduate and doctoral — students have powerful learning opportunities, working alongside leading scientists to shape the future through research that matters.
