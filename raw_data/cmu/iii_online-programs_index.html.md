### Rigor & Reputation of Carnegie Mellon University
Earn a degree that carries the full prestige and academic rigor of Carnegie Mellon University. Our online program features the same challenging curriculum as our on-campus offering, with the only difference being the flexible online format.
### Flexible & Stackable Structure
Balance your career and education with a part-time learning structure designed specifically for working professionals. Pursue a single certificate for specialized skills, or stack all four certificates plus the capstone course to earn a master’s degree.
### Project-Driven   
Interdisciplinary Curriculum
Learn and apply innovation frameworks to develop solutions for complex problems. Through a project-driven, collaborative curriculum, the online program provides students with concepts they can immediately apply in the workplace.
### Collaborative  
Classroom Experience
Be an active contributor, not a passive observer. Our collaborative learning environment mirrors a professional setting, providing ample opportunities to work in diverse teams.
### Ambitious & Driven   
Student Traits
Surround yourself with ambitious classmates from varied backgrounds. A shared dedication to professional improvement makes for a rigorous yet rewarding learning experience.
### Supportive  
Online Community
Join a supportive online community where you can build meaningful connections and receive personalized attention from faculty and staff.
## iii Online Advantages
### Study Without Sacrificing Your Career
Balance work, family, and education seamlessly. Attend live classes and engage with peers who understand the demands of a full-time career. You'll be learning and applying new skills while maintaining your professional momentum.
### Earn Credentials That Open Doors
Every course completed adds to your Carnegie Mellon University transcript. Stack individual certificates for immediate career impact, or combine them toward a full master's degree. 
### Access World-Class Curriculum
Engage with a globally recognized curriculum designed by industry experts. You'll connect with a global cohort of ambitious professionals, building lasting relationships with classmates, and faculty.

## Am I a Good Fit?
The iii's online coursework attracts career-minded individuals who are looking for a flexible learning structure that allows them to go to school and continue working full time.
Our online programs are ideal for engineers, designers, and business professionals looking to push the boundaries of what’s possible to create the next generation of products & services. 
### Common career paths include:
  * Architectural Designer, Product Design, Design Engineering, Product Management, Design Strategy Consulting, Director of Client Services, Head of IT, Product Development, UX Design, User Research, Manufacturing Engineer, Program Manager, Software Engineer, Quality Manager, Experience Design Leader.
