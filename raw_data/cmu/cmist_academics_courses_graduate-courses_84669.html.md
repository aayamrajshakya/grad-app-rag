# Decision Science for International Relations
## Course Number: 84-669 
Decision science studies how people make decisions, in order to learn from their wisdom and help them to do better. The course focuses decision science on high-stakes decisions in health, safety, environment, technology, and national security. It recognizes that experts are people, too, and examines the strengths and weaknesses of their judgments and analyses. It provides opportunities to think together about these challenging decisions.
