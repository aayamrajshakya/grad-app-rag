# Academic Advisors
Developing your course plan can be a daunting task due to the flexibility and options within each INI program. Your academic advisor is here to help with:
  * Course choices and academic direction
  * Developing a plan that best fits your interests, needs and goals
  * Petition, proposal and technical report review and approval
  * Advising on research topics for projects


## Your academic advisor is available by appointment.
