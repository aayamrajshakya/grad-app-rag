# Ji Young Denick
* * *
## Founder and Managing Partner, MineMR
Ji Young is the Founder and Managing Partner of MineMR, a market research company. MineMR empowers business through research, helping clients make strategic business decisions with confidence based on data. 
Ji Young holds two masters degrees in Arts Management and Entertainment Industry Management. While pursuing her Masters degrees, she worked at Twentieth Television, Inc. and Warner Bros Television, where she discovered her passion for research. 
Prior to starting her own company, she built her career at Hall & Partners and Kelton where she executed over 50 research projects for Fortune 500 companies including Microsoft, Target, Caesars Entertainment, Nest among others.
