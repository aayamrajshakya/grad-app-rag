#### Great stories need great leaders
Combining the renowned creative power of CMU’s College of Fine Arts with the management and quantitative expertise of Heinz College, the dual-city Master of Entertainment Industry Management (MEIM)
As an Entertainment Industry Management student, you will spend your first year taking core technology, analytical, and leadership classes at CMU’s main campus in Pittsburgh and gaining exposure to the industry, including a networking weekend in New York City. You’ll then spend your entire second year in Los Angeles
