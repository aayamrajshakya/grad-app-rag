## Bio
Yvette Li graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2024 with a B.S. in Economics & Mathematical Sciences and a minor in Computational Finance. During her undergraduate studies, Yvette researched on pricing Barrier Options with both Binomial Model and Black-Scholes Model. Her research helped her to join America's most prestigious honor society Phi Beta Kappa. She was active in several professional clubs which helps her to further explore her knowledge in financial markets and to train her communication skills. Upon completion of her MSCF degree, Yvette plans to pursue a career in quantitative trading, portfolio management, or strats & modeling.
