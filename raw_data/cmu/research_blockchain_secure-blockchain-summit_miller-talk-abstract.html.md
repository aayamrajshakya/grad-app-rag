Talk title
Reclaiming social media with TEE and OAuth3
Abstract
I'll discuss Teleport, an initiative to take back agency over our attention spans and our siloed platform data through "information diet apps" using secure account delegation backed by TEEs.
Bio
Andrew Miller is a product manager at Teleport, a startup incubated by Flashbots[X]. He is also Adjunct Associate Professor at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, in Electrical and Computer Engineering and affiliate in Computer Science, as wellas an Associate Director of the Initiative for Cryptocurrencies and Contracts (IC3), and a board member of the Zcash Foundation. He received his Ph.D. from the University of Maryland Cybersecurity Center.
