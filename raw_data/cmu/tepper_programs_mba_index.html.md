# Master of Business Administration (MBA)
Accelerate your career trajectory and unlock your leadership potential with an MBA from the Tepper School of Business. Join a success-focused MBA community with a record for producing business leaders who drive innovation across the global marketplace.

## Why Choose the Tepper School MBA?
The Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree program from the Tepper School of Business empowers you to lead with confidence in complex, uncertain environments.
With a STEM-designated curriculum, one-on-one leadership coaching, and an emphasis on creative teamwork, the Tepper School MBA program develops you into the tenacious, imaginative, and collaborative powerhouse that top companies know they will find at CMU.
### Key Features of the Master of Business Administration Program
  * Highly Ranked for High-Impact Careers:[our rankings](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/rankings) reflect the achievements of our students and follow them throughout their [careers](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/careers).
  * Three Formats, One MBA Experience:[full-time](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/full-time) or [online hybrid](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/online-hybrid) format, each of which has an option for an [accelerated track](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/accelerated). The MBA program is the same — faculty, rigor, cohort, and support — so you don’t have to sacrifice quality for your schedule.
  * Cutting-Edge Curriculum:[MBA curriculum](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum) to your goals with [12 concentrations](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum/concentrations), our [STEM designation](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum), [global opportunities](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum/global-study), and [leadership development](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/graduate-services/accelerate-leadership-center).

## CMU MBA Formats
At the Tepper School, you’ll find academic and support standards and a rigorous curriculum across all formats.
### Full-Time MBA
The Tepper School Full-Time MBA program offers an intimate, immersive setting defined by team-based work, cohorts that embrace diversity, and a world-class analytics-based curriculum focused on solving society’s biggest challenges.
  * On-site at Carnegie Mellon’s campus in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  * September-May
  * Summer internship support
  * Monday-Thursday class schedule
  * Full range of career services
  * Designed for two to eight years of work experience
  * 21-month duration
  * [Full-Time MBA class profile](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/admissions/ft-class-profile)


You can also pursue the [Full-Time MBA in an accelerated track](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/accelerated) if you have previously completed undergraduate business coursework. This option allows you to complete the Full-Time MBA in 16 months with an internship or 12 months without.
###  [Full-Time MBA](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/full-time)  
  
Online Hybrid MBA
The Tepper School Online Hybrid MBA delivers the same rigor and teamwork-focused curriculum in a flexible, part-time, STEM-designated format — built for the future of business.
  * Flexible online courses
  * Year-round coursework
  * Immersive Access Weekends
  * Full range of career services
  * 32-month duration
  * [Online Hybrid class profile](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/admissions/oh-class-profile)


If you have completed undergraduate business coursework, you can also pursue the part-time Online Hybrid [MBA in an accelerated format](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/accelerated). This pathway can be completed in 24 months, saving on costs and streamlining the curriculum for eligible students.

## MBA Program Details
Find out why the Tepper School MBA gives you a formidable advantage over the competition.
###  STEM-Designated MBA Curriculum
Our [STEM MBA curriculum](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum) prepares you to harness the science of management by utilizing emerging technologies and data analysis to make better business decisions. This unique designation reflects the program’s [academic approach](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum) to management science, enhanced by its focus on quantitative skill-building.  
  
Across every industry, employers are seeking analytical thinkers and decision-makers who know how to lead and solve problems in a business environment infused with technology. The [STEM designation](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum) is a sought-after addition to your MBA degree that shows employers you have the skills to lead in tech-centered environments.  
  
The STEM MBA offers an added benefit for international students. F1-visa students graduating from STEM MBA programs may be eligible to apply for a 24-month extension to their post-completion Optional Practical Training.  
  
[Learn why a STEM MBA matters](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum).
###  Small Community, Strong Connections
At the Tepper School of Business at [Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.cmu.edu/campus-life), you’ll pursue bold goals in an intimate setting defined by small, team-based classes. In our [close-knit community](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/life-at-tepper/journey.html), success is collective — it depends on students coming together to learn from diverse perspectives and skill sets. This environment fuels collaborative creativity that illuminates innovative strategies.
You’ll grow personally and professionally through ample opportunities to connect outside the classroom. Take a [global trek](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum/global-study) to Dubai, Japan, or South Africa. Join one of [40+ student clubs](https://tartanconnect.cmu.edu/club_signup). Share in a dynamic meeting of the minds in a case competition, or [explore Pittsburgh](https://www.cmu.edu/campus-life/living-in-pittsburgh) and its burgeoning foodie scene. Here, you’ll build a lasting professional network and make memories that stay with you for life.
###  Career Opportunities With an MBA
It is fair to say we have a pretty good track record when it comes to career placement. Tepper School MBA graduates commonly land top roles in consulting, technology, finance, marketing, and other functions across industries. Recent cohorts have achieved [100% internship placement](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/careers/internship-highlights) and secured [an average starting salary of $151,215, and an average total compensation of $189,275](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/careers/compensation-highlights).
The Tepper School’s career services are among the best in the world, with the [Masters Career Center](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/graduate-services/masters-career-center) ranking highly in the U.S. and abroad by the Financial Times. We provide a portfolio of exceptional leadership development services, including one-on-one career counseling with subject matter experts, recruiter relationships with more than 200 companies, career treks to major cities and headquarters, and so much more.  
  
[Learn more about Tepper School MBA career outcomes](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/careers).
###  Entrepreneurship at the Tepper School and the Swartz Center
The entrepreneurial spirit is strong among Tepper School MBA students, who have launched hundreds of startups, including ModCloth and Duolingo. The Swartz Center for Entrepreneurship is our hub for innovation, helping MBA students bring promising ideas to the global marketplace.
Entrepreneurial education is woven into every aspect of our MBA program. Our renowned [Entrepreneurship MBA track](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/mba/curriculum/tracks/entrepreneurship-in-organizations) offers two study options: High Growth Entrepreneurship and Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition. Our dedicated services for entrepreneurial careers mean extra preparation, expertise, and networking are available to help you launch and scale your best ideas.
[Explore the Swartz Center for Entrepreneurship](https://www.cmu.edu/swartz-center-for-entrepreneurship/index.html).
