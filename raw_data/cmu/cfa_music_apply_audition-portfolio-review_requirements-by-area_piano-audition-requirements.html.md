# Piano Audition Requirements
## Undergraduate
Piano applicants are required to submit a pre-screening video recorded audition. 
#### PRE-SCREENING REQUIREMENTS:
The length of the recording must be between 15 and 20 minutes
#### REQUIRED REPERTOIRE:
  * A Baroque or Classical work (From 17th century to Beethoven)
  * A Romantic, a 20th Century or a Contemporary work demonstrating the applicant’s pianistic and artistic ability
  * Only works for solo piano (not concertos) are accepted


Please see [Pre-screening Instructions](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/pre-screening-materials.html) for details on how to submit your pre-screening. Media must be uploaded to the School of Music [online application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) by December 1. Only complete applications, fulfilling the pre-screening requirements, are considered.
#### LIVE AUDITION REQUIREMENTS:
All undergraduate applicants to the piano program are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * A polyphonic work (late-Renaissance to contemporary)
  * One or more movements of a Classical Sonata (Haydn, Mozart, Clementi, Beethoven or Schubert)
  * A fast étude by Chopin, Liszt, Scriabin, Rachmaninoff, Debussy, or Prokofiev
  * A Romantic work demonstrating the applicant’s pianistic and artistic ability
  * A 20th-century or contemporary composition demonstrating the applicant’s pianistic and artistic ability


Only works for solo piano (not concertos) are accepted
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. 
## Graduate
Piano applicants are required to submit a pre-screening video recording. 
PRE-SCREENING REQUIREMENTS:  
The length of the recording must be at least 20 minutes
REQUIRED REPERTOIRE:
  * A Baroque or Classical work (From 17th century to Beethoven)
  * A Romantic, 20th Century, or Contemporary work demonstrating the applicant’s pianistic and artistic ability
  * Only works for solo piano (not concertos) are accepted


Please see [Pre-screening Instructions](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/pre-screening-materials.html) for details on how to submit your pre-screening. Media must be uploaded to the School of Music [online application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) by December 1. Only complete applications, fulfilling the pre-screening requirements, are considered.
LIVE AUDITION REQUIREMENTS: 
All graduate applicants to the piano program are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * A polyphonic work (late-Renaissance to contemporary)
  * One or more movements of a Classical Sonata (Haydn, Mozart, Clementi, Beethoven or Schubert)
  * A fast étude by Chopin, Liszt, Scriabin, Rachmaninoff, Debussy, or Prokofiev
  * A major Romantic work (e.g., a sonata by Chopin, Schumann, Brahms, Liszt, Scriabin, or Rachmaninoff, a Chopin Ballade or Scherzo, a cycle by Schumann, or any large-scale composition by Liszt) or a selection of characteristic pieces of contrasting moods (e.g., an opus of Chopin Mazurkas, selections from Liszt’s “Années de Pèlerinage” or Brahms’ Op. 116-119)
  * A 20th-century or contemporary work (from Debussy onward)
  * A virtuoso composition, such as an etude by Clementi (“Gradus ad Parnassum”), Czerny (Op. 740), Chopin, Paganini/Schumann, Liszt, Scriabin, Rachmaninoff, Debussy, Bartók, Prokofiev, Stravinsky, or Ligeti, or a toccata or toccata-like movement by Czerny, Schumann, Liszt, Saint-Saëns, Debussy, Ravel, Stravinsky, or Prokofiev


Only works for solo piano (not concertos) are accepted.
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition.

## Certificate
Piano applicants are required to submit a pre-screening video recorded audition. 
#### PRE-SCREENING REQUIREMENTS:
  * The length of the recording must be at least 20 minutes
  * Required Repertoire: 
    * A Baroque or Classical work (from 17th century to Beethoven)
    * A Romantic, 20th Century, or Contemporary work demonstrating the applicant’s pianistic and artistic ability
    * Only works for solo piano (not concertos) are accepted


Please see [Pre-screening Instructions](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/pre-screening-materials.html) for details on how to submit your pre-screening. Media must be uploaded to the School of Music [online application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) by December 1. Only complete applications, fulfilling the pre-screening requirements, are considered.
#### LIVE AUDITION REQUIREMENTS:
Candidates will be expected to perform an audition recital of 15-20 minutes, including a minimum of five works showing representative repertoire of various periods. Although there are no set requirements for this audition, please refer to the graduate audition requirements for guidelines.
