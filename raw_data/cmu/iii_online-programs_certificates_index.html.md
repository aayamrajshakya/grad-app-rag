## Designed with the working professional in mind.
You’re an innovator and design thinker. You're ready to advance your skills, but you're also busy with your career. Pursue any or all of our certificates on your own time, based on the skills you want to acquire. As you complete each step, you’ll have a Carnegie Mellon University transcript to show for it. Once a certificate is completed, you can return and earn more or work toward a master's degree as long as it's completed within five years of starting the process.
## Take your career to the next level with these certificates:
### [ Methods & Tools for Product Innovation   
Certificate   
](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/methods-tools-for-product-innovation.html)
**  
Hands-On Innovation Process**  
Students take ideas from concept to prototype using a fast-paced, project-based approach that mirrors real-world product development and emphasizes creativity, structure, and execution.
User-Centered Research  
Through practical ethnographic and qualitative methods, students build a research toolkit to uncover meaningful user insights, ask the right questions, and design with clarity and purpose.
Visual Communication Tools  
Learners gain visual storytelling skills—using sketching, UI design, dashboards, and pitch materials—to communicate ideas and insights in ways that drive understanding and action.
[ Curriculum Details ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/methods-tools-for-product-innovation.html#curriculum-details)
49-604: Innovation Process & Tools  
49-605: User Experience Research for Digital and Physical Products

Spring 2026, Summer 2026, Fall 2026
### [ New Product Management   
Certificate ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/new-product-management.html)
**  
Strategic Market Launch**  
You’ll gain the skills to create and execute comprehensive product launch plans, covering pricing strategies, market segmentation, and financial analysis.
Cross-Functional Leadership  
The program emphasizes mastering both product and brand management roles, teaching you to lead across marketing, engineering, finance, and manufacturing functions.
Hands-On Business Simulation  
Through interactive simulations, you'll learn how different business models and functional areas interconnect to inform strategic product decisions.  
  

[ Curriculum Details ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/new-product-management.html#curriculum-details)
49-606: Understanding Markets for Products & Services  
49-607: Product Strategy & Planning
Next Term Offered:  
Spring 2026, Fall 2026
### [ Product Design Innovation   
Certificate ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/product-design-innovation.html)
**  
Holistic Design Foundations**  
You’ll explore industrial, software, and UX/UI design techniques—such as free-form sketching, concept modeling, and prototyping—that enable turning user needs into tangible product ideas.
Full-Cycle Development Practice  
Through rigorous coursework and hands-on projects, you’ll learn to research, sketch, model, test, and present design concepts across physical and digital mediums.
Service & Experience Innovation  
The program equips you with frameworks and skills to design systems of products, services, and human interactions—ensuring comprehensive and impactful user experiences.
[ Curriculum Details ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/product-design-innovation.html#curriculum-details)
49-600: Introduction to Design Innovation  
49-601: Innovation of Services & Experiences
Next Term Offered:  
Spring 2026, Summer 2026
### [ Technology for Product Management   
Certificate ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/technology-for-product-management.html)
**  
Technical Proficiency**  
You’ll gain a deep understanding of current technologies, materials, and systems—including their capabilities and limitations—to inform smarter product decisions.
Engineering for Production & Sustainability  
Learn to apply engineering fundamentals—such as stress analysis, heat transfer, kinematics, and sustainable design—to create products optimized for manufacturability and lifecycle impact.
Connected & Intelligent Systems  
Learn to build AI-enhanced and IoT-connected devices. This certificate provides practical skills in data gathering, modeling, prototyping, and presenting technical information effectively.
[ Curriculum Details ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/certificates/technology-for-product-management.html#curriculum-details)
49-602: Designing for Production and Sustainability  
49-603: Cyber-Physical Product Design
Next Term Offered:  
Summer 2026, Fall 2026
## Your next career steps— at your pace.
Each certificate is comprised of two sections which may be taken concurrently or sequentially. This means that any certificate may be completed in either one (taking two sections) or two (taking just one section) semesters.
