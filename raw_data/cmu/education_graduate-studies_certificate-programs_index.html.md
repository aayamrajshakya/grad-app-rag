Carnegie Mellon University offers transformative educational experiences for graduate students and busy working professionals who want to master the innovative technologies that will take their careers to the next level.
Graduate certificates combine cutting-edge content taught by Carnegie Mellon faculty experts with a variety of proven interactive educational technologies. Together, they provide working professionals and graduate students with the flexibility they need to acquire some of today’s most highly sought-after skills.
**[Programs for working professionals](https://engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate-studies/certificate-programs/index.html#section-2) | [Programs for Ph.D. students](https://engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate-studies/certificate-programs/index.html#section-3)**
## For working professionals
[ AI Engineering Fundamentals Delivery mode:  This credit-bearing, graduate-level certificate program will enable engineers to use artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to generate more efficient, accurate, and productive engineering solutions. The 100% online nine-month program includes weekly 90-minute classes, which are held in the evening, and an optional weekly recitation session. ](https://www.cmu.edu/online/aimlmeche/index.html)
[ AI Engineering - Digital Twins & Analytics Delivery mode:  Digital twin technology is rapidly evolving, and engineering leaders need the most cutting-edge, widely respected approaches to stay ahead. In this program, engineers will learn the principles of digital twins and digital twin modeling, how to build a business case for digital twin adoption, and how to leverage the power of digital twins to harness data-driven insights and improve decision-making with predictive analytics. This 100% online 9-month program includes weekly, live-online classes with renowned CMU professors in the evening, and an optional weekly recitation session. ](https://www.cmu.edu/online/aie-dta/index.html)
[ 3D Bioprinting & Biofabrication Delivery mode:  This program’s unique, credit-bearing curriculum teaches the fundamentals of fabricating structurally complex 3D tissues. Physicians, biomedical engineers, and other medical professionals will acquire the skills needed to create bio-fabricated tissues and bring this actionable knowledge back to their organizations using easily accessible materials. The 100% online 12-month program includes weekly 90-minute classes, which are held in the evening, and an optional on-campus lab experience. ](https://www.cmu.edu/online/bme/index.html)
[ Integrated Innovation Institute certificates Delivery mode:  The Integrated Innovation Institute, which unites design, business, and engineering disciplines, offers four online certificates, which can be pursued individually or stacked towards a master’s degree. Certificates in Technology for Product Management Product Design InnovationMethods & Tools for Product Innovation New Product Management ](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/online-programs/index.html)
