#  Dr. Raja Sooriamurthi

Contact

## Bio
Dr. Raja Sooriamurthi is a Teaching Professor in the Information Systems Program at Carnegie Mellon University's Heinz College of Information Systems and Public Policy. He is also the Program Director for the Decision Analytics and Systems minor. Research interests include Artificial intelligence and cognitive science with an emphasis on case-based reasoning, knowledge management, distributed reasoning, machine learning, and systems thinking. Other interests include higher-order programming languages, software development, and computer science/information systems pedagogy.
