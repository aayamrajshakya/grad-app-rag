# Student Financial Services
### Government Shutdown & Financial Aid
As of October 1, 2025, the federal government has entered a shutdown. While this does not impact your access to federal financial aid or your ability to complete the FAFSA,[The HUB](https://www.cmu.edu/hub/contact/index.html) remains available to support you with financial aid questions or concerns.
# From applying for financial aid to understanding your invoice - our team is here to support you.
## Financing your education shouldn’t be complicated. Our expert team will provide personalized support to help you navigate forms, terminology, numbers, and deadlines associated with financial aid, billing and payments, and more. Together, we’ll create a plan that makes sense for you and your family’s unique financial circumstances.
