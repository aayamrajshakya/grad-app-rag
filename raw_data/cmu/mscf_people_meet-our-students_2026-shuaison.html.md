## Bio
Shuai is a pragmatic and detail-oriented problem solver, adept at pinpointing critical issues and devising effective solutions. Graduating in 2024, Shuai earned dual bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science and Finance from Tsinghua University, where he built a solid foundation in mathematics, programming, and financial markets. Beyond acquiring theoretical knowledge and practical engineering skills on campus, Shuai actively pursued hands-on industry experience across various market sectors, including Equities, Futures, Options, and Cryptocurrencies. His internships provided him with insights into a range of investment strategies and methodologies, such as Technical Timing, Factor Investing, Event-Driven, Market Making, Machine Learning, and High-Frequency Trading. Eager to further refine his quantitative skills and deepen his knowledge of global markets, Shuai joined MSCF. He is continually seeking opportunities to explore new markets and innovative techniques in the quantitative investment field.
