# Economics
Economics examines the behavior of markets, economies and their specific components. Research and study at the Tepper School include macroeconomic theory, banking and financial markets, fiscal and monetary policy, public finance, corporate governance, business cycles, labor market dynamics, microeconomic theory, contract theory and commerce.
