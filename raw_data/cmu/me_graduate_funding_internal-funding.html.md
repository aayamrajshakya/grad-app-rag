Today’s mechanical engineers are working across technologies and disciplines to solve society’s toughest challenges.
We answer the need for professional skills and talents with rigorous graduate programs that merge depth in fundamental mechanical engineering areas with breadth in emerging, interdisciplinary areas.
We balance classroom time with hands-on research and learning opportunities to give our students the ability to solve complex problems through innovation and collaboration.
### Our graduate programs offer:
  * flexibility to tailor your curriculum according to your interests and goals.
  * hands-on learning and collaborative problem-solving through project-based courses.
  * a balanced exposure to engineering and computer science.
  * ongoing services and programming to enhance communication, leadership, and team-building skills in the Carnegie Mellon and Pittsburgh ecosystems.
  * personalized career advising services and networking opportunities to ensure your long term professional success.
  * With approval from their academic advisor, PhD students can also earn a [certificate in engineering management, leadership, and innovation](https://engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate-studies/certificate-programs/engineering-management-leadership-innovation.html)


### Explore our online graduate certificates:
[Online graduate certificates](https://engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate-studies/certificate-programs/index.html) combine cutting-edge content taught by Carnegie Mellon faculty experts with a variety of proven interactive educational technologies. Together, they provide working professionals and graduate students with the flexibility they need to acquire some of today’s most highly sought-after skills. 
  * [AI Engineering Fundamentals](https://www.cmu.edu/online/aimlmeche/index.html) is a 100% online program that enables engineers to use AI and machine learning techniques to generate more efficient, accurate, and productive engineering solutions.
