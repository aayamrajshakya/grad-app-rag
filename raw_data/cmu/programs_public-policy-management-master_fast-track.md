# Earn your Public Policy master's degree in one year
* * *
You already know how you want to make an impact. You just need the skills to level up, and you don't want to put your career on hold to do it.
We've got you covered.
If you have three years or more of relevant professional experience*, our Master of Science in Public Policy and Management: Fast Track
With a degree from Carnegie Mellon University’s Heinz College, you will come out ready to use your skills to lead meaningful change.
