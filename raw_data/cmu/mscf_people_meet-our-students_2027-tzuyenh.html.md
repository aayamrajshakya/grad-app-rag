## Bio
Tzu-Yen (Yen) Hsu graduated from National Taiwan University with a dual major in Information Management and Finance, and a minor in Mathematics. He gained hands-on experience at Taipei Fubon Bank, where he built a Black-Litterman model with quantitative strategies for robo-advisors. At KGI Life Insurance, he developed Python-based pricing and risk models. He has also applied machine learning to bankruptcy prediction and ESG risk analysis in research studies. With a strong foundation in mathematics, programming, and finance, Yen joined MSCF to deepen his expertise in quantitative finance. After graduation, he aspires to pursue a career in quantitative research or risk management.
