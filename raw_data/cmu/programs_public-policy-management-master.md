## What public policy issues will i address?
At Heinz College, you will receive practical experience to complement your coursework through our Capstone projects, during which you'll solve a problem for real-life client. You will use your policy analysis and data analytics skills to improve social equity and data governance, contribute to the public service sector, and prepare for a career in public policy. Here are some of our past projects:
  * Optimizing efficiency of light-rail transit
  * Standardizing organ transplant handbooks nationwide
  * [Using large language models ](https://www.heinz.cmu.edu/media/2024/May/synergy-of-minds-future-of-ai.php)
  * Evaluating the economic impact of IT and cybersecurity education programs


  * [Policing illegal commercial fishing ](https://www.heinz.cmu.edu/media/2023/May/illegal-unregulated-unreported-fishing-capstone-project-national-geospatial-intelligence-agency.php)


  * [Enabling the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline to serve 20,000 additional callers](https://www.heinz.cmu.edu/media/2023/February/making-meaningful-impact-using-data-science-for-social-good.php)
  * Improving job outcomes
  * Creating shelter estimate models to aid natural disaster recovery
