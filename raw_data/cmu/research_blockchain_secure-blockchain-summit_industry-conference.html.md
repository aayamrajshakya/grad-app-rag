# CMU Blockchain Industry Conference
April 18, 2024
Location: Tepper Quad Third Floor, at Carnegie Mellon University's Swartz Center for Entrepreneurship
Presented by the Carnegie Mellon University Blockchain Group student organization, CMU's inaugural Blockchain Industry Conference will focus on real-world blockchain use cases and feature talks from industry practitioners.
## Presented by the CMU Blockchain Group
##  About | Schedule | Speakers | **[Organizers](https://www.cylab.cmu.edu/research/blockchain/secure-blockchain-summit/industry-conference.html#committee) **| Sponsors
## About
CMU's inaugural Blockchain Industry Conference will focus on real-world blockchain use cases and feature talks from industry practitioners. The conference will cover three main topics: Governance, DeFi, and AI & Blockchain Integration.
