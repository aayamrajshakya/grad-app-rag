# Olivia Barton
* * *
## Music Counsel at Epic Games
Olivia Barton is currently Music Counsel at Epic Games. Epic is a leading interactive entertainment company and also develops Unreal Engine, which powers many of the world’s leading games.
Olivia is currently Music Counsel at Epic Games. Epic is a leading interactive entertainment company and also develops Unreal Engine, which powers many of the world’s leading games and is also adopted across industries such as film and television, and broadcast and live events. Epic is best known for operating Fortnite, one of the world's largest games. Fortnite has featured artists like Snoop Dogg, Eminem, Sabrina Carpenter, Bruno Mars, Mariah Carey, and more.
Before Epic, Olivia worked in the sync licensing department at Sony Music where she represented numerous chart-topping artists such as Beyoncé, Adele, Harry Styles, The Chainsmokers, and Fifth Harmony in addition to representing the entire SME catalog for sync licensing such as Michael Jackson, Elvis Presley, and Jimi Hendrix.
Olivia graduated Magna Cum Laude from Syracuse University College of Law in December 2025.
