## Civil and Environmental Engineering (CEE) /   
Engineering and Public Policy (EPP) Ph.D. program
  * Students interested in the joint CEE/EPP Ph.D. degree program must successfully complete one year in EPP or CEE before applying to this program.
  * Current students in the EPP Ph.D. program will need to submit an application for the CEE Ph.D. program and must begin the joint CEE/EPP PhD program at least one year prior to their expected graduation date.
  * Candidates will be required to complete a comprehensive exam in both departments. Candidates will form one defense committee, complete one proposal exam, a final defense, and submit a dissertation.
