Our Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering with a Research Focus program is designed for students interested in pursuing careers in academia or research-based industries. In addition to traditional course-based education in your chosen [focus area](https://cee.engineering.cmu.edu/research/index.html), our MS research program teaches analysis skills, literature review, research question formulation, validation planning, and effective communication.
[Learn more about tuition and cost support.](https://cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/costs-funding.html)
You will work with a faculty advisor throughout the two-year program, conducting hands-on research in your chosen focus area of interest. Discover projects current M.S. in CEE - Research students are collaborating on and explore [CEE faculty](https://cee.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/index.html) to find an advisor that aligns with your reserch interests.
