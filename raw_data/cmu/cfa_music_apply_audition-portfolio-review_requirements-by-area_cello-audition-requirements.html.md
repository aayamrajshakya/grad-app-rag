# Cello Audition Requirements
Use the drop-down menus below to view the audition requirements for your degree/certificate area.
## Undergraduate
All undergraduate applicants to the Cello studio
  * Scales: two major and two minor scales in three octaves, of your choosing
  * One etude by Duport, Popper, or Piatti
  * A movement of a standard concerto
  * A prelude from one of the Bach Unaccompanied Suites


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._  

## Graduate
All graduate applicants to the Cello studio are expected to perform an audition recital of 15-20 minutes, including a minimum of five works showing representative repertoire of various periods. Although there are no set requirements for this audition, you are urged to choose: 
  * A solo piece
  * A movement from a concerto
  * Two orchestral excerpts
  * A movement from a Bach Suite
  * An etude that shows tone, musicianship and technique. 


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
## Certificate
All Advanced Music Studies Certificate (AMSC) applicants to the Cello studio are expected to perform an audition recital of 15-20 minutes, including a minimum of five works showing representative repertoire of various periods. Although there are no set requirements for this audition, please choose: 
  * A solo piece
  * A movement from a concerto
  * A movement of a Bach suite
  * Three orchestral excerpts
  * Etudes that show tone, musicianship and technique. 


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
