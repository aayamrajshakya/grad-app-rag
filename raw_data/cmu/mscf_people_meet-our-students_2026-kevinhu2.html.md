## Bio
Graduated from Cornell University in May 2024 with a major in Mathematics and minors in Computer Science and Psychology, Xinsheng (Kevin) is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computational Finance at Carnegie Mellon University. His passion for quantitative finance is supported by a robust academic foundation and diverse professional experiences. At Cornell, Xinsheng concentrated on Applied Mathematics, completing a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum such as PhD-level probability and Stochastic Calculus. Additionally, his background in Computer Science has equipped him with proficiency in programming languages including Python, Java, Ocaml, and Matlab. Professionally, Xinsheng gained valuable experience in fields ranging from quantitative research and asset management to cryptocurrency. These internship experiences allowed him to apply his academic knowledge to solving real-world financial problems. As an aspiring quant, Xinsheng is always eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore new opportunities.
