## Notes on Accompaniment for Auditions
### **Voice Auditions  
**
  * A collaborative pianist will be provided by CMU on-campus in Pittsburgh -  _Please note that outside collaborative pianists are not permitted at on-campus auditions; applicants should not bring their own pianist._
  * CMU will provide a collaborative pianist for the NYC regional audition day._NOTE:_
  * Your assigned collaborative pianist will reach out to you directly to finalize details regarding your music selection.
  * There will be no rehearsals.


### Instrumental Auditions
All instrumental auditions will be unaccompanied.
