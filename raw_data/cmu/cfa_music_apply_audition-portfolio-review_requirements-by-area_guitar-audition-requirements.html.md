# Guitar Audition Requirements
Use the drop-down menus below to view the audition requirements for your degree/certificate area.
## Undergraduate
Entering undergraduate Guitarists
All applicants to the guitar studio are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * Prepare one work from each of the following periods: 
    * Renaissance or Baroque (e.g. Dowland, Milan, Bach)
    * Classical or Romantic (e.g. Sor, Giuliani, Tarrega)
    * 20th century (e.g. Villa-Lobos, Brouwer, Dyens)


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
## Graduate
Graduates auditioning for the Guitar studio are expected to perform an audition recital of 15-20 minutes, including a minimum of five works showing representative repertoire of various periods. The audition should include:
  * One prelude or etude by Hector Villa-Lobos 
  * One Bach prelude from BWV 995, 996, 997, or 1006.


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
## Certificate
Those auditioning for the Advanced Music Studies Certificate (AMSC) in Guitar are expected to perform an audition recital of 15-20 minutes, including a minimum of five works showing representative repertoire of various periods. The audition should include: 
  * One prelude or etude by Hector Villa-Lobos 
  * One Bach prelude from BWV 995, 996, 997, or 1006.


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
