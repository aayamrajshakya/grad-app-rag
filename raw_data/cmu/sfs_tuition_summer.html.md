# 2025 Summer Tuition Rates
Summer tuition rates for Pittsburgh-campus undergraduates and visiting/non-degree students is charged at 60% of the regular fall/spring per-unit rates. There are no fees charged during the summer. 
## Undergraduate Tuition
Pittsburgh Per-Unit Tuition Rate - $539/unit
## Non-Degree/Visiting Tuition (for summer only)
Per-Unit Tuition Rate - $539/unit
