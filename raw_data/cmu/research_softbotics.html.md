# Softbotics
We are bringing robotics into everyday life by pioneering soft robotic materials and systems for motor assistance, teleoperation, interactive environments, and wearables. 
## Softbotics
* ### Wearables 
From sensorized artificial skin to stretchable electronics to prosthetic devices for robotic-assisted locomotion, our faculty are developing technologies for safe, minimally-restrictive human interaction.
Faculty involved: [Sarah Bergbreiter](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/bergbreiter-sarah.html), [Carmel Majid](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/majidi-carmel.html)i, [Burak Ozdoganlar](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/ozdoganlar-burak.html), [Victoria Webster-Wood](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/webster-wood-victoria.html), [Inseung Kang](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/kang-inseung.html), [Trevor Jones,](https://engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/jones-trevor.html) [Eni Halilaj](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/halilaj-eni.html)
* ### Bioinspired and biodegradable robots
Faculty are taking inspiration from nature to identify how to combine living materials, protein-based materials, and engineered materials to build larger scale, self-sustaining bio-actuators; and understand how to keep these materials alive in a real-world environment.
Faculty involved: [Carmel Majidi](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/majidi-carmel.html), [Vickie Webster-Wood](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/webster-wood-victoria.html),[ Reeja Jayan](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/jayan-reeja.html), [Trevor Jones](https://engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/jones-trevor.html)
Learn more.... [So tricky a robot can do it](https://engineering.cmu.edu/news-events/news/2023/08/14-defcon-win.html), [Now printing: seaweed-based, biodegradable actuators](https://engineering.cmu.edu/news-events/news/2023/06/14-seaweed-actuators.html)
* ### Liquid circuits and sensors
Faculty are fabricating liquid metal-based soft and stretchable circuits that hold much potential for various applications because of their ideal physical and chemical properties.
Faculty involved:[Carmel Majidi](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/majidi-carmel.html), [Burak Ozdoganlar](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/ozdoganlar-burak.html), [Trevor Jones](https://engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/jones-trevor.html)
Learn more...[Scalable manufacturing unlocks potential of soft electronics](https://engineering.cmu.edu/news-events/news/2023/08/28-scalable-manufacturing.html)
* ### Paleobionics
Paleobionics is a field aimed at using , robotics with flexible electronics and soft materials, to understand the biomechanical factors that drove evolution using robot designs based on extinct.
Faculty involved: [Carmel Majidi](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/majidi-carmel.html), [Phil LeDuc](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu/directory/bios/leduc-philip.html)
