# International Students
Carnegie Mellon University School of Music has a highly competitive program with many talented students from all over the world. Nearly 35% of Carnegie Mellon students are international students, representing 115 countries around the world. The university ranks in the top 10 "Most International Student Bodies," by percentage among four-year U.S. institutions.
Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania is a city of 300,000 people (over 2 million in the larger area) and home to a dynamic arts, cultural and technology community. Twenty members on the School of Music's faculty are members of the world renowned Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra. 
Please carefully review the application and [Audition & Portfolio Review Requirements](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/audition-portfolio-review-requirements.html) for your intended program of study as well as the International Student information listed in the sections below.
## International Undergraduate Admission
In addition to the [undergraduate music application process](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/undergraduate_admission/index.html), undergraduate international applicants should carefully review the requirements outlined by the [I](http://www.cmu.edu/oie/index.html)[nternational Undergraduate Admissions Page](https://admission.enrollment.cmu.edu/pages/undergraduate-international-students).
  * For information on English Language Proficiency test scores, please review the [language requirements section](https://www.cmu.edu/admission/admission/international-applicants#language) outlined by CMU Office of Undergraduate Admissions.
  * Undergraduate applicants should not send scores to the School of Music directly. English proficiency test scores supporting documents should be added to your Common Application. 


Please visit the [Frequently Asked Questions](https://www.cmu.edu/oie/faq/index.html) page on the [Office of International Education](https://www.cmu.edu/oie/) website for further information.
## International Graduate Admission
As an international graduate student, you will need to complete the following items along with the [Acceptd music application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) and supporting documents outlined in [Graduate Application Requirements:](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/graduate_admission/graduate_application_requirements.html)
  * Transcript Requirement: [TEC](https://spanside.my.salesforce-sites.com/SpantranApplication?Id=c8a37e2d-3f25-4d5d-8909-4dcd4f9c5084) (formerly SpanTran), [World Education Service](http://www.wes.org/) (WES), or [Educational Credential Evaluators](https://www.ece.org/) (ECE).  

    * Applicants may upload unofficial credential evaluations to the Acceptd for application review. Admitted students who choose to enroll will be required to send official, degree-conferred credential evaluations to the School of Music Office of Recruitment & Enrollment before the start of classes. 
    * Official course-by-course credential evaluations may be sent to Carnegie Mellon University - School of Music
    * Electronic documents are preferred.
  * Language Test Requirement: regardless of citizenship or prior educational experience,*
    * [TOEFL](https://www.ets.org/toefl.html) (Test of English as a Foreign Language)
    * [IELTS](https://ielts.org/) (International English Language Testing System), or 
    * [Duolingo English Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/applicants)
    * Electronic documents are preferred.


***The School of Music only accepts English Proficiency scores from a single test date, not MyBest scores. Test results that are over 2 years old will not be accepted.**
