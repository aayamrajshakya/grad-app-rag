## Bio
Yifeng Wang is an innovative and passionate thinker with expertise in computational finance. Graduating with a dual degree in Mathematics and Finance from Boston University, Yifeng developed a keen interest in combining intuition and modeling sophistication to produce meaningful statistical interpretation. Prior to joining MSCF, Yifeng gained practical experience in quantitative finance through roles at Guotai Junan Securities, China Securities, and Zhongtian Securities, three of the top securities firms in China. Yifeng was also involved in several research projects regarding optimization algorithms, market volatility prediction, and default risk prediction. Worked as a public welfare volunteer and teaching assistant, Yifeng improved her skills in leadership, communication, and teamwork. Upon graduating from MSCF, Yifeng plans to pursue a career in quant trading, quant research, or modeling.
