## Bio
David is a joint Ph.D. student in Behavioral Marketing and Decision Research at SDS and Tepper. Prior to his Ph.D. studies, David received his B.A. in Economics and Mathematical Statistics from the University of Virginia where he worked on research relating to social dilemmas, health insurance choices, sustainable development, and decision making.
