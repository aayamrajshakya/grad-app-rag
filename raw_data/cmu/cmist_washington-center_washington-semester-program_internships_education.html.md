#  Education[](https://www.opm.gov/policy-data-oversight/hiring-information/students-recent-graduates/)
Are you passionate about making a difference in the lives of students? Do you want to contribute to creating more equitable, innovative, and inclusive learning environments? If you're considering a career in education, teaching, or educational policy, the organizations below may offer the perfect opportunity to grow and gain hands-on experience! You’ll have the chance to work alongside educators, policymakers, and researchers, gaining valuable insights into how education systems function and how they can be improved.
If you’re interested in exploring education from a policy perspective, consider an internship with government agencies like the Department of Education. For those looking to dive deeper into research, advocacy, or non-profit work, organizations focused on educational equity, STEM education, or literacy offer incredible opportunities to help shape educational practices and policies.
