##  Workshops
Our Academic Coaching workshops help students improve their time management, productive habits, organization, stress management, and study skills. We collaborate with various graduate and undergraduate programs to offer workshops in a variety of study skills topics. Students can choose to attend one or more workshops that are available year-round.
Workshops are currently delivered using video and audio conferencing technology to provide all students with support.
**[View workshop topics](https://www.cmu.edu/student-success/programs/workshops/index.html)**
###  Request a Workshop
Workshop Requests will re-open mid Fall 2025.
Academic Coaches and professional staff from the Student Academic Success Center are available to facilitate on-campus or virtual informational sessions and workshops for academic departments, classes, organizations, or student groups.
Available workshop topics are listed below. To request a session or workshop, please carefully review the following guidelines and complete the form below. Please contact success@andrew.cmu.edu if you have any questions.
Before you complete the workshop request form, please take a moment to review our guidelines.
  * Requests for informational sessions and workshops must be made at least 10 business days before your first date preference.
  * If an outside speaker is requested, additional time may be required.
  * Virtual workshops are hosted on Zoom.
  * Attendance data can be provided after the session if needed.
  * Please note that we will do our best to accommodate your request; requests are not guaranteed and decisions are based on the availability of staff.
