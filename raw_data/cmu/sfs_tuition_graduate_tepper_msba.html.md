# Please Note:
  * 108 units are required to graduate.
  * Tepper Master's tuition rates and fees are evaluated annually and are subject to change.
  * Students are not eligible for federal financial aid for the first semester while enrolled on a less than half-time basis. 
  * Summer costs are the same as spring except: 
    * University fees are not charged in the summer.
    * The estimated budget for living expenses (housing and food) and miscellaneous is $7,695, and the estimated budget for transportation is $1,492.


  * A laptop computer with an additional estimated cost of $1,500 is required. Students may make a one-time request to borrow up to $1,500 to cover the documented purchase of a laptop computer for school use.
  * Students who must retake a course or complete additional coursework due to a failing/non-passing grade will be charged tuition for the units associated with the additional coursework.
  * Tuition, university fees, and health insurance are charged to your student account. You have the option of opting out of the health insurance cost if you have existing coverage.
  * Students may choose to borrow for non-tuition costs like room and board, even though these costs are not charged to the student account.
