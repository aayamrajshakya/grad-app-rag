## Bio
Roni has been working in asset management for the past two decades and is currently a senior quant credit researcher at Citadel. Previously, Roni was Chief Investment Officer and President at NDVR, where he was responsible for overseeing quantitative research and portfolio management. Roni also worked at AQR Capital Management as a Principal, where he managed AQR's options portfolios and portfolio implementation research for equities.   
  
His research has been published in leading journals, including the Journal of Alternative Investments, the Journal of Portfolio Management, and the Financial Analysts Journal. He is a member of the Editorial Board for the Journal of Alternative Investments and the MSCF Advisory Board for the Carnegie Mellon University Tepper School of Business.
