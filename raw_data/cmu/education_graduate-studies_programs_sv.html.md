## Envision the future
The San Francisco Bay Area is the center of the high-tech revolution, with a dynamic atmosphere fueling creativity and entrepreneurship. It is a natural fit for Carnegie Mellon University, one of the world’s premier technical education and research institutions, which chose Silicon Valley as the place to establish its West Coast educational outpost.
This is the perfect place for aspiring industry leaders to further their technology and management skills and build valuable relationships. [CMU Silicon Valley](https://www.sv.cmu.edu/index.html) students are prepared to meet technological and managerial challenges, succeed in the 21st-century workforce, and take a proactive part in shaping the future of the software and technology industry.

## Master’s degree programs
  * **[Master of Science in Software Engineering](https://www.sv.cmu.edu/programs/msse.html)**  
The Master of Science in Software Engineering is a unique program offered exclusively at CMU’s Silicon Valley location. The curriculum provides a robust foundation of software engineering principles within the context of ECE’s world-renowned Computer Engineering program. Students gain the knowledge and skills needed to become architects and project leaders, building systems in which software plays a critical role. 
    * [Application deadlines](https://www.ece.cmu.edu/admissions/graduate-application-deadlines.html)
  * **[Master of Science in Mobile and IoT Engineering](https://www.cmu.edu/ini/academics/bicoastal/index.html)  
** The bicoastal Master of Science in Mobile and IoT Engineering program enables its graduates to analyze historical and state-of-the-art mobile and IoT technologies relating to devices, networks, providers, data, and applications. They also apply mobile and embedded system skills and principles in order to design and develop products and services.  

    * [Application deadlines](https://www.cmu.edu/ini/admissions/howtoapply.html)
  * **[Master of Science in Information Technology - Information Security](https://www.sv.cmu.edu/programs/msit.html)**  
The Information Networking Institute’s bicoastal Master of Science in Information Technology - Information Security program equips students with the tools to evaluate trade-offs between technical security and privacy solutions and potential business and economic impacts. Graduates are capable of designing and implementing secure systems and services by applying knowledge and skills in information security and privacy. 
    * [Application deadlines](https://www.cmu.edu/ini/admissions/howtoapply.html)
  * **[Master of Science in Software Management](https://www.sv.cmu.edu/programs/mssm.html)**  
The Master of Science in Software Management is a distinctive, interdisciplinary degree program offered by CMU’s Integrated Innovation Institute, a joint initiative of the College of Engineering, Tepper School of Business, and the College of Fine Arts School of Design. The program prepares future product managers, entrepreneurs, and innovation-minded professionals to meet the rapidly changing demands of the software industry. 
    * [Application deadlines](https://www.cmu.edu/iii/admissions/index.html)
