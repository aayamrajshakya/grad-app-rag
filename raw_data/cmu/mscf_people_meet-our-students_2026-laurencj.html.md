## Bio
Laurence (Zhao) Jin holds a Bachelor of Science in Quantitative Finance and Risk Management Science from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. During his undergraduate studies, the interdisciplinary nature of Laurence's curriculum has allowed him to explore a diverse range of subjects, equipping him with not only essential technical skills as a quant, but also valuable insights into the financial market. Prior to the MSCF program, Laurence actively engaged in quant-related internships, where he absorbed cutting-edge techniques and tackled complex financial problems. The experience at Dymon Asia Capital familiarized him with multiple index trading strategies and enhanced his programming skills. Following the MSCF program, Laurence aspires to pursue a career as a Q-Quant under the derivatives trading desk.
