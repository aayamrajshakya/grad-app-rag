## Bio
Michelle graduated from the London School of Economics in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics. Before the MSCF, Michelle interned at GIC, Singapore’s sovereign wealth fund, where she joined the Global Mid-Cap Equities team to conduct fundamental equity research. She also interned at Berenberg Bank and the Asian Institute of Digital Finance, where she focused on sell-side ESG research and credit research respectively. At LSE, Michelle was Head of Research for the 14th Asia Investment and Banking Conference, and Executive Committee member of the LSE M&A Group. Michelle joined the MSCF program to further build on her interdisciplinary training in finance, econometrics, and programming and is interested in roles in quantitative research and trading.
