## Bio
Tristan Roemer received his Bachelor of Science from the University of Mannheim in Germany, majoring in Computer Science with a Data Science concentration and a minor in Business. Alongside his undergraduate studies, Tristan pursued his interest in financial markets and investing by joining an Asset Management boutique in Frankfurt for a part-time Quantitative Research position. After his studies, Tristan gained additional experiences through internships at PwC and HSBC Asset Management before joining Carnegie Mellon to pursue his MS in Computational Finance. With the MSCF, Tristan aims to hone and extend the technical skills currently relevant in Quantitative Trading, Research, and Portfolio Management.
