# Training
###  Online Export Control Training[http://www.citiprogram.org](http://www.citiprogram.org/)).
  * Current CITI Users  

    * log-in as usual;
    * from the Main Menu screen select "Add a course or update your learner groups for Carnegie Mellon University"; 
    * select the Export Controls course (at the bottom of the page); and 
    * take the course at your convenience.


  * New CITI Users  

    * select the New Users "Register Here" link on the CITI homepage
    * complete the required registration fields, making sure to select Carnegie Mellon University from the drop-down list of "Participating Institutions" in section #1; 
    * the system will take you through several screens including one where you can select which courses to take; 
    * select the Export Controls course (bottom of the page on the course enrollment screen); 
    * once you complete your enrollment you will automatically be taken to the Main Menu screen; and 
    * you can then begin taking the course or log-out and take the course at your convenience.


Note: Export training is required for all participants on a TCP
In-person training is available periodically or upon request. Contact the Export Compliance Group at [export-compliance@andrew.cmu.edu](https://www.cmu.edu/research-compliance/export-controls/training.html#) for more information or to schedule a training session.
