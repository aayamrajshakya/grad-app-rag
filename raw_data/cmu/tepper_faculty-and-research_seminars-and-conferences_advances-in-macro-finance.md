# Advances in Macro Finance
Carnegie Mellon’s Tepper School of Business and UC Santa Barbara’s [Laboratory for Aggregate Economics and Finance](https://laef.ucsb.edu/) co-host an annual Advances in Macro-Finance conference, convening top scholars to explore cutting-edge research at the intersection of macroeconomics and finance.
