## Bio
Xiyue Yan graduated from Wuhan University with a Bachelor’s degree inEconomics, majoring in Financial Engineering. Before joining the MSCFprogram at Carnegie Mellon University, she gained internship experience asa quantitative researcher, applying modeling and programming to solvepractical financial problems. At Shanghai Lien Capital Management, sheimplemented an intraday futures strategy using option volatility metrics,achieving a 14.77% return in simulated live trading. At Guangfa Securities,she developed a composite alpha factor that delivered a 40% portfolioreturn and researched herding behavior in the A-share market to enhancestock selection. Earlier, at China Merchants Securities, she evaluated over2,600 H-share companies for privatization potential and conductedcross-border fundamental analysis to identify high-potential investmentopportunities. In the MSCF program, she is further strengthening her quantitative,analytical, and programming skills to effectively apply computational toolsin financial markets. After graduation, she aspires to pursue a career inquantitative research.
