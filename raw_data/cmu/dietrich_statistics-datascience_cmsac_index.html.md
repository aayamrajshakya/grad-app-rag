## The Department of Statistics & Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University has been a leader in sports analytics for many years, with a rich history of developing cutting-edge research and hosting accessible community events.
In Fall 2024, the department’s collective sports analytics initiatives [officially launched as the Carnegie Mellon Sports Analytics Center (CMSAC)](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/news/2024/carnegie-mellon-launches-sports-analytics-center.html).
CMSAC’s mission as a national leader in the growing field of sports analytics is to:
  * Push forward the sports analytics discipline through the dissemination of [state-of-the-art research](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/cmsac/research-lab/index.html) that contributes to statistics and machine learning methodology.
  * Create [innovative statistics and data science education experiences](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/cmsac/education-initiatives/index.html) to create pathways into sports analytics careers.
  * [Engage with professional sports teams and industry partners](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/cmsac/engage-with-cmsac/index.html) on capstone projects and fellowship opportunities.
  * [Host accessible events](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/cmsac/events/index.html) for community outreach, such as the annual [CMSAConference](https://www.stat.cmu.edu/cmsac/conference/).
