# Andrew Saul
* * *
## Sr. Vice President of Production Finance, Sony Pictures Television
Andrew Saul serves as Sr. Vice President of Production Finance at Sony Pictures Television, an operating segment of Sony Pictures Entertainment.
Andrew Saul serves as Sr. Vice President of Production Finance at Sony Pictures Television, an operating segment of Sony Pictures Entertainment. Andrew leads a team of executives responsible for production finance operations, credits and incentives, reporting and controllership for SPT’s slate of original programming.
Andrew and his team are actively involved in executing the studio’s broad content production strategy for both dramatic and non-dramatic television. Furthermore, his team supports executive management by providing ad-hoc analysis of fiscal production trends in the rapidly changing landscape of the television studio business.
Prior to joining Sony, Andrew oversaw day-to-day production finance operations for Universal Television and Disney Media Networks. 
Andrew holds a bachelor’s degree in Cinematic Arts from the University of Arizona and an MBA from the University of Southern California. He resides in Studio City with his two children and enjoys spending his free time traveling.
