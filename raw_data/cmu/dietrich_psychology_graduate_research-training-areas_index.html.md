# Graduate Research Training Areas
Whether you are interested in the Psychology Ph.D. or the Cognitive Neuroscience Ph.D., our faculty train graduate students in one of three areas of research:
  * [Cognitive Science](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/psychology/graduate/research-training-areas/cognitive-science/index.html)
  * [Development and Education Psychology](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/psychology/graduate/research-training-areas/developmetal/index.html)
  * [Social and Health Psychology](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/psychology/graduate/research-training-areas/social-health/index.html)
