# Oboe Audition Requirements
## Undergraduate
All applicants to the oboe studio are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * 2 contrasting etudes of your choice
  * 1-2 sonata and/or concerto movement(s) of your choice


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
## Graduate
All applicants to the oboe studio are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * 2 contrasting etudes of your choice
  * 1 or more solo pieces of your choice
  * 1-2 movements of a concerto of your choice
  * 2-3 contrasting orchestral excerpts


* * *
All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. _Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied._
