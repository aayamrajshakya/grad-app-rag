# 2025-2026 University Fees
## Carnegie Mellon University assesses several standard fees to incoming and current students. A brief description of these fees are below, along with the cost per semester and assessment requirements.
## Questions? TheHUB@andrew.cmu.edu
# Note About Fees:
The fees listed here are standard fees that appear on the student invoice. Additional academic-related fees may be charged depending on a student's program of study. 
## Undergraduate Student Fees
## Student Activities Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2025-26 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR):* | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The student activities fee is included with all other charges (tuition, fees, etc.) on a student's account and collected in the same manner as all other components of the balance due. The fee is assessed on a nightly basis to any student who meets the fee requirements. The revenue from the fee - both undergraduate and graduate - is allocated to the Student Organizations office (under the direction of the Division of Student Affairs) and used to fund the expense of approved student activities. | $157 / $314 | Students registered for 19 or more units studying in Pittsburgh, Washington D.C. or Qatar  
*The total amount of Student Activities Fee collected in 2024-2025 was $3,999,771. This includes undergraduate and graduate at all campuses and locations.
## Technology Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2025-26 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR):* | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The technology fee is included with all other charges (tuition, fees, etc.) on a student's account and collected in the same manner as all other components of the balance due. The fee is assessed at the time of a student's enrollment each semester. The revenue from the fee is allocated to the university's central Computing Services unit, where it is ostensibly used to fund the networking expenses incurred by the university on the student's behalf. |  $240 / $480 | All locations  
*The total amount of Technology Fee collected in 2024-2025 was $7,412,620. This includes undergraduate and graduate at all campuses and locations.
## Transportation Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2025-26 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR):* | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The transportation fee is included with all other charges (tuition, fees, etc.) on a student's account and collected in the same manner as all other components of the balance due. The fee is assessed at the time of a student's enrollment each semester. On the Pittsburgh campus, the revenue from the fee is allocated to the university's Facilities Management & Campus Services which funds the student portion of the university's agreement with Pittsburgh Rapid Transportation (PRT), as well as the campus shuttle service. These services include access to the city's public transportation system and campus shuttling services. | $141 / $282 |  All locations  
*The total amount of Transportation Fee collected in 2024-2025 was $3,709,789. This includes undergraduate and graduate at all campuses and locations.
## First-Year Experience Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2025-26 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR):* | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The first-year experience fee is included with all other charges (tuition, fees, etc.) on a student's account and collected in the same manner as all other components of the balance due. The fee is assessed at the time of a student's enrollment into each of their first two semesters. The revenue from the fee is allocated to the First-Year Orientation and Family Engagement office, where it is used to fund the expenses incurred by the university for orientation activities for students and their families who are new to the university. |  $340 / $680 |  Incoming first-year undergraduates and incoming transfer students at all locations  
*The total amount of First-Year Experience Fee collected in 2024-2025 was $1,193,160. This includes undergraduates at all campuses and locations.
