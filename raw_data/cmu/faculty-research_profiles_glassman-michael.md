# Michael Glassman
* * *
## EVP of Acquisitions and Distribution, LD Entertainment
Michael is the EVP of Acquisitions and Distribution at LD Entertainment, an independent studio that finances the development, production, and distribution of film (BIUTIFUL), television (EVERWOOD), and theater (ANGELS IN AMERICA, DEAR EVAN HANSEN).
Michael manages the sales, marketing, and licensing and distribution of all company projects. He oversees a finance team responsible for tracking revenue for a library of over 50 feature films (including JUDY, JACKIE, ANTHROPOID, THE GREY, and a slate of a dozen high school comedies licensed exclusively by Hulu). He also liaises with investors for cash flow management and revenue projection. Michael is an Executive Producer on over 20 LD films, including I.S.S., WORDS ON BATHROOM WALLS, FAST COLOR, the critically acclaimed documentary MAMA’S BOY, BIG TIME ADOLESCENCE, and SUGAR BABIES, which premiered at Sundance in 2025. 
Before LD, Michael was a partner at studio-based production company Outlaw Productions, where he oversaw all development and production.
Michael holds a bachelor’s degree in economics from Tufts University and an MBA from the University of Southern California. He lives in Glendale, CA, with his wife and twin boys.
