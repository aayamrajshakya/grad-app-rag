# Please Note:
  * Tuition is charged on a per-class basis and is reflective of expected tuition increases. Includes books and other reading materials.
  * Tuition and university fees are charged to your student account.
  * Students may choose to borrow for non-tuition costs like room and board, even though these costs are not charged to the student account.
  * If you are married and/ or have dependents and you anticipate your costs being higher than listed in the budget for your program, please email thehub@andrew.cmu.edu.


* Estimated Loan Fees listed in the cost of attendance only apply to students who file a FAFSA. These fees will be included in the cost of attendance when calculating federal aid eligibility. The fees are not applicable to students who do not borrow federal loans.
## Scholarships & Fellowships
For more information regarding scholarships and fellowships available to Heinz College MMM students, please visit the [Master of Medical Management (MMM)](https://www.heinz.cmu.edu/programs/medical-management-master/financial-aid-and-scholarships-mmm) scholarship information webpage. 
## Borrowing
MMM Students are not eligible to borrow federal loans in the third and fifth semesters of the program based on the number of units they take during those periods of enrollment. However, in the third and fifth semesters, MMM students may consider [alternative loan options](https://www.cmu.edu/sfs/financial-aid/types/private.html) that do not require half-time enrollment.
