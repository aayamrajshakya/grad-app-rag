# Others
Are you interested in some other area? The following organizations offer internships that allow you to contribute to important causes and gain experience in a variety of sectors. Whether you're interested in advocating for marginalized communities, supporting cultural institutions, or working on policies that impact social welfare and public services, there’s an opportunity for you!
Interns at these organizations may assist with community outreach, event coordination, research, social media management, and advocacy work. These internships provide valuable hands-on experience in fields that span from public policy to arts administration and health advocacy.
Keep in mind that these are just a few examples! Whatever your area of interest—whether it's in nonprofit work, the arts, government, or beyond—there are countless other organizations in Washington, D.C. and beyond where you can find internship opportunities that align with your goals. Be open to exploring a wide variety of sectors—your perfect internship might be at an organization you haven't thought of yet!
