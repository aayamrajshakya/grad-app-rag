## Bio
Aaron Balleisen is a Ph.D. student in Behavioral Decision Research at SDS. He studies decision making by individuals in strategic contexts, and is particularly interested in perceptions of relative skill-level in both adversarial and cooperative environments. Aaron is a competitive bridge and chess player, and many of his research ideas stem from observations of strategic and psychological dynamics in these and other games.
