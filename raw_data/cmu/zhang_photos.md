### Huaiying receives Eberly Family Professorship Chair
### 09.10.25
Huaiying was honored with the Eberly Family Career Development Professorship of Biological Science by CMU’s Mellon College of Science. These named professorships are presented by the university to dynamic and innovative faculty members working on groundbreaking research.[ Read more here. ](https://www.cmu.edu/mcs/news-events/2025/0910_mcs-faculty-members-recognized-chairs)

## Congratulations to Rongwei on her publication!
### 07.28.24
Rongwei’s article is accepted by Genes and Development; it showcases that SUMOylation, DNA repair factors and BLM helicase promote telomeric DNA synthesis and alternative lengthening of telomeres.

## Hosting the inaugural Condensates conference
### 09.18.23
Huaiying collaborated with other members of Pitt and CMU to organize and launch the Pittsburgh Symposium for Biomolecular Condensates (aka Condensates). It was an informative day of talks encompassing researchers from diverse areas in the field of biocondensates.
