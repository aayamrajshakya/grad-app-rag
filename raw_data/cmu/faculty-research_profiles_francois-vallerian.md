# Francois Vallerian
* * *
## Vice President of Research, Paramount Global Content Distribution
Francois is a seasoned entertainment research executive with a global expertise in content analytics who is passionate about film and TV.
Francois (He/Him/His) has been working in entertainment research for 20 years in France and in the United States from a network, producer, studio and content distributor perspective with global and multi-platform expertise. He has a true passion for TV, film and the business of entertainment in general and watches over 50 hours of content a week.
He started his research career in France whilst a student at Skema Business School (Masters in Project Management) at one of the French leading TV Networks, M6, for 4 years. He then joined the largest independent production company in the world, Zodiak Media (now Banijay) to develop global research and insights. In 2014, Francois moved to Los Angeles and extended his mission with Zodiak Media for a year before being hired by 20th Century FOX Television Distribution between 2015-2018. In 2018, he joined Paramount Global Content Distribution and since then, has been leading global research to support content sales and home entertainment. Francois has also been a MEIM Capstone client representing Paramount on two projects.
