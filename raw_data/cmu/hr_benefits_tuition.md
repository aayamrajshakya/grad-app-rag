# Tuition and Child Care Benefits
## Tuition Benefits
Benefits-eligible faculty and staff members can receive tuition remission or assistance for themselves and their dependent children. Courses may be taken at CMU or at another institution. View tuition benefits for:
  * [Faculty members](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/faculty.html)
  * [Dependent children of faculty members](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/faculty-children.html)
  * [Staff members ](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/staff.html)
  * [Dependent children of staff members](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/staff-children.html)


And review important information on [taxation and tuition benefits](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/taxation.html).
## Child Care Benefit Programs
Carnegie Mellon also offers [several benefit programs](https://www.cmu.edu/hr/benefits/tuition/childcare.html) to assist with child care costs, including the Cyert Center for Early Education Sliding Scale benefit, and a Dependent Care Reimbursement Account.
