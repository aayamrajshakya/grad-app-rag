## What is information systems?
Information Systemsempowers you to bridge technical expertise with strategic insight. Whether your foundation is in business, health care, law, agriculture or architecture, this interdisciplinary edge positions you to drive meaningful impact in a digitally connected world.
## what can a degree in information systems do for me?
