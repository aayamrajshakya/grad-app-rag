## Revolutionize
Carnegie Mellon’s [Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/index.html) (CEE) is shaping highly skilled engineers through the expertise of its esteemed faculty and by utilizing state-of-the-art research facilities and advanced technologies. Students are characterized by technical excellence, social consciousness, ethical conduct, innovative thinking, and critical system analysis. Proficient in engaging with new technologies and scientific developments, the department’s graduates are poised to meet society’s needs and make significant contributions to the improvement of their chosen professional fields.

## Doctor of philosophy degree program
  * **[Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/phd-programs/phd-cee.html)**  
The Doctor of Philosophy program places students at the forefront of engineering innovation, equipping them with the skills to drive progress in civil and environmental disciplines. Graduates are prepared to become leaders and change-makers in academia, industry, and beyond.


## Master’s degree programs
  *     * **[Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/ms-programs/ms-cee.html)  
** The Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering program allows students to build extensive expertise in a specialized concentration within one of four academic pathways. Rigorous interdisciplinary coursework and a collaborative and supportive community offer students a comprehensive and well-rounded learning experience.
    * **[Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering - Research](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/ms-programs/ms-cee-research.html)**  
The Master of Science in Civil and Environmental Engineering - Research program is tailored to meet the evolving needs of aspiring researchers. Diverging from conventional course-based programs, this research-focused degree places a significant emphasis on cultivating research skills and equips students with the necessary tools to excel in careers centered around research.
    * **[Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Engineering - Civil Engineering](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/ms-programs/ai-engineering.html)**  
The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence Engineering - Civil and Environmental Engineering offers you the opportunity to gain mastery of AI while learning how to integrate these approaches within the engineering design process, as well as how to integrate engineering domain knowledge into AI solutions.
    * **[Affiliated master’s programs](https://www.cee.engineering.cmu.edu/education/graduate/ms-programs/other-programs.html)  
** The Civil and Environmental Engineering Department offers several interdisciplinary and dual degree programs.
