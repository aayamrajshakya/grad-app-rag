# 2024-2025 University Fees
## Carnegie Mellon University assesses several standard fees to incoming and current students. A brief description of these fees are below, along with the cost per semester and assessment requirements.
## Questions? TheHUB@andrew.cmu.edu
# Note About Fees:
The fees listed here are standard fees that appear on the student invoice. Additional academic-related fees may be charged depending on a student's program of study. 
## Undergraduate Student Fees
## Student Activities Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2024-24 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR): | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The student activities fee is used to cover student programming and events on campus. | $154 / $308 | Students registered for 19 or more units studying in Pittsburgh, Silicon Valley, Heinz College LA, and Heinz College DC  
## Media Fee
Beginning with the 2024-2025 academic year, there will be no media fee included in the cost of attendance. 
## Technology Fee
EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2024-24 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR): | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The technology fee is used to support information technology, software licensing, and the Andrew email system. |  $235 / $470 | All locations

**** EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2024-25 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR): | MANDATORY FOR:

## Transportation Fee
****  
**** EXPLANATION OF FEE: | 2024-25 COST (SEMESTER/ACADEMIC YEAR): | MANDATORY FOR:  
---|---|---  
The transportation fee is used to support students' local transportation needs. In Pittsburgh, this provides students with unlimited access to the local Pittsburgh Regional Transit (PRT) system as well as the Carnegie Mellon University Shuttle and Escort Service. | $131 / $262 |  Pittsburgh campus  
In Silicon Valley, this provides students with unlimited access to Valley Transportation Authority (VTA) and light rail lines in Santa Clara County, as well as Cal Train passes. | $131 / $262 |  Silicon Valley location  
In Washington, D.C., the WMATA's UPass program will provide MSPPM-DC track students with unlimited metro access during the academic year at a significantly reduced rate. | Heinz DC campus: $140 / $280 |  All Heinz students studying in Washington, D.C.
