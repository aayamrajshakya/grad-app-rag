## Bio
Duncan Wood is a Ph.D. student in Cognitive Decision Science at SDS. Before his Ph.D., Duncan used computational modeling, empirical analysis, and machine learning to research various topics, including homophily, minorities' internet use, and dynamic pricing in costly search markets.
