## GENERAL APPLICATION INFORMATION
  * Undergraduates ([including BXA](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/requirements-by-area/bxa-audition-review-requirements.html)) – Apply to Carnegie Mellon University via the [Common Application](https://www.cmu.edu/admission/admission/apply) and to the School of Music via the Acceptd application.
  * Graduates and Certificates – Apply to CMU School of Music via the [Acceptd](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) application.
  * All applications are due DECEMBER 1.
    * Pre-screening recordings and portfolios must be submitted by this date along with application.
    * Letter(s) of recommendation are due by JANUARY 5.A link will be sent directly to your recommender(s) upon submission of your Acceptd application. Please follow-up with your recommenders directly to ensure they submit their references by the noted deadline.
  * ALL applicants to [programs that require an audition](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/audition-portfolio-review-dates.html) must perform live and in-person. Pittsburgh on-campus and NYC options are available for live auditions.
