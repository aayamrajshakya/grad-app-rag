# Bill West
* * *
## Strategy, Finance and Marketing Executive at West Coast, Inc.
Bill is a senior strategy and finance media executive who has worked for traditional and interactive media companies, including Walt Disney, Warner Bros., Universal, The Jim Henson Company, Acclaim, Codemasters, and Redbox. 
In 2016, Bill founded West Coast Inc. to assist companies in improving revenue and profits, establishing more efficient operations, and raising capital. Bill has worked as an executive producer and production accountant for over a dozen film and television productions, including Space Jam, Inside Man, Parent Trap, Father’s Day, Selena, Batman and Robin, Lethal Weapon 4, Aladdin (animated), Hercules, and Emperors New Grove.  
  
In addition, Bill has launched over 100 video games internationally and helped the companies he worked for open offices in North and South America, Asia, and Europe. Some games he’s worked on include Formula One, Dirt, Grid, Turok the Dinosaur Hunter, Tron Legacy, Wanted, Mortal Kombat, Fallout 4, Oblivion Skyrim, and Call of Duty.  
  
Bill has been responsible for raising over 500 million dollars in both the movie and games space and was instrumental in the sale of the Muppets to the Walt Disney Company and Codemasters to Electronic Arts resulting in acquisitions worth over $1 billion.  
  
In his spare time, Bill volunteers at the local high school and city for choir and sports.
