## About Us
In the Bridges lab we use diverse approaches to study how bacteria make developmental decisions based on extracellular sensory information. Read about our [research](https://labs.bio.cmu.edu/bridges/research/) for more information. In addition, we strive to cultivate a healthy and productive scientific environment!
