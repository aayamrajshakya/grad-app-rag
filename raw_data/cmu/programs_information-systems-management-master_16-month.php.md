In our Master of Information Systems Management (MISM), 
In just 16 months, you'll go from the classroom to the real world. Three semesters of intense learning, plus a summer internship, will prepare you for a career where you're not just a player—you're a game-changer. (Students with three or more years of work experience are welcome to complete the internship, but can also apply for a waiver.)
Forget endless lectures. We believe in learning by doing. Our secret? We partner with companies to give you hands-on experience through Capstone Projectsbefore
Duration: 16 months / 3 semesters (Fall, Spring, Summer Internship, Fall)
