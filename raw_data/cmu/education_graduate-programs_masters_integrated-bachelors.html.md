The Integrated master’s/bachelor’s program (IMB) is an exciting, accelerated opportunity for current mechanical engineering undergraduate students who excel academically and want to pursue both degrees.
You may apply for admission to the program at the end of the first semester of your junior year and must meet the department's requirements for graduate admission.
