# Erin Franzman
* * *
## Advisor at Social Dept AI
Erin Franzman currently sits on the Advisory Board of Social Dept AI, bringing automation to entertainment marketing teams.
Erin currently sits on the Advisory Board of Social Dept AI, bringing automation to entertainment marketing teams.
As the Vice President of Digital and Social Strategy at CBS, Franzman oversaw the online marketing for CBS Entertainment, News and Sports, guided the digital transformation opportunities through AI and web3, and worked across Paramount IP on company initiatives, such as the Super Bowl. Shows include Matlock, Survivor, The Drew Barrymore Show, Ghosts, 60 Minutes, The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, and the Star Trek franchise. 
Prior to working at CBS, Franzman was Content Director at leading New York social-media firm Big Fuel, working with HBO to create social campaigns for shows like The Newsroom and GIRLS. Her work as Director of Interactive Programming at Madison Square Garden was nominated and won multiple New York Emmys, and she was also Managing Editor of AOL’s city guides. She started her career at alternative-weekly newspapers, including New York Press and Seattle’s Stranger, where she was the music editor, and her freelance writing and photography have been published in the New York Post and Los Angeles magazine.
