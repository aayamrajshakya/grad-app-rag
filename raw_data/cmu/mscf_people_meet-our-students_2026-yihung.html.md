## Bio
Yi Hung graduated from National Taiwan University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Politics. Yi's passion for quantitative finance led him to intern at WorldQuant, where he applied deep learning techniques to develop trading alphas from raw data. Following this, Yi gained hands-on experience in high-frequency trading and cryptocurrency at Quantrend. He also started to oversee the process of executing and maintaining strategies as a trader, which further solidifying his expertise in the field. His practical experience is complemented by rigorous coursework from the MSCF program at Carnegie Mellon University, equipping him with advanced quantitative skills and a robust understanding of financial markets. Yi Hung is now focused on pursuing a career in quantitative research and trading. His unique blend of technical proficiency, practical experience, and academic rigor makes him a valuable asset to any buy-side quant team.
