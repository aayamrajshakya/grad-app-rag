## Bio
Nisha Sen graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2022 with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics and Minors in Statistics and Computer Science. She then joined the CashPro product organization within Global Payments Solutions at Bank of America for two years. As a product analyst for CashPro, a digital banking platform used by over 40K clients, Nisha worked on multiple CashPro teams (Portfolio, Data and Reporting, Data Intelligence), gaining experience in product management, data analytics, and executive data presentation. Nisha is joining the CMU MSCF program in 2024 as a Squarepoint Fellow and hopes to pursue a career in quantitative research.
