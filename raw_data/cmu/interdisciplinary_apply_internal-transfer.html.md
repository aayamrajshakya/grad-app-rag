# Internal Transfer Admission
The BXA Intercollege Degree Programs
Timeline  

  * Due Mid-Semester on October 1, and March 1, every semester. Admission is for the following semester. See below for important exceptions. Decisions are sent out in advance of registration for the following semester.


Application Contents and Criteria
  * Signed application
  * Essay of Interdisciplinary Intent 
  * Cumulative QPA of 3.0
  * Pre-Application Coursework


## Exceptions
Students must complete their first semester at Carnegie Mellon before applying for internal transfer. This enables students to take required/recommended preliminary courses in their target area of concentration before being reviewed for admission.
BXA design only considers applicants currently enrolled in Design.
BXA dramaturgy only considers applicants in the fall semester for spring enrollment, unless currently enrolled in Drama dramaturgy. BXA design only considers internal transfer applicants currently enrolled in Drama design. BXA directing only considers applicants currently enrolled in Drama directing. BXA PTM only considers internal transfer applicants currently enrolled in Drama PTM. Neither acting nor musical theater are options for [BXA drama concentrations](http://coursecatalog.web.cmu.edu/intercollegeprograms/bxaintercollege/#bha-drama "BXA drama concentrations").
BXA music performance only considers applicants in the spring semester for fall enrollment, unless currently enrolled in Music performance.
## Apply for BCSA Internal Transfer
Students currently in SCS[SCS advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bcsa-scs-advisors.html) as well as the advisor in the CFA school they’d like a concentration in. We recommend that students take one or two courses in their target area of concentration before applying, and most CFA schools will require either a portfolio review or an audition. These reviews/auditions must be scheduled with the [CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) in that school.
Students currently in a CFA School[CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) as well as the [SCS advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bcsa-scs-advisors.html). To apply to BCSA from outside SCS, students must take 15-112, 15-122, 15-150 and at least one of the 200-level core course (15-210, 15-213, 15-251), earning an average of B or better in those courses (excepting 15-112). Students must also take 21-127 as a co/pre-requisite with a C or better.ost students are eligible to apply by their sophomore year or later.
## Apply for BESA Internal Transfer
Students currently in Engineering[assistant dean for undergraduate studies in ENG](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/besa-eng-advisors.html) as well as the advisor in the CFA school they’d like a concentration in. We recommend that students take one or two courses in their target area of concentration before applying, and most CFA schools will require either a portfolio review or an audition. These reviews/auditions must be scheduled with the [CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) in that school.
Students currently in a CFA School[CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) as well as an [assistant dean for undergraduate studies in ENG](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/besa-eng-advisors.html). To apply to BESA from outside ENG, students must take 21-120, 21-122, 33-141 and at least one of the introduction to engineering courses, earning an average of B or better in those classes. ECE applicants must also take 15-112. This sequence takes a minimum of two semesters, and most students are eligible to apply by their sophomore year or later.
## Apply for BHA Internal Transfer
Students currently in Dietrich CollegeDC primary advisor[CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) in that school.
Students currently in a CFA School[CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) as well as the advisor in the DC department they’d like a concentration in. Students must take preliminary DC coursework in their target area before an application will be considered. Please see the list of [required completed and in progress DC courses](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/assets/bha-internal-trans-dc-courses.pdf "BHA Internal Transfer Required DC Courses") [pdf] before applying to BHA and meet with the appropriate [DC advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bha-dc-advisors.html).
## Apply for BSA Internal Transfer
Students currently in Mellon College[MCS advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bsa-mcs-advisors.html) as well as the advisor in the CFA school they’d like a concentration in. We recommend that students take one or two courses in their target area of concentration before applying, and most CFA schools will require either a portfolio review or an audition. These reviews/auditions must be scheduled with the [CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) in that school.
Students currently in a CFA School must consult with their [CFA advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bxa-cfa-advisors.html) as well as the advisor in the MCS department they’d like a concentration in. Students must take preliminary MCS coursework in their target area before an application will be considered. Please see the list of [required completed and in progress MCS courses](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/assets/bsa-internal-trans-mcs-courses.pdf "BSA Internal Transfer Required MCS Courses") [pdf] before applying to BSA and meet with the appropriate [MSC advisor](https://www.cmu.edu/interdisciplinary/people/bsa-mcs-advisors.html).
