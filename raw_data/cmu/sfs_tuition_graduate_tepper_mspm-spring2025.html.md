# Tuition

# Please Note:
  * The spring 2025 estimated cost of health insurance runs from January 1, 2025, to July 31, 2025. Students who graduate in fall 2025 may keep coverage until July 31, 2026, or apply for a pro-rata refund, which may not be available if significant claims have been paid as determined by the Student Health Services Office. [Visit the University Health Services site](https://www.cmu.edu/health-services/student-insurance/index.html) for more information.
  * A laptop computer with an additional estimated cost of $1,500 is required. Students may make a one-time request to borrow up to $1,500 to cover the documented purchase of a laptop computer for school use.
