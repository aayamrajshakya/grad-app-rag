#  Abbe Depretis 
##  Associate Teaching Professor of Business Communication 
Contact

## Bio
Abbe Depretis is an Assistant Teaching Professor of Business Communication at the Tepper School of Business at Carnegie Mellon University and was formerly an Assistant Professor of Instruction in the Department of Communication and Social Influence at Temple University. Her area of study is rhetoric and social change, business communication, and DEI in the workplace. Depretis has a Ph.D. in Rhetoric and Social Change from the University of Maryland and an MBA from Temple University.
