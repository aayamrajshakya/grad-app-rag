# Teaching Overview
I teach upper-level undergraduate lab courses that cover a wide range of subjects: Experimental Techniques in Molecular Biology, Experimental Cell and Developmental Biology, and Experimental Neuroscience. 
# Programs & Outreach
I teach for two different high school groups in the summer - the [Pennsylvania Governor's School for the Sciences](http://sciences.pa-gov-schools.org) (PGSS), and the [Summer Academy for Math and Science](https://admission.enrollment.cmu.edu/pages/diversity-sams) (SAMS), where I typically lead team research projects involving neuroscience topics.
