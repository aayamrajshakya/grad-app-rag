We use our research to enhance the manufacturing of products and processes, which helps to create a stronger and more vibrant industrial community. Our expertise in the fields of additive manufacturing, medical device manufacturing, and micro/nano-manufacturing is continuously strengthened by our interdisciplinary approaches and industrial partnerships. Our researchers are constantly exploring new materials for 3-D metal printing, innovating the latest techniques in micro-machining, and developing innovative hands-on medical training tools.
