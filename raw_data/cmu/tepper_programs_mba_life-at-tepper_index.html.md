# Life at the Tepper School
Life at the Tepper School extends beyond the classroom. Whether you are an undergraduate, master’s, or PhD student, you will be part of a close-knit community within Carnegie Mellon. The Tepper School environment is collaborative, diverse, and designed to help you build relationships that last a lifetime.
## Hear From a Graduate Student Ambassador
The best way to understand life at the Tepper School is to hear directly from someone who is experiencing it. Our graduate student ambassadors can share their perspectives on classes, clubs, career paths, and living in Pittsburgh. They are also available to answer your questions about what it is like to be part of the Tepper School community.
[Connect With a Graduate Student Ambassador](https://www.cmu.edu/tepper/programs/masters-admissions/student-ambassadors)
## Student Clubs and Organizations
Student-led organizations bring the Tepper School community to life. Clubs give you the chance to connect with peers, build leadership experience, and pursue professional and personal interests.
[Browse Student Clubs on TartanConnect](https://tartanconnect.cmu.edu/club_signup)
## Part of the Carnegie Mellon Community
As part of Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School students benefit from a campus community that spans disciplines. You’ll collaborate with peers in technology, engineering, design, and the arts while enjoying the full range of CMU resources and traditions.
[Experience the CMU Community](https://www.cmu.edu/campus-life)
## Living in Pittsburgh
Pittsburgh is an accessible, livable city that blends opportunity and culture. From Fortune 500 companies to cutting-edge startups, world-class museums to neighborhood cafés, students benefit from a city that is both welcoming and affordable.
[See What Pittsburgh Has to Offer](https://www.cmu.edu/campus-life/living-in-pittsburgh)
