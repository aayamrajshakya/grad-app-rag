Carnegie Mellon University’s CyLab Security and Privacy Institute brings together some of the top security and privacy researchers. Linking all relevant faculty members and researchers across CMU’s seven colleges, CyLab offers a way to learn directly from industry leaders in education and research in the world. 
CyLab offers over a dozen courses, many of which can be combined and customized as needed to meet the needs of our partners. CyLab offers executive education programs across the [14 research areas](https://www.cylab.cmu.edu/research/index.html) encompassed within the Institute, ranging from Applications of Security and Privacy, Blockchain, Machine Learning & AI, and Security (Hardware, Network, Software, or Systems).
CyLab partners may be eligible to receive discounts on CMU security or privacy executive education and professional development training programs.partnerships@cylab.cmu.edu to verify eligibility and to claim discounts.
The following courses are research training for intermediate to expert level practitioners and engineers. All of them are administered by the CyLab Security and Privacy Institute unless otherwise noted.
