# Violin Audition Requirements
## Undergraduate
Violin applicants are required to submit a pre-screening video recorded audition. 
#### PRE-SCREENING REQUIREMENTS:
  * One etude from Kreutzer, Fiorillo, Rode, Dont or Gavinies, or a caprice by Wieniawski or Paganini (played by memory)
  * An allegro movement of a non-Baroque concerto (played by memory)
  * One movement of a Bach solo sonata or partita (played by memory)


Please see [Pre-screening Instructions](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/pre-screening-materials.html) for details on how to submit your pre-screening. Media must be uploaded to the School of Music [online application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) by December 1. Do not mail recorded audition media to the Office of Undergraduate Admission.
#### AUDITION REQUIREMENTS:
All applicants to the violin studio are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * One etude from Kreutzer, Fiorillo, Rode, Dont or Gavinies, or a caprice by Wieniawski or Paganini 
  * An allegro movement of a non-Baroque concerto (played by memory)
  * One movement of a Bach solo sonata or partita (played by memory)


All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied.
Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied during the live auditions.
## Graduate
Violin applicants are required to submit a pre-screening video recording. 
PRE-SCREENING REQUIREMENTS:
  * Two contrasting movements of any solo sonata or partita by Bach (played from memory)
  * A Paganini Caprice or a movement from an Ysaye solo sonata
  * A first movement of a major concerto written after 1830


Please see [Pre-screening Instructions](https://www.cmu.edu/cfa/music/apply/audition-portfolio-review/pre-screening-materials.html) for details on how to submit your pre-screening. Media must be uploaded to the [online application](https://app.getacceptd.com/cmumusic) by December 1.
AUDITION REQUIREMENTS:
All applicants to the violin studio are expected to prepare the following audition requirements:
  * Two contrasting movements of any solo sonata or partita by Bach (played from memory)
  * A movement of a complete Classical, Romantic, or 20th Century concerto (played from memory)
  * A Paganini Caprice, a movement from an Ysaye solo sonata, or any virtuoso work by Sarasate, Wieniawski, Saint-Saens, or Ravel


All performance candidates are required to complete an audition. Note: All solo instrumental auditions are unaccompanied.
