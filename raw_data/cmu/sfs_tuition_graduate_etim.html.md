The full-time MS program in ETIM begins during the spring term, includes a summer internship, and concludes at the end of the fall term during the same calendar year. The ETIM MS program increases tuition from fall to spring during the middle of the academic year instead of at the end of the regular academic year. The ETIM summer internship practicum is a 3-unit course charged at the per-unit tuition rate of $798/unit for summer 2025, totaling $2,394.
##### As of March 27, 2025.
# Please Note:
  * ETIM tuition includes the 2025 summer internship three-unit rate ($798 per unit).
  * Part Time Per Unit Tuition: $798/unit for spring 2025 and fall 2025.
  * Technology Fee, Student Activities Fee and Transportation Fee are standard required fees.
  * Living expense rates are standard for the fall and spring semesters. There may be additional costs associated with the summer internship and the fee amounts depend on a variety of factors. [Contact ETIM](http://www.cmu.edu/engineering/etim/contact/index.html) for more information.
  * The listed tuition rate is for reference only. The actual tuition rate may vary depending upon the department or program of study. For the actual tuition rate for your department or program of study, please reference your student account on [SIO](https://s3.andrew.cmu.edu/sio/index.html#home) or follow up with your departmental administrator.
