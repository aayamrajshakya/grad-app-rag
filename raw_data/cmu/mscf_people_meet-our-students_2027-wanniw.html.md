## Bio
Wanni Wu graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles in 2025, with a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Economics. Her academic background in stochastic processes, econometrics, and financial modeling sparked a deep interest in data-driven investment strategies. She has applied time series analysis, machine learning, and simulation techniques to analyze market signals, evaluate portfolio risk, and optimize decision-making in her research projects and past internships. Wanni is particularly passionate about staying on top of evolving industry trends, with a strong interest in the intersection of sustainability, innovation, and finance. She has explored the energy sector through her previous internships. Wanni joined the MSCF Program to further strengthen her quantitative skill set and gain a deeper understanding of global financial markets. She hopes to pursue a career in quantitative research or quantitative portfolio management.
