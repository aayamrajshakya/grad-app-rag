## Bio
Qing (Claire) Dong graduated from Georgia Tech in 2024 with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a Minor in Computational Data Analysis. She is passionate about applying her mathematical skills to model real-world scenarios.
Claire gained valuable experience in data cleansing and visualization as a Data Analytics Intern at The Coca-Cola Company. She further expanded her expertise by taking courses in trading and fixed income, and actively participating in financial clubs such as the GT Investment Committee and Trading Club, where she gained experience in fundamental stock research and market making.
Her exposure to the financial sector led her to intern at PwC's Deals department in Boston, where she analyzed M&A cases for TMT companies leveraging Large Language Models. To further integrate her skills in math, programming, and finance, Claire joined the MSCF program. Upon graduation, she plans to pursue a career in quantitative research.
