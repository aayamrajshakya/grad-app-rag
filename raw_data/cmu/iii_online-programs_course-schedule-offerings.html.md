# Online Course Schedule Offerings
Recognizing the rigorous demands of a professional schedule, the majority of our live online sessions are offered during evening hours (EST). We are committed to helping you achieve your academic and professional goals, and we regularly test and add timeslots. Our core mission is to provide scheduling flexibility that empowers you to seamlessly integrate high-quality education into your demanding life.
