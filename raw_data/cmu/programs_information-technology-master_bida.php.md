# Online IT Master's Degree: Business Intelligence & Data Analytics (BIDA)
* * *
The Business Intelligence & Data AnalyticsMaster of Science in Information Technology 
This is the ideal program for experienced professionals who wish to remain on the job while advancing their careers in data analytics. Combining business strategy, analytics, IT, and communications classes, we train you with the elite skills you need to be competitive in this rapidly growing field.
The program offers flexibility in both curriculum and structure to meet the needs of working students. You can continue to work full-time while earning your degree part-time, allowing you to leverage your new knowledge and skills in real time.
[Admissions Requirements](https://www.heinz.cmu.edu/programs/information-technology-master/admissions-how-to-apply.php)
