#  Fikret Kalagoglu 
## Bio
Fikret Kalagoglu joined BAM in January 2023 as a Macro Portfolio Manager, in our New York office. Prior to BAM, Fikret was with Moore Capital Management from 2004 to 2022. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics from SUNY at Stony Brook, as well as a Master's in Computational Finance from Carnegie Mellon University. He has been a CFA charterholder since 2003.
