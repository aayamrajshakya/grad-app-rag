## Bio
Dorian is an interdisciplinary AI researcher whose research gravitates toward both the theory and implementation of reasoning for artificial intelligence, as well as ethics and policy problems raised by increasingly ubiquitous use of AI. Prior to joining the Carnegie Mellon community, he worked for several years in the technology industry as a software engineer.
