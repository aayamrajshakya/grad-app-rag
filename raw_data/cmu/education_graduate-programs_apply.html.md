## Apply
Need help?
For questions about applying to our graduate programs, please contact the Office of Faculty and Graduate Affairs.
Apply to the graduate programs in the College of Engineering using the [online application](https://gradadmissions.engineering.cmu.edu/apply "College of Engineering online application").
  * You do not have to complete the online application in one sitting. You may access your application and change your answers as many times as you like.
  * Once you have submitted, you will be able to upload required materials, edit recommendation requests, and enter new test scores through your application status page. You can reach this by logging into your account. Applications may not be reviewed until all required materials are received.
