## Bio
Dhwani is interested in how individuals respond and adapt to environmental challenges. Prior to her Ph.D., Dhwani worked at Busara, a research consultancy that applies behavioral science to challenges in the global south, where she designed studies and tested interventions across data privacy, healthcare, and household finance.
