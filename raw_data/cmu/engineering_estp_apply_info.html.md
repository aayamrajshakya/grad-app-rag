# EST&P Information Sessions
The EST&P admissions team provides unique opportunities to connect and learn more. Interested students can connect with us by participating in live, online information and chat sessions, or by simply contacting energy@andrew.cmu.edu.
Have questions about the EST&P degree programs, CMU, or Pittsburgh? Ask us online by registering for a live information session or joining a live admissions chat!
These evens are offered annually in December while our Fall application is open. They are held virtually via Zoom. Please note that all listed times are PA Eastern Standard Time (EST)here.
The Fall 2026 application is now open!
**[Apply Here](https://gradadmissions.engineering.cmu.edu/apply/ "Apply")**
## Virtual Information Session
In our live information session, we present on the culture and history of EST&P, give general program information, and answer some FAQs regarding the admissions process. You'll have the chance to hear from Professor Paul Salvador, Director, Alyssa Bernens, Academic Program Manager and current students of the program. There will be a Q&A chat session moderated by the Admissions and Program Coordinator, Nicole Rihn, throughout and at the end of the presentation._[](https://gradadmissions.engineering.cmu.edu/register/?id=07e78d3d-ca56-455d-9ef4-bb208689913f)_
## Virtual Chat Sessions
Have your application questions answered by the admissions team.
