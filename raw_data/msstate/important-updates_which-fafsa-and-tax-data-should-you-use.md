Important Reminders:
  * Consent and approval from the student and all contributors are required to automatically retrieve federal tax information from the IRS for the FAFSA form.
  * Providing consent and approval ensures the FAFSA form uses accurate tax data and reduces the time needed to complete the financial section.
  * Without consent and approval from all contributors, the student will not be eligible for federal student aid, including grants and loans.
  * To complete the FAFSA[Click Here](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa).
