#  Communities 
The Small Town Center has provided planning and design services for communities across Mississippi over the last 40 years.
##  Small Towns We've Helped 
The Small Town Center works to provide community-specific solutions to local challenges. We do this by providing a variety of design services including community engagement and visioning; master planning; project feasibility studies; downtown revitalizations; bike and pedestrian planning; research; creating and building; grant writing; and design seminars and workshops.
