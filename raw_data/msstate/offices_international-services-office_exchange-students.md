#  Exchange Students
The International Institute at Mississippi State University (MSU) hosts international exchange students from our global partner universities to provide a rewarding academic experience and true cultural exchange. At MSU, we value our international students and strive to enrich the lives of exchange students through strong academic programs and offering beneficial international services. MSU offers competitive programs in agriculture and life sciences; architecture, art, and design; arts and sciences, business, education, engineering, forest resources, and veterinary medicine. Attending MSU as an international exchange student will put you on a path to successfully reaching your academic, career, and personal goals.
