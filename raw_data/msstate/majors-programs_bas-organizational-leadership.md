##  About the Program   

KEY INFORMATION:
Rather than following a traditional business administration model, this degree equips students with real-world leadership, communication, and management skills that reflect current workplace needs. From strategic thinking to organizational communication and change management, the curriculum is crafted to help students lead teams, improve systems, and make meaningful impact in their organizations.
The program is fully online and tailored for working professionals looking to finish their degree and unlock new opportunities—on their schedule, at their pace.
