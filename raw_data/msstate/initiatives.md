# Initiatives
## Training & Education
### ORED Programs
Each semester ORED hosts a series of programs including seminars, symposia, and special events to help train and educate MSU faculty and staff on various topics and foster interdisciplinary collaboration. The presenters range from MSU employees, program officers and guests we bring to campus.
[Current and Archived Program Information](https://www.ored.msstate.edu/initiatives/seminars)

## 2025 Spring Research Celebration
Join us in celebrating Mississippi State University’s leadership in research impacts, innovation, and discovery during the 2025 Spring Research Celebration Monday, March 17, through Wednesday, May 14, 2025. This celebration features conferences, student-focused opportunities, and a dynamic research showcase.

## Honors and Awards
Every year, ORED and the Division of Agriculture, Forestry and Veterinary Medicine honor MSU students, faculty and staff across campus for their accomplishments, research and creative endeavors. The program includes awards for each college, university center and institutes, ORED and MSU-Meridian, as well as the presentation of the Ralph E. Powe Research Excellence Award, the university’s highest research honor.
[View Research Awards](https://www.ored.msstate.edu/initiatives/research-awards)
