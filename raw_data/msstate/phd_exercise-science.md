#  Exercise Science PhD
##  Exercise Science PhD   

The focus of the exercise science doctoral concentration is the scientific study of how biological systems function during physical activity, exercise, and sport, emphasizing applications to both clinical and healthy populations.
The program is intended for students who desire teaching and/or research careers in higher education, industry, and other research institutions.
