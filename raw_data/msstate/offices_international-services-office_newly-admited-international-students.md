#  Newly Admitted International Students
Congratulations on being admitted to Mississippi State University!
For international students newly admitted to MSU, please visit the links to the right. There you will find information about receiving your I-20, steps to get an F-1 visa, and pre-arrival information to guide you as you prepare to travel. You’ll also find information about what to do your first days on campus. We can’t wait to meet you!
