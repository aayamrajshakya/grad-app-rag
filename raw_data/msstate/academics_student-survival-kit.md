## Navigational Equipment
  * Academic
    * [ECE Writing Resource Center](https://www.ece.msstate.edu/academics/student-survival-kit/ece-writing-resource-center/) - Get help on writing assignments
    * [MSU Student Success](http://www.tlc.msstate.edu/) - Beef up learning skills or find tutorial support for English, Math, Physics and Chemistry courses
    * [Honors Program](http://www.honors.msstate.edu/) - Enrich your academic experience
    * [Student Support Services](http://sss.msstate.edu/) - academic support for students with disabilities and first generation college students.
  * Career and Finances
    * [Career Center](http://www.career.msstate.edu) - Find out about career options and job opportunities
    * [Co-Op Program](http://www.coop.msstate.edu) - Gain experience while obtaining you academic education
    * [Student Financial Aid and Scholarships](http://www.sfa.msstate.edu) - Obtain financial planning assistance
  * Health and Safety
    * [Longest Student Health Center](http://www.health.msstate.edu) - Get convenient help with medical needs
    * [Recreational Sports](http://www.recsports.msstate.edu) - Find a healthy lifestyle
    * [Student Counseling Services](http://www.health.msstate.edu/scs/) - Obtain assistance in dealing with personal concerns
    * [Collegiate Recovery Community](http://recovery.msstate.edu/) - support services for recovering students.
    * [Campus Police](http://www.police.msstate.edu/) - Get help for a safer environment
