## About this Event
Interested in learning about all things creepy and crawly in the world of veterinary and medical parasitology? Next Thursday (October 30) and Friday (October 31), join us in The Clinical Skills Lab for “Life in the Dark: Parasites & their Hosts,” a showcase of parasites in and on their hosts.
Come prepared to be overwhelmed by the biodiversity of >100 parasite species on display with their hosts of veterinary and medical importance!
Please use the entrance near the flagpole in front of the Wise Center.
