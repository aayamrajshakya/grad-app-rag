# Accelerated B.S./M.B.A. Program
The BSIE/MBA-PM Accelerated Program allows highly-qualified undergraduate students in industrial engineering to begin pursuing their MBA degree while still pursing their BSIE degree. Students in this program apply 9 hours of coursework to both degree programs simultaneously. Program Admission Requirements: Students must be currently enrolled in the MSU Industrial Engineering degree program. Students must be of junior or senior standing. Students must have a cumulative GPA of 3.50 or higher. Graduate School Admission Requirements: Students accepted into the accelerated program will need to complete a full BSIE-MBA Application
