# Omega Nu Lambda National Honor Society
MS State Online undergraduate and graduate students who have completed at least 15 credit hours of online coursework at MSU with a 3.2 GPA or better will be invited to join the [Omega Nu Lambda National Honor Society](https://www.omeganulambda.org/). Qualifying students will receive an invitation via email and must respond to the invitation by the deadline specified in the email in order to join.
Joining this exclusive group of online students acknowledges your academic excellence and provides access to benefits such as networking opportunities and eligibility for Omega Nu Lambda scholarships.
For more information about Omega Nu Lambda at Mississippi State University, please contact Anusha Rijal.
