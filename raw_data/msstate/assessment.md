#  Assessment
##  CTE Assessments   

The Assessment Team at the Research and Curriculum Unit (RCU) plays a vital role in advancing Career and Technical Education (CTE) across Mississippi. Through strategic collaboration with educators, industry partners, and state agencies, the team facilitates the integration of nationally recognized certifications, embedded work-based learning (WBL) experiences, and comprehensive program quality assessments. These efforts ensure that CTE programs are aligned with workforce demands, provide students with real-world skills, and maintain high standards of instructional excellence. By supporting these key components, the RCU helps prepare Mississippi students for successful careers and lifelong learning.
##  Assessment   

The Assessment team with the Research and Curriculum Unit facilitates national certifications, embedded WBL, and program quality assessments for career and technical education in Mississippi.
