#  3MT 2015 Winners
Congratulations to Piyush Porwal who was selected the Grand Champion winner of the 3 Minute Thesis Competition November 13, 2015. Piyush will advance and represent Mississippi State University at the 3 Minute Thesis Competition hosted by the Conference of Southern Graduate Schools in Charlotte, NC on February 21, 2016.
## Piyush Porwal, MS Grand Champion
James Worth Bagley College of Engineering, Mechanical Engineering  
Thermal and Fluidic Characterization of Tesla Valve  
Advisor: Dr. Scott Thompson
## Dafne Alves Oliveira, PhD – Grand Champion Runner up
College of Agriculture and Life Sciences, Biochemistry, Molecular Biology, Entomology and Plant Pathology  
Thesis Title: Characterization of the Protein products of Genes to Defeat Aflatoxin in Corn  
Advisor: Dr. Kenneth Willeford
## Abbey Wilson, MS Grand Champion Runner Up
College of Agriculture and Life Sciences

Advisor: Dr. Darrell Sparks
