# Management Systems Engineering
The goal of this research is to facilitate the timely delivery of high-quality, cost-effective products and services through the application of engineering principles to the design and operation of management systems.
Research and education efforts are to:
  * provide and utilize the appropriate tools and methods for the design, assessment, analysis, implementation, and improvement of management systems.
  * provide decision support through the development and application of approaches, methodologies, technologies, and systems in the areas related to process management, quality management, and cost management.
