#  Sample Retention and Disposal
Unless alternative arrangements have been made when samples are submitted to the laboratory, the excess from samples not used for analysis will be retained by the laboratory after reporting results for the following time period:
Drinking Water -- 1 Month
Wastewater/Sludge/Soil -- 7 Days
Food/Perishables -- 7 Days
All Other Samples -- 1 Month
