# Sport Studies PhD
The sport studies doctoral concentration involves studying sport from an interdisciplinary perspective, including the areas of sport management, sport pedagogy, sport sociology, and sport philosophy.
The program is intended for students who desire teaching and research careers in higher education institutions. Interested students should familiarize themselves with the research interests of graduate faculty in the sport studies doctoral program: [Dr. Soyoun Lim](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/slim "Dr. Soyoun Lim"), [Dr. Gregg Twietmeyer](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/gtwietmeyer "Dr. Greggory J. Twietmeyer"), [Dr. James Watkins](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/jw733 "Jim Watkins bio page"), and [Dr. Brad Vickers](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/bv44 "Dr. Brad Vickers").
