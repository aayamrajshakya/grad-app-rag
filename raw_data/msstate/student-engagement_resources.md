# Resources
Students can log their volunteer and other involvement hours in Cowbell Connect and create a co-curricular transcript as a means of measuring their impact. 
  * Go to [Cowbell Connect](https://msstate.campuslabs.com/engage/).
  * Click "Explore Cowbell Connect."
  * Sign in with MyState Duo.
  * Click the circle icon to view a drop-down list (top-right corner).
  * Select "Experiences."
  * Click the blue button that says, "Add Experience" and select "Service."
  * Complete the form and click submit.
