#  Ph.D. in Community College Leadership
##  Ph.D. in Community College Leadership   

The Ph.D. in Community College Leadership readies professionals for leadership in community colleges with a comprehensive online curriculum.
##  Program Overview   

The Ph.D. degree program in Community College Leadership is designed to prepare professionals for leadership positions in community colleges. The degree program is designed to prepare the next generation of community college leaders.
The program consists of courses in history and philosophy of the community college, leadership and organizational theory, leading and managing a community college, and research and statistics. The program is offered through the Center for Distance Education and is fully online.
