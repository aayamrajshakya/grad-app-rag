According to the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics, jobs in Public Relations are projected to grow 6 percent from 2018 to 2028.
As the largest department concentration with approximately 250 students, the concentration incorporates scholarship with practical application that aids students' development of skills in critical thinking, research, strategic planning, goal making, problem-solving and effective written and oral communication. 
Our students have interned with MSU Athletics, Walt Disney World, Coca Cola, The U.S. Congress, local news stations, and community-based organizations.   
  
We provide students more opportunities to hone their skills 
For more information about the online public relations concentration or to apply for the program please visit <https://www.online.msstate.edu/copr>.
