## ELI Admission
### Full-Time F-1 Students
Information for applying as a full-time student at the English Language Institute.
The English Language Institute requires all prospective students to submit a language score. We accept the TOEFL ibt, IELTS, and DuoLingo English Placement test. Scores must be less than 6 months old. You may submit your application forms and test score PDF by email to eli@msstate.edu. Acceptance will be based on expected enrolment and class availability. 
  1. Complete the [F-1 Application form](https://www.international.msstate.edu/sites/www.international.msstate.edu/files/2025-05/English%20Language%20Institute%20%20F1%20Application.pdf) and submit it to the ELI.
  2. Submit your language test score along with the application. You must submit a test score for your application to be evaluated.
  3. You will be notified of your admission status by email once a decision has been made.


If you are admitted, you will be sent instructions on how to:
  1. Pay the non-refundable $75 Fee.
  2. Complete the[ Certification of Finance from](https://www.international.msstate.edu/sites/www.international.msstate.edu/files/2025-05/ELIfinances25_26.pdf)[ ](https://www.international.msstate.edu/sites/www.international.msstate.edu/files/2025-05/ELIfinances25_26.pdf)and submit it to the ELI along with your proof of funding.
  3. Submit a copy of the passport Bio page for yourself and any dependents you plan to bring.


Once all application requirements have been met, we will issue an acceptance letter and the initial form I-20. International students should be prepared to fulfill all mandatory [medical requirements](https://www.international.msstate.edu/offices/english-language-institute/program-information#medical) as part of orientation when they arrive.
#### F-1 Transfer Students 
In addition to the documents listed above, you must also:
  1. Submit copies of all other immigration documents (visa, I-94, and copy of current I-20).
  2. Submit the completed [ELI transfer in form](https://www.international.msstate.edu/sites/www.international.msstate.edu/files/2025-05/ELI%20transfer%20form.pdf).
  3. Request that your current school release your SEVIS record to:  
Mississippi State University English Language Institute  
School Code: NOL214F10163001


### Part-Time Students
Non F-1 visa holders, U.S. permanent residents, U.S. citizens, and community members are welcome to study at the English Language Institute (ELI)!
All applicants must:
  1. [Application Form](https://www.international.msstate.edu/sites/www.international.msstate.edu/files/2025-05/English%20Language%20Institute%20non%20F1%20application.pdf): complete and return to register to take ELI placement test.
  2. Pay the $25 test registration fee.
  3. Take the ELI placement test.
  4. If accepted, you will be notified of when to attend the required orientation session 
  5. Be prepared to fulfill all mandatory[ medical requirements ](https://www.international.msstate.edu/offices/english-language-institute/program-information#medical)as part of orientation.
