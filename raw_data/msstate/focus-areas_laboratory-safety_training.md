#  Laboratory Safety Training
## Live Classes
### Lab Safety Training
General Lab Safety training is required annually for all laboratory personnel; this 1-hour course is designed to aid researchers in identifying hazards, understanding chemical and physical hazards, and to aid them in mitigating the inherent risks of working in a laboratory.
Content includes GHS hazard communication standards, Safety Data Sheets (SDS), risk mitigation strategies (engineering controls, administrative controls, personal protective equipment), and appropriate chemical storage, handling, and labeling.
  * [View Upcoming Sessions and Enroll](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/workshops)
  * [Request a Training](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/training/request-training)


### Non-Laboratory Chemical Safety
This training is required annually for individuals who work with chemicals outside of a laboratory setting. For example, individuals who utilized chemicals for cleaning or spraying pesticides or herbicides.
Content is designed to communicate safe chemical storage and handling.
  * [View Upcoming Sessions and Enroll](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/workshops)
  * [Request a Training](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/training/request-training)


### Lab Awareness Training
This course is intended for non-laboratory personnel who may work around labs or may visit labs. This course will introduce laboratory door signage, guide hazard communication and identification, highlight ways to protect yourself, and provide points of contact if you have any safety-related questions or concerns.
  * [View Upcoming Sessions and Enroll](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/workshops)
  * [Request a Training](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/training/request-training)


## Online Classes
When you click on a class below, you will be prompted to log into CAS and then taken to the MyState page where you can self-enroll. Once there, look for the "Canvas Self Enrollment Courses" section at the bottom of the page. You must then select the correct course from the "Select a Course" dropdown. Once you have selected your course, click "Confirm Enrollment?" and then click "Self Enroll". Your selected course will now be available through your Canvas Courses.
_Would you like to enroll your entire group?_ If you are an instructor, lecturer, or professor who teaches an online, hybrid, in-person course or laboratory and would like to have your class complete one of the online training courses offered by EH&S, please complete the Enroll Your Class in a Training Course eform below. Then, each member of the group can individually log into Canvas to complete their training.
