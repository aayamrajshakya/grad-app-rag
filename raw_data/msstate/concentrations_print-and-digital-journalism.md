#  Print & Digital Journalism
[Curriculum Guide (Prior to Fall 2023 Entry)](https://www.comm.msstate.edu/sites/www.comm.msstate.edu/files/2024-10/PDJ-Pre-FA23.pdf) [Curriculum Guide (Fall 2023 Entry+)](https://www.comm.msstate.edu/sites/www.comm.msstate.edu/files/2024-10/PDJ-FA23%2B.pdf)

Print & Digital Journalism offers courses in writing, editing, photography, reporting, digital storytelling, ethics and law.
Our graduates are immediately competitive applicants for positions at newspapers, magazines and digital publications
We offer a variety of internship experiences including local, regional and national media outlets.
