# Department of Management & Information Systems
Regardless of your specific career aspirations, expertise in management is valuable given its benefit in a variety of industries.
Regardless of your specific career aspirations, expertise in management is valuable given its benefit in a variety of industries. In Management coursework, students learn the principles of planning and organizing a business, managing human resources, and controlling the operations of a business. 
Likewise, a good background in business information systems is valuable as it prepares students to successfully develop, acquire, and integrate information technology across levels and functions of a firm in the continually changing global business environment.
As part of the College of Business, the Department of Management & Information Systems offers degrees at the Bachelor, Master, and Doctoral levels. Please take your time to explore our website. If you can't find what you're looking for, or if you have any questions, please contact us at 662.325.3928 or stop by and visit us at 302 McCool Hall.
