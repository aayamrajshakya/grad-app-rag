#  Equipping Future Entrepreneurs
Program Overview:  
The Equipping Future Entrepreneurs (EFE) program aims to bridge the gap between research and entrepreneurship. 
Program Goals:
  * Empower researchers to translate promising ideas and technologies into marketable products.
  * Foster an entrepreneurial mindset among participants.
  * Provide resources for technology transfer and commercialization.
  * Prepare participants for project launch or licensing.


Program Sessions:  
Our program consists of five interactive sessions designed to equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the world of research entrepreneurship.
