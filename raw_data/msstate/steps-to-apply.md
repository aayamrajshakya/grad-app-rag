# Steps to Apply
###  Step 1 
Create an FSA ID
The FSA ID is a username and password combination that allows you to sign your FAFSA electronically. Parent (if dependent student) and Student must both create an FSA ID.
[FSA ID](https://studentaid.gov/fsa-id/sign-in/landing)
###  Step 2 
Complete and e-sign the FAFSA on the Web
List MSU as a college to release information to.
School Code: 002423
[FAFSA How To](https://www.sfa.msstate.edu/steps-to-apply/fafsa-how-to) [studentaid.gov](https://studentaid.gov/)
###  Step 3 
Submit all requested documents to the Financial Aid Office
[SFA Forms](https://www.sfa.msstate.edu/forms)
###  Step 4 
Apply for Admission
Entering students must complete the MSU Admission Application process before eligibility for financial aid can be determined <http://apply.msstate.edu/>
###  Step 5 
Check your myState often and Accept your Financial Aid awards as soon as they are offered.
[MSU MyState](https://my.msstate.edu/)
###  Step 6 
Complete all loan-related requirements
If you have loans complete all loan-related requirements, such as Entrance Loan Counseling and MPN at [www.studentaid.gov](http://www.studentaid.gov/)
###  Step 7 
Refund Options
If aid exceeds account charges there are two refund options available:
  * E-Refund (direct deposit) to student's checking or savings
  * Paper check - processed weekly and mailed to home address on file


<https://www.controller.msstate.edu/accountservices/refunds/>
###  Step 8 
State Aid (for Mississippi Residents only)
[MS Financial Aid](https://www.msfinancialaid.org/)
###  Step 9 
Alternative/Private Loans
[Elm Select](http://www.elmselect.com/?schoolid=41#/)
###  Step 10 
Summer Aid
  * Aid year runs Fall, Spring, & Summer.
  * Summer is a separate term and is a trailer (end of Financial Aid award year).
  * Students wishing to receive student aid funds for summer school should register for summer classes and ensure your 2024-25 FAFSA is complete and the results are sent to our Financial Aid office.
