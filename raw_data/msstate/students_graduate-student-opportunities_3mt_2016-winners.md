#  3MT 2016 Winners
Congratulations to Caitlin Wenzel who was selected the Grand Champion winner of the 3 Minute Thesis Competition November 2016. Caitlin will advance and represent Mississippi State University at the 3 Minute Thesis Competition hosted by the Conference of Southern Graduate Schools in Annapolis, Maryland on March 4-5, 2017.
## Caitlin Wenzel, MS, Grand Champion
College of Veterinary Medicine  
Department of Clinical Sciences   
IV Magnesium Sulfate: A Rescue for Horse & Human Asthmatics  
Advisor: Dr. Jackie Bowser
