# Facility
* * *
The CAVS-Extension Center was built in 2003 near Nissan in Canton,MS and the center of the state to help facilitate work with manufacturers throughout Mississippi.
### Quality Laboratory
* * *
The Manufacturing and Quality Laboratory contains equipment and instrumentation focused on helping our clients improve quality.
  * 3D Laser Room Scanner
  * 3D Whitelight Scanner
  * FARO Measuring Arm
  * Coordinate Measurement Machine
  * Surface Roughness Tester
  * Optical Comparator
  * Laser Scan Micrometer
  * Digital Durometer
  * Ultrasonic Thickness Guage
  * Tensile Test Machine
  * Sanding Center
  * Metallurgical Microscope
  * Robot w/ Resistance Welder
  * Robot w/ Lincoln Mig Welder


Please call Jonathan Curtis at (601) 407-2731 for additional information.
### Meeting Areas
* * *
The CAVS Extension Center is a unique facility where business leaders, government officials, educators, and other professionals can gather to plan or train. Fulfill your business needs at the CAVS-E Center with state-of-the-art telecommunication capabilities, video conference meeting rooms, and computer class rooms.
  * Main Auditorium
  * Computer Class Room(s)
  * Activity Room
  * Conference Room


Please call Andrea McNaughten at (601) 407-2710 if you would like to rent or tour the meeting areas to make arrangements.
