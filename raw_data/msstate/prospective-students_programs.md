###  B.A. in Philosophy with a Concentration in Religion 
### Departmental Requirements
#### Philosophy Requirements
Option 1 — complete each of the following:
  * PHI 1103 - Introduction to Philosophy
  * PHI 1113 - Introduction to Logic


Option 2 — complete each of the following:
  * PHI 3023 - History of Western Philosophy I
  * PHI 3033 - History of Western Philosophy II


#### Religion Requirements
  * REL 1103 - Introduction to Religion
  * REL 3213 - World Religions I
  * REL 3223 - World Religions I
  * REL 3033 - Theory and Method in Study of Religion
  * Philosophy and Religion Electives (12 hours)
