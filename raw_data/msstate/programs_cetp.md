#  Chevron Engineering Transfer Program
Through a strategic collaboration between the Bagley College of Engineering and the Chevron Corporation, our Chevron Transfer Program offers invaluable support to transfer students transferring to Mississippi State University's Starkville and Gulf Coast Campuses.
Our mission is clear: to enhance student retention, facilitate a smooth transition from community college to a four-year institution, and elevate the academic performance of our students. This program is open to all MSU transfer students.
Key program components include:
  * A fun and informative Transfer Student Immersion Experience
  * [Supplemental Instruction](https://www.si.msstate.edu/) provided throughout the academic year for courses with the highest DFW rates (Defined as "W" for withdrawal and letter grades of D or F).
  * Personalized, supplemental academic advising available throughout the academic year to support students' academic journey.
  * Peer mentors and transfer-student events offered throughout the academic year to foster a supportive community and facilitate networking among peers.
  * Insightful industry visits to tour Chevron facilities, offering valuable insights into the engineering profession.
  * Engaging mixers with Chevron professionals, providing opportunities for students to connect with industry experts and gain invaluable career insights.


Join us as we pave the way for your academic success and professional growth!
### What is the Transfer Student Immersion Experience?
Designed exclusively for newly admitted transfer students joining engineering disciplines at Mississippi State University, our Immersion Experience is the ultimate preparation for success in the Bagley College of Engineering. It is an extended orientation that compliments information learned at your MSU Orientation through an immersive, multi-day, small-group experience.
Held during August before your MSU journey begins, our program offers a dive deep into the heart of engineering excellence at the Bagley College of Engineering. You'll sharpen your academic prowess with specialized workshops tailored to equip you for the challenges of third-year engineering courses. Plus, you'll have invaluable opportunities to connect with esteemed engineering faculty and mingle with fellow transfer students who share your passion and drive. Explore exciting undergraduate research opportunities while touring engineering research laboratories across campus.
