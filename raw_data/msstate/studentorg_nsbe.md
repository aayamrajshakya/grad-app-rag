### About the MSU Chapter
The Mississippi State Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) mission is to 'Increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.' We are open to STEM related majors. Our ultimate goal is to Engineer Black Excellence.
### Mission
The mission of the National Society of Black Engineers is **"to increase the number of culturally responsible Black Engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community."**
