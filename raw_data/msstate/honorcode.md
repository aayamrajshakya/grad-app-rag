> # "As a Mississippi State University student, I will conduct myself with honor and integrity at all times. I will not lie, cheat, or steal, nor will I accept the actions of those who do."
## Reporting a Case
The violations in the Student Honor Code are: Cheating, Plagiarism, Academic Fraud, Complicity, Multiple Submissions, Fabrication, Falsification, and Violation of Dept/College Rules. If you or someone you know has knowledge of an honor code violation, we strongly encourage you to let us know.
[Report a Case](https://www.honorcode.msstate.edu/reporting-case)

## Academic Integrity Program
The Academic Integrity Program (AIP) is the educational sanction offered through the Honor Code process. It contains a library workshop and an online seminar.
[Academic Integrity](https://www.honorcode.msstate.edu/aip)
