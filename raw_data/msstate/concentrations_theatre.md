In the Theatre concentration, students pursue a broad course of study   
  
MSU theatre students take courses in acting, stagecraft, playwrighting, and directing, stages four Mainstage productions each year,  
  
As an undergraduate-only training program, MSU students can build substantial performance or technical resumes before they graduate.  
  
Theatre MSU is notable in Mississippi as one of the only programs with a sustained commitment to Theatre for Young Audiences,  
  
If you have a unique experience and a unique story to tell, please consider telling it with us!
