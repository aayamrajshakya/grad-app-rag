# Captain Kermit O. Evans, Sr. Annual Scholarship
#### Background
Capt Kermit O. Evans, 31, an Explosive Ordnance Disposal officer, was killed Dec. 3, 2006 in Iraq when a helicopter he was riding in made an emergency water landing in western Al Anbar Province. CPT Evans was a native of Hollandale, Mississippi. CPT Evans was also an alumi of Mississippi State University, with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Chemical Engineering.
In June 2010, his wife, Perneatha Evans, established the Captain Kermit O. Evans, Sr. Scholarship Fund at Mississippi State University.
* * *
#### Criteria
Candidates for the Captain Kermit O. Evans, Sr. Scholarship Fund must meet the following criteria:
  * Must be a veteran, active service member or a dependent attending Mississippi State University.
  * Provide an essay documenting how they have made a difference through community service.
  * Preference will be given to a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. at Mississippi State University.
