#  Bachelor of Music in Performance
## Bachelor of Music in Performance
The Bachelor of Music with a Major in Performance is a 121 hour professional degree program that prepares students to work independently towards performance at the highest possible level; possess knowledge of applicable solo and ensemble literature; and to gain experience with the fundamentals of music pedagogy. The Department of Music offers four concentrations of the BM in Performance: Guitar, Instrumental, Keyboard and Vocal.
