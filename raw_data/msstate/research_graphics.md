# Graphics
Computer graphics studies the digital display of information in manners realistic, illustrative, or informative. From movie screens to video games, medical imagery to weather forecast, computer graphics impacts our daily lives. MSU faculty research novel methods for visualizing, analyzing, and interacting with data, and perceiving information through augmented and virtual reality.
