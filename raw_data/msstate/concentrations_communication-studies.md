In Communication & Media Studies, we specialize in the theory, critique, and practice of communication.   
  
A significant advantage of the concentration in Communication & Media Studies is its flexibility: Graduates in Communication & Media Studies have pursued jobs in community outreach and engagement, including advocacy work, community organizing, diplomacy, grant writing, non-profit work, politics, and public affairs.  
  
Many students interested in graduate work
