# Dawg Days
Dawg Days is an array of shared activities and events during the two weeks prior to fall semester classes. All are designed to help incoming freshmen and transfer students become acclimated to the Starkville campus and life as a Bulldog.
[ Learn More about Dawg Days](http://www.dawgdays.msstate.edu/)
