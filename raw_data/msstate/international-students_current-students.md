#  Current Students
The International Services Office is here to assist our international students in acclimating to campus life, maintaining valid immigration status, and taking advantage of immigration benefits.
Advisors are available by appointment. [Contact an advisor.](https://www.international.msstate.edu/offices/international-services-office/advisors/)
[International Student Portal](https://iso.msstate.edu/index.cfm?FuseAction=Security.AngLogin)
## Know your Documents
  * Passport
  * Visa
  * I-20/DS-2019[ISO portal](https://iso.msstate.edu/) at least 30 days prior to your current program end date. 
  * I-94[download your new I-94](https://i94.cbp.dhs.gov/home) each time you re-enter the United States, and you should carry your I-94 with your other immigration documents. 
  * Employment Authorization Document (EAD card)


## Traveling Internationally?
F-1 and J-1 students (including exchange students)
Be sure to request a travel signature through the [ISO portal](https://iso.msstate.edu/) at least two weeks prior to your departure date. You will need to upload scans of your passport/visa and enter your travel dates. An advisor will review your request and send you an email to arrange a physical or digital travel signature. 
J-1 scholars
Please email your ISO advisor to request a copy of the J-1 travel form. You will need to fill the form and email it back to your advisor, along with scans of your passport, visa, and DS-2019. An advisor will review your request and send you an email to arrange a physical or digital travel signature.
