#  Financial Conflict of Interest Training
FCOI training is offered in Canvas and the Financial Disclosure Form is an eForm with sections for reporting Significant Financial Interests, Foreign Government Talent Recruitment Programs, and Other Support.
* * *
As of July 1, 2020
  * To Self Enroll in FCOI training in Canvas (As of October 2022):


  1. Go to [my.msstate.edu](https://my.msstate.edu/ "myState")
  2. Scroll down the bottom of your myState
  3. Under the "Classroom" section, the "Canvas Course Self Enrollment" box is located on the left hand side. This training is NOT
  4. Click on "Select a course" and choose Financial Conflict Of Interest 2024/2025
  5. Mark the sliding tab that says "Self enroll, Yes/No" and mark "Yes" and then "Confirm"
  6. Go to [canvas.msstate.edu](https://canvas.msstate.edu/ "MSU Canvas") to confirm the course is available to you on the Dashboard. Note - you may need to scroll down past any announcements.


A minimum score of 75% is required to successfully complete the training course.
