## Camp Overview 2025

## Camp Activities
  * Various sports related activities such basketball, volleyball, kickball, wiffleball, kanjam, ultimate frisbee, soccer, etc.
  * Climbing Wall
  * Bouldering
  * Swimming
  * Nutrition Demos
  * Various Arts & Crafts related to annual camp theme
