#  Student Veterans Association
## Mission Statement
The Student Veterans Association is a student led organization comprised of veterans, service members, dependents, survivors and other military supporters. We work to help the military affiliated students at Mississippi State University and our community through social events, fundraisers, community service projects, fitness programs and provide several other support services.
