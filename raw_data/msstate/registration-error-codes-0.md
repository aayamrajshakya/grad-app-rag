# Registration Error Codes
When registering for classes, you may run into some issues. You'll receive a light red box in the top right corner of your registration page. This box gives you an error message. This chart gives you meaning behind the different messages you may receive. 
** Please note, when contacting the department, please refer to the [Course Override](https://www.advising.msstate.edu/course-overrides) page and the [College Override Contact](https://www.advising.msstate.edu/course-overrides/college-override-contacts) page. These pages show the ways different departments require you to request overrides, as well as information that is needed for overrides **
Contact your advisor if you have questions or issues.
