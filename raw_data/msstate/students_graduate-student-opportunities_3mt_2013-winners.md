#  3MT 2013 Winners
Congratulations to Josh Winter who was the Grand Champion winner of the 3 minute Thesis Competition. Josh will advance and represent Mississippi State University at the 3 Minute Thesis Competition hosted by the Conference of Southern Graduate Schools in San Antonio, Tx on February 22, 2014.
## Josh Winter, Grand Champion
Masters Thesis  
Title: The Effect of the Flipped Model on an Intro Physics Course  
Department of Curriculum, Instruction and Special Education  
Faculty Advisor: Dr. Ryan Walker
## Evangel Kummari, First Runner up
Doctoral Dissertation

Department of Basic Sciences  
Faculty Advisor: Dr. Jeffrey Eells
