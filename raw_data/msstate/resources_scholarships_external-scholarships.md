# External Scholarships
Please note that the scholarships listed here are provided by external organizations and are not administered by Mississippi State University or the Center for America's Veterans. All application processes, interviews, and requirements are determined solely by the respective scholarship providers. For specific details or inquiries, please contact the awarding organization directly.
