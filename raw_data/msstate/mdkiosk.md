#  MSU Math Domain
The Math Domain serves students enrolled in MA 01013 (Intermediate Algebra), MA 1313 (College Algebra), MA 1323 (Trigonometry), MA 1613 (Business Calculus), and MA/ST 2113 (Intro to Statistics).
Students come to the Math Domain to fulfill lab requirements, complete homework, see assistance on course assignments, and take quizzes and tests for each respective course (students should refer to their syllabus for more information). 
The Math Domain is staffed with faculty and student workers who are available to answer questions and help students understand various mathematical concepts.
We love math and we want to share that with each student that comes through our door!
