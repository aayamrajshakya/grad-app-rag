Full-Time Students
There is no membership fee for full-time students. To be considered full-time, an undergraduate student must be enrolled in 12 or more hours at MSU in the fall or spring semester, or at least 6 hours in the summer term; graduate students must must be enrolled in 9 or more hours at MSU in the fall or spring semester, or at least 6 hours in the summer term.
Student Membership Dates
August 1 - December 20 (Fall)  
January 2 - May 10 (Spring)   
May 11 - August 6 (Summer)
Corporate 5+  
Local businesses may be eligible for the corporate rate. Must pay for 5 or more employees to receive rate. Contact the member services office at 325.7244 to inquire.  
Early Bird  
Early Bird access is allowed 5:30am-11am, Monday-Friday and all operating hours during weekends, holidays and interim schedules.  
Family Plans  
Partner/Spouse must show proof of same residence. No Children over 17 years of age allowed.

Student Family Plans  
Partner/Spouse must show proof of same residence. Student Family memberships end when student's membership ends. No Children over 17 years of age allowed.
