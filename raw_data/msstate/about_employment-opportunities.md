#  Employment Opportunities
Welcome to the College of Veterinary Medicine at Mississippi State University, where "Taking Care of What Matters" is more than a motto—it's our mission. Our college is renowned for its exceptional clinical education, with students in the four-year DVM program spending two full years in clinics and completing over 55 surgeries before graduation. We foster a collaborative environment enriched by diverse opportunities in clinical services, research programs, and community outreach activities that rival any veterinary college.
Join our team of dedicated faculty and staff, and benefit from competitive compensation, a supportive community, and a commitment to work/life balance. Enjoy the perks of Mississippi’s low housing prices and affordable cost of living, enabling a comfortable and fulfilling lifestyle. If you're passionate about veterinary medicine and want to make a difference, we invite you to apply and become a part of our family.
