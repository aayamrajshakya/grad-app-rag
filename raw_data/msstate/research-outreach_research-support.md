##  Policies and Procedures   

[ Research Policies and Procedures  Proper administration of sponsored project funds includes policies set forth by sponsors, and policies, procedures, and guidance set forth by Mississippi State University. ](https://www.osp.msstate.edu/procedures) [ Limited Submissions Notices  External funding agencies, such as the National Institutes of Health, National Science Foundation, U.S. Department of Agriculture and U.S. Department of Energy, often limit the number of proposal submissions that a university may submit. This information is always provided in the agency announcement. To make sure that Mississippi State University provides an equal opportunity to all researchers pertaining to a limited submission, it is necessary that a process be in place to ensure an equal opportunity. Therefore, all limited submissions are required to go through the Office of Research and Economic Development (ORED) before being submitted to OSP. ](https://www.research.msstate.edu/initiatives/limited-submissions)

##  MSU Institutional Information   

OSP has compiled general information you may need for writing your grant proposal. Information such as the university DUNS number, EIN, and institutional representatives can be found on the [OSP home page](https://www.osp.msstate.edu/index.php) on the bottom left corner.
##  Budget Basics   

Listed below are several tools to help you prepare your grant or project budget.
  * College of Education Budget Template: Helpful spreadsheet that will do budget calculations, such as increases in salary and indirect costs (F&A), for you. Please contact Kyle Baggerly at k.baggerly@msstate.edu for the most up-to-date version.
  * [Preparing Budget Justifications](https://www.educ.msstate.edu/sites/www.educ.msstate.edu/files/inline-files/Budget%20Justification%20Template%20Guide.docx "Budget Justification Template Guide"): Information on how to prepare your budget justification.
  * [F&A Cost Rate Agreement](https://www.controller.msstate.edu/cas/casex/facilities.php): Our indirect cost rates are determined by an agreement with the federal government and MSU.


##  MSU Trainings   

  * [ORED Seminar Series](https://www.research.msstate.edu/seminars/): Seminars offered both in the fall and spring. Topics include IRB, project evaluation and writing tips.
  * [Library Workshops](http://lib.msstate.edu/workshops/): Workshops are scheduled throughout the semester and taught by knowledgeable MSU faculty and staff. Topics include research tools and methods, citation management and copyright.
