# CAEP Accountability Measures
The Council for Accreditation of Educator Preparation (CAEP) requires annual reporting of four accountability measures from the Educator Preparation Provider (EPP-College of Education) demonstrating accountability and performance of the initial-undergraduate licensure teacher education programs and advanced graduate licensure educational leadership programs to share with stakeholders and prospective students.
##  Impact Measures   

Impact Measures
Measure 1 (Initial). Completer effectiveness and impact on P-12 learning and development
  * [Measure 1: Completer Effectiveness (Component R4.1 - Initial)](https://www.educ.msstate.edu/indicators-teaching-effectiveness)
  * [Measure 1: Completer Impact (Component R4.1 - Initial)](https://www.educ.msstate.edu/impact-p-12-learning)


Measure 2 (Initial and/or Advanced). Satisfaction of employers and stakeholder involvement
  * [Measure 2: Satisfaction of Employers and Stakeholder Involvement (Component R4.2, R5.3 - Initial)](https://www.educ.msstate.edu/about/accreditation/satisfaction-employers-stakeholder-involvement)
  * [Measure 2: Satisfaction of Employers and Stakeholder Involvement (Component RA.4.1 - Advanced](https://www.educ.msstate.edu/about/satisfaction-employers-stakeholder-involvement)
