# Department of Finance & Economics
The Department of Finance and Economics serves the state of Mississippi and the world by providing advanced educational opportunities for students in the fields of economics, finance, and insurance.
The Department of Finance and Economics serves the state of Mississippi and the world by providing advanced educational opportunities for students in the fields of economics, finance, and insurance. As part of the College of Business, we offer degrees at the Bachelor, Master, and Doctoral levels. This website is intended to provide you with an overview of our department's degree programs, student organizations, and exceptional learning facilities.
If you can't find what you're looking for or just have a question, please contact us at 662.325.2342 or stop by and visit us at 312 McCool Hall.
