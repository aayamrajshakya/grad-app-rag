#  New Students
Congratulations on your acceptance to The Graduate School at Mississippi State University! On behalf of the students, administrators, graduate faculty and staff, I welcome you to this important phase of your academic and professional development. We are pleased you decided to pursue graduate studies at Mississippi State, and we will do our part to ensure your educational needs are met. As a Mississippi State graduate student, you will join a dynamic student community in a place of endless opportunities. Mississippi State University offers an unparalleled setting for the next chapter of your life.
