# Radiation Safety
Background Image Alternative Text: Ben Crider of ICET using a radiation measuring device
##    
Exposure History Request 
Radioactive material and other sources of ionizing radiation are used for teaching and research at MSU. The Radiation Safety Program is responsible for promoting the safe use of ionizing radiation to prevent unnecessary exposure to MSU faculty, staff, students, the general public, and the environment.
[Exposure History Request Form](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/focus-areas/radiation-safety/exposure-history-request)
##    
Authorized Users 
MSU operates under an educational broad scope license. This license allows us to utilize a variety of radioactive materials for research and educational purposes.
[Authorized Users](https://www.ehs.msstate.edu/focus-areas/radiation-safety/authorized-users)
