# Dak - Bully XXII
The official mascot of Mississippi State University is an English Bulldog. There have been 22 live mascots serve as Bully. The current Bully goes by Dak, but is still considered a Bully. Bully's have inherited their status in a ceremony called Passing of the Harness, typically held during Super Bulldog Weekend.
