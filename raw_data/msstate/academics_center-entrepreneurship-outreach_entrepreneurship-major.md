#  Entrepreneurship Major
Want to be your own boss? Whether you dream of launching your own company, growing a family firm, or driving innovation inside an existing business, the Entrepreneurship major at Mississippi State University gives you the tools and support to make it happen. 
### Entrepreneurship Major - 120 hour program
Entrepreneurs are the driving force behind innovation, growth, and economic development. The Entrepreneurship major at Mississippi State empowers students to think creatively, act strategically, and lead boldly. Our program prepares students for success as:
  * Founders of startups and service-based companies
  * Leaders in family businesses
  * Innovators inside corporations and nonprofits
  * Social entrepreneurs and change makers


With courses designed by successful entrepreneurs, hands-on project work, and extensive co-curricular support, our students are equipped to lead from day one.
