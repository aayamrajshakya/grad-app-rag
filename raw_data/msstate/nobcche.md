# National Organization for the Professional Advancement of Black Chemists and Chemical Engineers (NOBCChE)
The National Organization for the Professional Advancement of Black Chemists and Chemical Engineers​(NOBCChE) seeks to build an eminent cadre of people of color in science and technology​. The MSU student chapter provides:
  * Leadership and professional development opportunities​
  * Access to internships, colleges, graduate schools, and careers​
  * Mentoring and outreach​


For more information about NOBCChE student chapter at MSU, email msunobcche@gmail.com.
