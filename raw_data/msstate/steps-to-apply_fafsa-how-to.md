##  Notification of FAFSA Process Completion   

Students who provide an email address on their FAFSA will receive an email notification from the U.S. Department of Education confirming that their FAFSA has been processed and sent to their selected schools.
To ensure accuracy, review your FAFSA information online and verify that Mississippi State University is listed as a recipient (Federal School Code: 002423)
* * *
##  Complete Verification if Selected   

If your FAFSA is selected for verification, you will receive an email notification from the U.S. Department of Education and/or the MSU Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships. Verification requires that we collect additional documents from you and your parents (if applicable) to confirm the accuracy of your FAFSA information before proceeding with the review process.
You may also be required to submit other documents to our office. To check for any outstanding requirements or additional documentation needed log into your [myState](https://my.msstate.edu) account, select Banner, Students, Financial Aid and Scholarships, [_Eligibility Requirements_](https://mybanner.msstate.edu/BannerExtensibility/customPage/page/msuFADocReqTracking).

##  Providing Tax Information   

Transfer Tax Data Using FTI:
FAFSA contributors (student, parent/stepparent, spouse, as applicable) must provide consent for the U.S. Department of Education (ED) to retrieve Federal Tax Information (FTI) from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). This consent allows tax return information, if filed, to be securely transferred for determining a student's eligibility for Title IV federal student aid. 
Providing Tax Information for Non-Filers:
  1. Visit our [Forms](https://www.sfa.msstate.edu/forms) page
  2. Choose the appropriate form based on undergraduate or graduate student and academic year


  * Dependent Students:Submit the Dependent Student Nontax Filer Form
  * Independent Students:Submit the Independent Student Nontax Filer Form
  * Parents of Students:Submit the Parent Nontax Filer Form
  * Spouses (Student’s or Parent’s Spouse):Submit the Spouse Nontax Filer Form


Providing Tax Information for Filers:

##  Amended Returns   

If you filed an amended tax return, you must submit:
  * A signed copy of the original IRS 1040 tax return, and
  * A signed copy of the amended tax return (IRS Form 1040-X).


Submitting Other Required MSU Forms:
