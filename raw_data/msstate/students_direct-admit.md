#  Direct Admit
Direct Admit - designed for domestic students interested in a non-thesis master's degree. 
What's the difference between "thesis" and "non-thesis"? Watch the video below.
### Direct Admit allows applicants who meet certain criteria to be automatically admitted to participating programs.
Step 1  
Complete the online application, pay the application fee, and submit any required documents.
Step 2  
The admissions team will evaluate your application. If you meet the criteria for a Direct Admit program, you’ll be admitted automatically. Otherwise, your application will go to the department for further review.
No waiting. No stress. No delays.
* * *
##### DIRECT ADMISSION CRITERIA:
  * Bachelor's degree in major (Exception: Program marked with * will accept any major)
  * GPA of 3.0 or higher
  * Three supportive recommendation letters (not required for certificates)


##### ENGINEERING ONLY - DIRECT ADMISSION CRITERIA:
  * ABET-accredited bachelor’s degree in major
  * GPA of 3.5 or higher
  * Three supportive recommendation letters (not required for certificates)
