#  Request for Airport Pickup
## Fall Airport Pickups
The International Institute provides airport pickup services at Golden Triangle Regional Airport (GTR) for new, incoming international students only.
Fall pickups will be offered from August 1, 2025 through August 19, 2025.
If you have any questions about airport pickup, please email  _international@msstate.edu_.
