#  Graduate Programs
Today's business environment is one of constant change. A Master of Professional Accountancy (MPA) or Master of Taxation (MTX) degree from Mississippi State University will further your knowledge and equip you with the skills needed for a successful professional career in accountancy.
* * *
## Master of Professional Accountancy (MPA)
The objective of the Master of Professional Accountancy (MPA) program is to further the student’s education in business and accounting while preparing for a professional career in accountancy. The accountancy program is accredited by AACSB International, the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business, as part of the overall accreditation of the College of Business, as well as the separate and additional accreditation of accounting programs at both the graduate and undergraduate levels.
[MPA Admission & Completion Requirements](http://catalog.msstate.edu/graduate/colleges-degree-programs/business/accountancy/#text)
(Please note: no more than 12 hours of coursework in the 30-hour program may be at the 6000 level.)
* * *
## Master of Taxation (MTX)
The primary objective of the Master of Taxation (MTX) program is to further the student’s education in business and accounting with an emphasis in taxation while preparing for a professional career in accounting and taxation.
[MTX Admission & Completion Requirements](http://catalog.msstate.edu/graduate/colleges-degree-programs/business/accountancy/#programsofstudytext)
(Please note: no more than 12 hours of coursework in the 30-hour program may be at the 6000 level.)
