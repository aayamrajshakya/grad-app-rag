Who Is Eligible for Induction?
To be considered for induction into the BCoE Student Hall of Fame, students must be nominated by an MSU faculty/staff member or by the President of an Engineering Student Organization.
  * Nominees must be an undergraduate or a graduate student currently enrolled in a degree program housed within the Bagley College of Engineering who are scheduled to graduate in May, August or December 2025
  * To be inducted, nominees must also be in good academic and disciplinary standing.


### 2025 Nominations are now closed.
