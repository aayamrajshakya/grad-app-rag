#  Master's in Disability Studies
##  Master's in Disability Studies   

The concentration in Disability Studies develops knowledgeable professionals who can address the physical activity and rehabilitation needs of persons experiencing disabilities. In addition, the program prepares students seeking advanced graduate study leading to a doctoral degree. The Disability Studies program accommodates students with diverse interests within Kinesiology.
For information about graduate study in the Department of Kinesiology, contact the Graduate coordinator, [Dr. Adam Knight](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/aknight "Dr. Adam Knight"). Students desiring to pursue a master's degree in Disability Studies may also wish to contact relevant members of the graduate faculty individually to discuss their research interests: [Dr. Stamatis Agiovlasitis](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/sagiovlasitis "Dr. Stamatis Agiovlasitis"), [Dr. Stanley Brown](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/spb107 "Dr. Stanley P. Brown"), [Dr. Adam Knight](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/aknight "Dr. Adam Knight"), [Dr. John Lamberth](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/jgl "Dr. John Lamberth"), and [Dr. Zhujun Pan](https://www.kinesiology.msstate.edu/directory/zp147 "Dr. Zhujun Pan").
