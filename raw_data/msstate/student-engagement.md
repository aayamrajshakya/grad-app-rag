##  Maroon Volunteer Center   

In the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina in 2005, many students with ties to the Mississippi Gulf Coast decided to work together on relief projects. Their initiative became The Maroon Volunteer Center.
[ Learn More on the MVC](https://www.engage.msstate.edu/student-engagement/maroon-volunteer-center)
##  Study Abroad   

The Office of Study Abroad is responsible for coordinating the study abroad opportunities for MSU students along with overseeing all aspects of Faculty-led program offerings. OSA is the primary resource for students regarding advising and assisting them through the entire study abroad process.
[ Discover More on Study Abroad](https://www.engage.msstate.edu/student-engagement/study-abroad)
