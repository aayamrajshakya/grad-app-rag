# THE HUB | REVIEW REQUEST
MSU seeks to ensure all advertising and external marketing materials meet quality standards and follow brand guidelines. This includes print advertising, digital ads and graphics, radio and television ads, billboards, magazines (pre-publication proofs), webpages, e-newsletters and other promotional materials
[Review Request Form](https://form.jotform.com/231415555831151)
For example, communications targeting external audiences, including magazines, brochures, flyers, invitations, student recruitment materials, posters, conference brochures, and programs, are to be submitted to MSU’s Office of Public Affairs for review at the final proof stage.
Please work through your area’s lead communications representative for submissions. Items may be submitted via the above Review Request Form button. If you’re unsure of your communications representative, contact MSU’s Office of Public Affairs, or OPA, at 662-325-0442.
OPA will respond with approvals or requests for more information within 2-5 business days for small projects5-7 business days for larger projects10-14 business days for special projects
For urgent requests, contact Sid Salter, chief communications officer, at 662-325-7454 or ssalter@opa.msstate.edu.
