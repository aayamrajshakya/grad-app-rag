##  Bachelor of University Studies   

##  About the Program   

KEY INFORMATION:
The Bachelor of University Studies at Mississippi State University offers a unique pathway to graduation, diverging from traditional single-discipline majors. This program recognizes that today's professionals often require interdisciplinary knowledge and skills, ensuring graduates are prepared for diverse career opportunities.
Designed for students with 90+ credit hours who have not yet completed a bachelor's degree, the major focuses on leveraging prior learning while integrating coursework from multiple disciplines. Students work with academic advisors to create a customized educational plan that aligns with their career goals and builds upon their existing knowledge base.
