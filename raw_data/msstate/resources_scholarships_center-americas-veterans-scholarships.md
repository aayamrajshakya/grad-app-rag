# Center for America's Veterans Scholarships
The Center for Americaâ€™s Veterans Scholarships are provided by alumni and friends of Mississippi State University. These scholarships are to assist student veterans, service-members and dependents enrolled at Mississippi State University with any un-met financial needs while attending school. 
* * *
#### Scholarships Awarded
  * Center for America's Veterans Scholarship
  * Tom Crosby Scholarship
  * Russell Gaines Scholarship
  * William C. Forbus Scholarship
  * Leslie Hester Scholarship
  * Tommy Nusz Scholarship


* * *
#### Criteria
  * Must be a veteran, service-member or dependent of a veteran / service-member of any branch of the service.
  * Must have un-met financial needs based on the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).


* * *
#### Award
All scholarships are awarded annually in the amount of $1000(Except Nusz), contingent upon the funds received.
