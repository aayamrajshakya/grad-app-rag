#  Our Expertise
##  Our Expertise   

Our work covers the gamut of social science research with each discipline conducting quantitative and qualitative data analysis. We partner with faculty, researchers, and agencies to conduct work whether it is across campus at MSU, at the state or national capital, in communities across the country, or even around the globe.
##  Evaluation   

Our Evaluation and Research Group has expertise to help with every step of the evaluation process in a variety of subject areas including juvenile justice; public health; early childhood, K-12, and post-secondary education; substance use disorders and more. Contact us for information about designing an evaluation plan for your funded project or your work.
[ Learn More about our evaluations](https://evaluation.ssrc.msstate.edu/)
##  Survey Research   

Our Wolfgang Frese Survey Research Laboratory has completed 100’s of phone and online surveys. They can assist in data collection and analysis for agencies, nonprofits, and other groups in Mississippi and around the globe.
[ Learn More about our survey research](https://srl.ssrc.msstate.edu/)
##  Policy & Systems Change   

We’re looking to address and change policies and systems to bring about a stronger future for all.
[ Learn More about our policy work](https://scl.ssrc.msstate.edu/)
##  Substance Abuse   

Through our intervention program, researchers are working to reduce DUI recidivism rates and increase highway safety in Mississippi. 
[ Learn More about our intervention program](https://masep.org/)
##  Data Science   

With our secure data lab and expert scientists, we can help you apply data science techniques and methods including data visualization to social science research and program implementation. Our data science work spans across a variety of data sources including state agency data and open-source data such as social media content and images.
[ Learn More about our data science work](https://ds3.ssrc.msstate.edu/)
##  Juvenile Justice   

We have been researching policies and structures around juvenile justice for decades, working with both court systems and state agencies.
[ Learn More Learn more about our work in Juvenile Justice](https://cjru.ssrc.msstate.edu)
##  Communication Science   

Our Message Laboratory provides communication research support and comprehensive message development support for research collaborators.
[ Learn More about our communication work](https://commlab.ssrc.msstate.edu/)
##  Public Health   

Our Tobacco Control Unit exists to provide objective, timely data to inform programmatic and policy decisions regarding tobacco control. Additionally, we conduct work around early childhood development and milestones impacting the children of Mississippi and have research teams studying the demography of several populations.
