# Courses
Dr. Daniel Peterson is a member of the faculty of the [Department of Plant & Soil Sciences](http://www.pss.msstate.edu/) at Mississippi State University. He is also a member of the MS State [Biochemistry, Molecular Biology, Entomology & Plant Pathology](http://www.biochemistry.msstate.edu//) graduate faculty. In alternating fall semesters, Peterson teaches the graduate courses Genomes and GenomicsTopics In Genomics

## Genomes & Genomics
NOTEGenomes & Genomicsetc[MySTATE](https://my.msstate.edu/cp/home/displaylogin) portal.
CATALOG DESCRIPTION  
PSS/BCH 8653. Genomes and Genomics. (3) Three hours lecture. (Prerequisites: BCH 4113/6113 or BCH 4713/6713 or BCH 8643 or consent of instructor). Overview of genome structure and evolution with emphasis on genomics, the use of molecular biology, robotics, and advanced computational methods to efficiently study genomes.
For the most recent course syllabus, [click here](https://www.igbb.msstate.edu/downloads/G&G_syllabus2014.pdf).
