Your safety is our number one priority. While attending classes at the MGCCC campus, consider these safety tips.
  * Become familiar with MGCCC Evacuation and Shelter in Place instructions.
  * Verify that you are receiving MGCCC alerts in addition to MSU Maroon Alerts.
  * Develop a communication plan to get help or inform friends and family of your situation.
  * Know evacuation routes for your area.


For more information about MGCCC emergency information, please visit: [https://mgccc.edu/about/campus-police-safety](https://mgccc.edu/about/campus-police-safety/).
## Receiving Alerts
You will be enrolled in MGCCC alerts while attending courses at the coast. For questions about the alert system, please contact Chief Charles Houston, cary.houston@mgccc.edu or Dr. Ashley Rollin, ashley.rollin@mgccc.edu.
For information about your MSU account and Banner information, contact servicedesk@its.msstate.edu.
