# Thomas Crosby Annual Scholarship
#### Background
In June 2012, Thomas Crosby established the Thomas Crosby Annual Scholarship, in the G.V. “Sonny” Montgomery Center for America’s Veterans. The annual scholarship is awarded to deserving students enrolled at Mississippi State University who is a veteran in the College of Business.
* * *
#### Criteria
Candidates for the Thomas Crosby Annual Scholarship must meet the following criteria:
  * Must be a full-time undergraduate student majoring in Business at Mississippi State University.
  * Must be a veteran
  * Must have demonstrated record of academic achievement, with at least a cumulative GPA of 3.0 (for incoming freshman as well).
