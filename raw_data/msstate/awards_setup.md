#  Set Up
## Award Notice from Sponsor
  * Forward all award documents immediately to OSP. Do not sign.


## Award Negotiation
  * Grant awards/contracts are legally binding documents.
  * Terms and conditions must be reviewed carefully.
  * Award review is carried out by OSP, Legal Counsel, the Office of Technology Management, and/or the Office of Research Security, depending on the terms.
  * After review, OSP will negotiate with the sponsor.
  * Once terms are agreed upon, OSP and the sponsor will sign the document.


## Award Notification from OSP
  * Once signed, OSP will process in Banner.
  * PI and designated contacts will receive a Notice of Award email.
  * OSP will forward the original award documents to Sponsored Programs Accounting.
  * SPA will load the award budget into Banner and assign a fund number to the award.
