##  COURSE CURRICULUM   

## Course Curriculum
Four carefully designed courses that build upon each other to develop comprehensive leadership capabilities.
1
### PCS 6343: Foundations of Organizational Leadership
Discover the power of personal accountability, building relationships, effective communications, proper prioritization of tasks, and empowering people to create a positive impact on organizations and their missions.
Key Focus:
2
### PCS 6333: Principles of Effective Leadership
Examine the balance within leadership by analyzing common dichotomies that leaders must navigate to be effective. Develop the skills to foster curiosity, build credibility, and drive positive outcomes for your team and organization.
Key Focus:
3
### PCS 6313: Organizational Culture
Explore the concept of organizational culture and how effective leadership can influence and enhance it. Learn strategies for creating positive culture change that drives results.
Key Focus:
4
### PCS 6323: Effective Organizational Discourse
Investigate the principles of effective discourse within an organization. Understand how clear communication drives meaningful change and learn to master difficult conversations.
