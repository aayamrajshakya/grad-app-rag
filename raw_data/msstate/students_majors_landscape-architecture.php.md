# Landscape Architecture Major
Get ready for a career where you'll engage in shaping the environmental and cultural landscape through planning and design to improve quality of life. This program teaches the artful synthesis of social and ecological processes related to planning, designing, building and managing regenerative communities. You will experience an immersive, intense, and rewarding education structured around a studio environment that promotes critical thinking and creative problem solving.
## [Landscape Architecture](https://www.lalc.msstate.edu/students/la-prospective.php)
Get ready to plan and design environmental and cultural landscapes that improve quality of life while sustaining our ecosystems. The BLA program is the only LAAB accredited bachelor of landscape architecture program in the three-state region of Alabama, Mississippi, and Tennessee.
### Career Opportunities:
  * Environmental specialist—median annual salary of $71,130
  * Land surveyor—median annual salary of $51,0200
  * Landscape architect—median annual salary of $ 65,760
  * Urban and regional planner—median annual salary of $73,050
