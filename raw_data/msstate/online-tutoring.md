# The Writing Center
Students may access resources from the [Writing Center](http://www.writingcenter.msstate.edu/) tutoring program to help students develop writing and critical thinking skills. Tutoring is available online for MS State Online students.
Follow the steps below to schedule an appointment:
  1. Go to the [WCONLINE 5](http://msstate.mywconline.com) website.
  2. Click on "Register for an account(You may skip this step if you are a returning student.)
  3. Return to the main page and log in to the scheduler.
  4. After logging in, select the drop down menu beneath the heading "Current Schedule" and select "ONLY Distance Learners (MSU Writing Center Online).
  5. Browse the available appointments, click on an available time slot (white block), fill out the required information, and select "Yesâ€”Meet Online
  6. Click "Save Appointment
  7. In order to start the online appointment, log in to the same schedule, and click on the orange block (your appointment time). Next, click on "Start or Join Online Consultation
