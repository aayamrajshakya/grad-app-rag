#  Small Town Revitalization 
Follow us on social media (@smalltowncenter) to stay up to date about STR projects!
[](https://www.smalltowncenter.msstate.edu/sites/www.smalltowncenter.msstate.edu/files/2024-06/STRP_IDENTITY_SYSTEM_WORKING_FINAL_STR_PIN_WORDMARK_TAGLINE_HORIZONTAL_COLOR.png)
The Small Town Revitalization, STR, is a community-focused placemaking and revitalization program supported by the Small Town Center.
Twenty-seven applications were received, and funds were awarded to 11 communities to support projects ranging from master plans to pocket parks to street art to retail hubs and more.
