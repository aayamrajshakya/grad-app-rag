### Research Interests
Biomass pyrolysis/liquefaction and bio-oil upgrading to transportation fuels.  
Catalytic conversion of biomass to liquid fuels and value-added chemicals.  
Sustainable carbon materials as adsorbents for water purification and CO2  
Nanocellulose-based composites for packaging and environmental applications.
