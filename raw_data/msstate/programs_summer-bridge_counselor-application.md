#  Summer Bridge Counselor Application
Thank you for your interest in becoming a counselor/mentor for the Bagley College of Engineering's Summer Bridge program which is sponsored by Ergon.
  * Counselors will be expected to live in the Summer Bridge dorm for the duration of the program. 
    * Move-in for counselors will be May 31 before noon.
    * Move-in for participants will be June 1.
    * Move-out will be July 3.
  * Additionally, counselors will be required to attend multiple training sessions prior to Summer Bridge in March, April, and May.
  * Counselors will receive free housing, some meals, and a $2,500 stipend for serving as a Summer Bridge Counselor.


Please complete the application below to be considered for the position. All applications and reference evaluations are due by 5PM CST on February 7
