# Expertise
The IGBB is noted for scientific expertise in...
**[Genomics (including Transcriptomics)](https://www.igbb.msstate.edu/genomics.php)** - Nucleic acid isolation and purification, molecular biology, molecular cloning, DNA sequencing, qRT-PCR, & RNASeq
**[Biocomputing (Bioinformatics& Computational Biology)](https://www.igbb.msstate.edu/biocomp.php)** - Analysis of large molecular datasets, development of new and/or improved computational tools, database creation and maintenance, integration of diverse data types, data mining, and the use of high performance computing to expedite knowledge discovery
**[Proteomics& Metabolomics](https://www.igbb.msstate.edu/proteomics.php)** - Protein isolation and purification, HPLC, gel-based and liquid-based mass spectrometry, metabolome profiling, and proteome analyses
