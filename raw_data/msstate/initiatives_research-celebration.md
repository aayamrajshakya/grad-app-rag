## 2025 Spring Research Celebration 
## The 2025 Spring Research Celebration focuses on innovation, discovery, and real-world impacts that make Mississippi State University the lead research institution in the state. 
Join us in celebrating Mississippi State University’s leadership in research, innovation, and discovery during the 2025 Spring Research Celebration, happening Monday, March 17, through Wednesday, May 14, 2025. 
The 2025 Spring Research Celebration highlights the diverse scholarly, collaborative, and creative activities taking place on our campus, while promoting the extensive resources available to support research at MSU. Don’t miss this opportunity to explore, engage, and be inspired by the groundbreaking work happening at State.
