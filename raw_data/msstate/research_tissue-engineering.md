# Tissue Engineering
The Tissue Engineering Research Center Conducts Research in various areas of cell/biomaterials interaction, mechanical and physiological testing of tissues including heart valves, tendon, and other organs. Models development and other testing and verification methodologies are among the most recent activities undertaking in the laboratories.
