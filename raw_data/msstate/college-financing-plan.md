#  College Financing Plan
##  College Financing Plan   

Mississippi State University, in collaboration with the U.S. Department of Education, offers the College Financing plan to help students and their families compare the cost of attendance (COA) and their financial aid awards. 
* * *
##  Accessing Your College Financing Plan   

You can view your personalized College Financing Plan by following the steps below:
  * Log into your [MyState](https://my.msstate.edu/helpfullinks) account using your NetID and NetPassword
  * Under Apps & Services, click Banner
  * Click Student
  * Under the Financial Aid and Scholarships column, click 'College Financing Plan'


* * *
