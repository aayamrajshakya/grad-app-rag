# Ph.D. in Instructional Systems & Workforce Development
##  Program Overview   

The Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Instructional Systems and Workforce Development (ISWD) is an online individualized degree program that builds on your existing work and educational experience and can be tailored to your unique career goals.
Graduates pursue careers as faculty, researchers, administrators, evaluators, directors, high-level managers, or consultants. ISWD students are encouraged to engage in scholarly research, publish in referred journals, and present at national and international conferences.
Our degree is completely online.
##  Application Deadlines   

Please refer to the [Graduate School website](https://www.grad.msstate.edu/students/admissions/deadlines) for deadlines.
##  Who Should Apply   

The Ph.D. degree is for you if you enjoy:
  * writing at a professional level
  * researching and writing about teaching and learning with technology
  * analyzing complex problems and proposing their solutions
  * infusing technology into teaching and learning activities
  * presenting new ideas to others
  * exploring emerging technologies and learning methods
