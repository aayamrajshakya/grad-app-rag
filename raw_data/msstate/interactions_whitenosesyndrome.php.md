### Interesting Facts
  * WNS is caused by a recently discovered non-native fungus called Geomyces destructans which loves cold weather
  * Bats save us billions of dollars every year in agriculture by helping us mitigate expenditures on pest control
  * They are economically and ecologically vital! By regulating insect populations every night (eating up to half their body weight each night) they benefit humans, forest, other animals, and our crops
