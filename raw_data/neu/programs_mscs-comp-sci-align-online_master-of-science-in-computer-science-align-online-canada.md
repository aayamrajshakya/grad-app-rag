Program overview 
##  Launch your tech career 
You don’t need a background in tech to have a future in it. Northeastern University’s Align Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) Online—Canada helps you translate your prior degree into a CS career. Start with online graduate-level bridge courses for beginners, combined with two short, in-person residencies on Northeastern’s Vancouver campus. You’ll build community, get hands-on support, and apply your skills to real projects.
Continue online or transfer to campus for master’s-level courses—then add an optional paid co-op or internship (remote or in person). You’re admitted to the master’s program with no reapplication after completing the bridge. Keep your life on track while preparing for in-demand roles across Canada in the AI, cloud, gaming, clean tech, and visual effects.

Flexible online program 
The Align MS in Computer Science Online—Canada offers asynchronous online courses with optional live sessions to help you balance your studies with work and family life. Study full-time to complete the program in as quickly as 2.5 years, or study part-time for added flexibility. You’re admitted to the master’s program from day one, after completing your bridge courses. You can continue online for your remaining master’s courses or transition to on-campus study in Vancouver.
Vancouver residencies 
Meet your cohort, connect with faculty, and practice your skills in a campus setting during two, brief on-campus residencies. Your experience includes community-building, along with a deep dive into advanced tools and applied real-world industry projects. Participating in residencies helps you focus on learning and building your network. Travel, room, and board for your residency are included in tuition.
Optional co-op or internship 
Northeastern leads in providing students with co-ops and internships that enhance learning.
You can opt for a full-time, four- to eight-month co-op or internship, either remote or on-site. We partner with 800-plus organizations across North America, including top Canadian employers in Vancouver’s AI, gaming, VFX, cloud, and clean-tech ecosystems. That’s why we’re ranked #1 in co-ops and internships by U.S. News & World Report

Admissions dates 
Applicants must submit the online application and all required admission materials no later than the stated deadlines to be considered for admission. Admissions decisions are made on a rolling basis.
Spring Term
Apply by December 1 for a January start.
  

Admissions requirements 
  * PDF or scanned copies of unofficial undergraduate transcripts; you can submit official transcripts from colleges/universities attended at the time of admission
  * Two out of three essay prompts
  * Statement of purpose that should include career goals and expected outcomes and benefits from the program
  * Recent professional resumé listing detailed position responsibilities
  * Three letters of recommendation
  * General GPA minimums: 3.0 on a 4.0 scale, 8.0 on a 10.0 scale, or 80 on a 100 scale
  * Recommended TOEFL (90 minimum) or IELTS (6.5 minimum) examination scores (international students only)
  * GRE Optional


Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
The Align program equips you with the technical skills and experience to design software, think algorithmically, collaborate on teams, and stay ahead in emerging fields.
Design and build software 
Gain proficiency in software design, implementation, and testing—using modern tools, practices, and documentation to deliver reliable, maintainable code.
Apply CS theory to practice 
Use algorithms, data structures, and core CS principles to solve problems from diverse application areas—across industry and research settings.
Work in agile, high-impact teams 
Collaborate effectively in small teams. Communicate clearly, review code, manage scope, and ship features that meet real user needs.
Keep pace with what’s next 
Build the meta-skills to learn new domains—from AI and machine learning to cloud and cybersecurity—so you can grow with the field and lead in the workplace.
