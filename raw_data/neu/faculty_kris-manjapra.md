#  KrisManjapra
Stearns Trustee Professor of History and Global Studies 
Kris Manjapra works at the intersection of global history and the critical study of race and colonialism. His research connects the Caribbean and Indian Ocean worlds. His books include a comparative study of global emancipation processes and the implications for reparations movements today: Black Ghost of Empire: The Long Death of Slavery and the Failure of EmancipationColonialism in Global PerspectiveAge of Entanglement: German and Indian Intellectual across Empire**[Black History in Action](https://www.bhacambridge.org/)** , dedicated to the restoration and reactivation of a Black cultural heritage center in Cambridge, MA. Professor Manjapra directs the **[Arts& Humanities Social Action Lab](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/ahalab/)** at Northeastern University.
