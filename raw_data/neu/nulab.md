# The NULab for Digital Humanities and Computational Social Science
[Welcome](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/about/)
## Mission
The NULab supports [research projects](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/research/projects) and scholarly exchanges through [grants](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/research/seedling-grants/) and other programs; convenes a group of [core faculty](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/community/faculty-and-staff/) who teach a wide range of [courses](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/teaching/courses/) and [publish](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/research/publications/) broadly in the digital realm; trains graduate students in DH and CSS skills through our [Graduate Certificate in Digital Humanities](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/dh-certificate/) and [NULab Fellows](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/community/fellows/) programs; sponsors [talks and symposia](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/events); offers support to [visiting scholars](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/research/visiting-scholars/); helps to bring digital and computational methods into a broad range of classes through our [Digital Integration Teaching Initiative](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/diti/); and provides fora for discussions of technology, teaching, and research at Northeastern. The NULab also works closely with Northeastern’s [Digital Scholarship Group](http://dsg.neu.edu/) and the [Centers for Digital Scholarship](https://cds.library.northeastern.edu/) to host workshops and events, support project teams, and foster a collaborative community of digital scholars.
To learn more about the research projects supported by the NULab, go [here](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/research/projects/); to see a list of our upcoming events, go [here](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/events/); to see the NULab in the news, go [here](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/category/news/).
