## Electronic Contract and Legal Agreement Workflow System (eCLAWs)
eCLAWs is a simple contract and agreement workflow system that asks questions to help you provide the right information needed by your Reviewer(s) and Signatories in order to process your contract as quickly as possible.
If your research involves Human Subjects, review the university [Policy on Human Subject Research](https://policies.northeastern.edu/policy505/).
To get started, review the eCLAWs Research and Innovation Purpose Step by Step [instructions;](https://res.research.northeastern.edu/quick-cards-and-guides/) Review the training information [here](https://res.research.northeastern.edu/ecd-eclaws-epaws/training/). [Login](https://epaws.northeastern.edu/?redirect=%2Feclaws#_ga=2.49121647.854118474.1568638874-306768007.1564495103) with your myNEU username and password when ready to submit.
