The Online MS in Management program from Northeastern's D'Amore-McKim School of Business is a highly specialized program designed for individuals seeking to take on leadership roles in the healthcare industry without any clinician experience required.
Created in collaboration with the Mayo Clinic College of Medicine and Science, the program offers two concentrations: healthcare administration and digital transformation in healthcare.
With a focus on management, innovation, and strategy, the program is perfect for those looking to switch to the healthcare industry or upskill their careers in healthcare.
## Choose your concentration
##### Digital Tranformation in Healthcare
If you want to utilize cutting-edge techniques and lead healthcare into the digital revolution, the Digital Transformation in Healthcare concentration will equip you with the knowledge to lead digital initiatives that create value for patients and stakeholders. 
[Learn more about the Digital Transformation in Healthcare concentration.](https://online.northeastern.edu/business/onlinemsm/digital-transformation/)
##### Healthcare Administration
If you’re looking to transition into the growing healthcare industry or add business acumen to your existing healthcare experience, the Healthcare Administration concentration offers a comprehensive overview of the industry, including healthcare policy, management, finance, and quality improvement. 
[Learn more about the Healthcare Administration concentration.](https://online.northeastern.edu/business/onlinemsm/healthcare-administration/)
## Learn from star faculty and industry experts
You’ll be taught by world-leading experts from the healthcare industry. You’ll learn from a team of star faculty from Northeastern’s D’Amore-McKim School of Business and Mayo Clinic College of Medicine and Science, plus top executives from Mayo Clinic. You’ll also do case studies drawn from the real world of Mayo Clinic.

With a focus on experiential learning, you will have the opportunity to participate in real-world healthcare projects, giving you practical experience in the field. Additionally, each course gives you access to top executives from the Mayo Clinic, who will provide insights into the healthcare industry and share their expertise.
