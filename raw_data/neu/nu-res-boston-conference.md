Northeastern University Research Enterprise Services (NU-RES) Conference Boston June 8-9, 2022
The inaugural NU-RES Boston Conference was a great success! We brought together in one place all NU-RES team members, including those based in remote locations, as well as our College Grant Administrator colleagues and Faculty. The conference was designed to strengthen our working relationships across the university, look for ways to reduce Faculty burden and improve processes, as well as giving us an opportunity to design and deliver valuable professional development sessions for NU-RES and college research administrators, and to participate in some in-person team building activities. Its overall goal was improving research administration skills, abilities, and relationships as part of our ongoing effort to improve the support of our faculty, and the university’s research endeavors.
We came away with learned experiences, strengthened relationships, identified opportunities for improvements, and a renewed energy on working together to reduce administrative burden and work toward the president’s ambitious research funding goals!
The conference included all those in the Northeastern research community including, but not limited to:
  * Research Administrators
  * Principal Investigators and Professors
  * Researchers and Scientists
  * Postdocs and New Researchers


  * Lab Managers
  * Department Business Managers
  * ITS, Research Computing, Libraries
  * Research Development and Advancement
