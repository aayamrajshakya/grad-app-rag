Program overview 
##  The future of chemistry 
Northeastern’s Master of Science in Chemistry program offers a flexible and rigorous curriculum designed for both working professionals and full-time students. The program is taught by accomplished and research-active faculty—delivering a strong foundation in fundamental and applied chemistry. You can customize your learning experience by selecting from concentrations or choosing an elective path. With the added benefit of optional co-op opportunities, you’ll gain valuable real-world experience that enhances your academic and professional growth.

  * Join a vibrant scientific community in Boston—a world-class research center.

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
Students attain expertise in chemistry and the skills necessary to become industry leaders.
Develop expertise 
The program provides practicing chemical professionals with richer understanding of the field. Students graduate prepared for broader leadership opportunities in the field.
Refine your expertise 
Students have the option to select a concentration in analytical, inorganic, organic, or physical chemistry, and in interdisciplinary fields such as polymers, materials, and bio-related chemistry.
