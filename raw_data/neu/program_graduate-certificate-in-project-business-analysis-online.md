#### Program Overview
Through a targeted curriculum, you will gain the knowledge, in-demand skills, tools, and techniques needed to effectively create and manage requirements that align with stakeholder and organizational needs.
#### Program Objectives
  * Develop a strong foundation in business analysis and its role within project management.
  * Understand and analyze the voice of the customer to explore potential solutions for their needs.
  * Apply tools and techniques to elicit and define business and stakeholder requirements.
  * Assess current business processes to identify inefficiencies and areas for improvement.
  * Apply analytical skills and a data-driven approach to the business analysis process.
  * Design future-state processes by translating business needs into clear, concise, and high-quality requirements (including solution, functional, and non-functional requirements) and defining a structured approach to implementation.
  * Develop a personal leadership strategy to engage multiple stakeholders and customers effectively, ensuring success in implementing change or improvement initiatives as a business analyst.
