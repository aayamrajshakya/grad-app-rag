## Build artistic and creative skills for the dynamic gaming industry
Advancing technology and ongoing innovation mean the gaming industry is always growing and changing. From social and mobile games to augmented and virtual reality to the gamification of health, education, and science, there are endless opportunities for professionals who have experience designing engaging games. 
Our innovative MBA x Game Design program—offered in collaboration with [Northeastern's College of Arts, Media and Design](https://camd.northeastern.edu/) (CAMD)—will equip you with a foundation in the best practices and theories of game design. Through courses taught by CAMD faculty and drawing from disciplines like psychology, media studies, systems design, art, architecture, and software development, you'll learn about the creative and artistic processes involved in game design. 
You don't need a background in game design. This concentration is open to anyone interested in designing engaging games that people love.
