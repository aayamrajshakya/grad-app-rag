#  Visa Applications 
Applying for a visa requires having the right forms and documents. With the help of the Office of Global Services, F-1 and J-1 Exchange Visitors get the guidance they need to complete it—from start to finish. 
##  I want to know about the visa process for: 
[ ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/f-1-visa-process/)
###  [ F-1 Students ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/f-1-visa-process/)
To provide time for visa processing, students are encouraged to apply for their visa as soon as they receive their I-20 issued by Northeastern University. U.S. Embassies or U.S. Consulates are able to issue F-1 entry visas up to 120 days before the program start date listed on your I-20.
[ Learn More ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/f-1-visa-process/)
[ ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-visa-process/)
###  [ J-1 Students ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-visa-process/)
Are you an international student participating in an exchange program with a government, organizational, or university sponsorship? If so, you will need to travel with a J-1 student visa.
[ Learn More ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-visa-process/)
[ ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-intern-visa-process/)
###  [ J-1 Student Interns ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-intern-visa-process/)
J-1 student interns are currently enrolled and pursuing a degree in accredited post-secondary academic institutions outside of the U.S. Whether you intern at a Fortune 500 or a start-up company, it is important for all J-1 student interns to understand how to maintain their status apply for a visa.
[ Learn More ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/new-students/visa-application/j-1-student-intern-visa-process/)
##  Need to pay the SEVIS fee? 
[The I-901 Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) fee](https://www.fmjfee.com/i901fee/index.html) supports the Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP) and the automated system that keeps track of students and exchange visitors-ensuring they maintain their status while in the U.S. [Click here for additional information. ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/getting-started/paying-sevis-fee/)
