##  Excel in a data-powered world 
Harness data to drive business strategy with a STEM-designated, AI-powered curriculum that blends machine learning, advanced analytics, and real-world applications. Gain hands-on experience through industry partnerships and experiential learning, mastering the tools to extract insights and optimize decision-making. The MS in Business Analytics program equips you with the in-demand skills and practical expertise to lead in the data-driven economy.

Accreditations 
Accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business International—the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide.
Experience-powered learning 
[Capstone project:](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/capstone-project/) Tackle real business problems using data-driven solutions for an employer partner during your capstone course. By applying what you have learned, you’ll see how analytics can shape strategic decision-making. Required.
[Graduate co-op:](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/graduate-career-center/students/experience-fueled-learning/graduate-co-op/) Dive into real-world projects at top companies or startups. Apply to work full time during the summer to leverage your data analytics and strategy skills to tackle specific projects, immerse yourself in company culture, and contribute to corporate goals. Optional.
[Community Impact Initiative:](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/graduate-career-center/students/experience-fueled-learning/community-impact-initiatives/) Use your expertise to make a difference at a nonprofit. Apply to join a team focused on strategic projects; collaborate with senior leadership, project managers, and peers; and help the organization achieve its mission. Optional.

Other opportunities 
Gain real-world experience and sharpen your financial skills through hands-on opportunities like managing a mutual fund with the [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/) or earning [Bloomberg](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/bloomberg-business-lab/) financial certifications. Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * An undergraduate-level, full-semester course in Statistics
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your credits toward an MBA 
If you think an MBA might be in your future, good news: your MS in Business Analytics degree can get you halfway there.
Once your degree is complete and you’ve maintained at least a 3.0 GPA and a minimum grade in each class, you may be able to apply your 30 credits toward an eligible MBA program at Northeastern for up to three years following completion of your degree.[Double Husky scholarship](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/admissions-aid/tuition-financial-aid/scholarships/double-husky-scholarship/), which reduces your tuition by 25 percent. _(Note that your existing master’s degree is not a guarantee of acceptance to an MBA program.)_
The eligible master’s program for this program includes:
  * [Online MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-online/online-mba/)


Contact an enrollment counselor to learn more about eligibility requirements.
