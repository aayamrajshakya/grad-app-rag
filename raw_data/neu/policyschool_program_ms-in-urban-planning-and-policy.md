#  Master of ScienceUrban Planning and Policy
## Find Place-Based Solutions. Transform Communities.
Lead transformative change where it matters most: in your neighborhood, school, workplace, city, region, or beyond. The MS Urban Planning and Policy will help you address urgent environmental, social, and political challenges using place-based solutions.
# Choose Your Area of Impact 
Plan the change you want to see in the world. The MS Urban Planning and Policy is a collaborative program between Northeastern’s School of Public Policy and Urban Affairs and School of Architecture, offering you a choice of four concentrations:
  * Urban Sustainability and Resilience:
  * Urban Analytics:
  * Urban Development Policy and Planning:
  * Urban Design and Physical Planning: 


[Focus Areas + Curriculum](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/policyschool/program/ms-in-urban-planning-and-policy/curriculum/)
# Build Experience Improving Urban Spaces
The MS Urban Planning and Policy is founded on analysis and action. You’ll learn through real projects and research so that you’re ready to step into a professional or leadership role as soon as you graduate. In this program, you can:
  1. Hold an optional, paid co-op or internship within Northeastern’s #1 ranked offerings 
  2. Conduct impactful research [the Dukakis Center for Urban and Regional Policy](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/dukakiscenter/), [the Boston Area Research Initiativ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/bari/)e, [the Global Resilience Institute](https://globalresilience.northeastern.edu/), and [the Social Impact Lab](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/impactlab/).
  3. Go on an optional exchange experience 
  4. Support a local, community-led initiative to help a public service or nonprofit 


[See Co-op + Experience](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/policyschool/program/ms-in-urban-planning-and-policy/experiential-opportunities/)

# Learn from Expert Faculty with Diverse Expertise 
Your faculty should be as diverse as the challenges you’re tackling. Ours are. Faculty have led the Anti-Displacement Studio to engage communities in urban planning and design, contributed to Boston’s desegregation discussions, led equitable development projects, worked as architects, and provided testimony to Congress on law and social policy. Their work influences climate progress, public health, sustainable development, and more.
[Meet Our Faculty](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/policyschool/program/ms-in-urban-planning-and-policy/urban-planning-policy-faculty/)
# Choose a Flexible, High Value Program
Increase your earning potential and career fulfillment in just 2 years. You can attend this flexible MS Urban Planning and Policy program full time or part time—or fit it into a workday, 
Funding opportunities include:
  * Paid experience while you earn your degree with a 6-month full-time co-op
  * Part-time or full-time internships during the summer or regular semester
  * Automatic consideration for scholarships up to 50% off tuition
  * Additional opportunities for Northeastern alumni or current Northeastern undergraduate students
