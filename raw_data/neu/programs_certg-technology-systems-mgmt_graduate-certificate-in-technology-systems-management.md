Program overview 
##  Lead with strategic technology 
Northeastern University’s Graduate Certificate in Technology Systems Management bridges the gap between technology, engineering, and business. This four-course program is designed for non-engineers seeking to develop management skills in a tech-driven environment.
You’ll explore best practices in managing projects, human resources, and technology development, all while making data-driven, financial, and economic-based decisions.
Whether you’re looking to advance in your current role or pivot your career, this program offers a blend of technological and managerial knowledge.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Advanced and Intelligent Manufacturing](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-advanced-intelligent-mfg/master-of-science-in-advanced-and-intelligent-manufacturing/)
  * [Master of Science in Data Analytics Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-data-analytics-engineering/master-of-science-in-data-analytics-engineering/)
  * [Master of Science in Human Factors](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-human-factors/master-of-science-in-human-factors/)
  * [Master of Science in Industrial Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msie-industrial-engineering/master-of-science-in-industrial-engineering/)
