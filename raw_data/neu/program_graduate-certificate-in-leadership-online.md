Today's cross-functional teams and organizations require a leadership style that capitalizes on the collective expertise and capabilities of the group.
Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Leadership starts with the premise that everyone can lead . The program studies every aspect of leadership dynamics - from the leader as an individual, to working in teams, from the organization itself to the development of strategic leadership capabilities.
Through rigorous targeted coursework, you will gain a working knowledge on a series of alternative perspectives of leadership - including an in-depth focus on collaborative models.
Using the course's action-learning methods, students build a personal model of leadership that can be put to immediate use in the workplace.
### Program objectives
  * Build a personal model of leadership that can be put to immediate use in the workplace
  * Exposure to a series of alternative perspectives of leadership, including collaborative models.
  * Expand leadership experience through exposure to relevant and up-to-date leadership strategies.
  * Development and mastery of collaborative leadership skills


### Unique Features
  1. Boston campus and online format allow maximum flexibility for working professionals
  2. Credits earned in this certificate program can be applied to Northeastern's MS in Corporate and Organizational Communication, MS in Organizational Leadership, and MS in Project Management
