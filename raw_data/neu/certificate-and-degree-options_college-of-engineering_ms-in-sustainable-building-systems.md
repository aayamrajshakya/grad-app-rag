## Total Semester Hours Required: 36
The Civil and Environmental Engineering Department encourages students pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Engineering Leadership paired with an MS in Sustainable Building Systems to complete their MS coursework requirements in their first year and their GIEL certificate requirements in their second year. Students who prefer to complete their GIEL certificate requirements in their first year are asked to speak with their MS degree adviser beforehand.
Guidance for students pursuing the MS in Sustainable Building Systems with Graduate Certificate in Engineering Leadership.
Please refer to the Sustainable Building Systems homepage for the most up-to-date course requirements and other program information.

## Sustainable Building Systems Coursework
All students will be required to take 3 [Sustainable Building Systems](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/civil-environmental/sustainable-building-systems-mssbs/#programrequirementstext) core courses (12 semester-hours) and 2 elective courses (8 semester-hours).
