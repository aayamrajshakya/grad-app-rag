Program overview 
##  Learn to leverage AI 
Students from a broad spectrum of academic and professional backgrounds will gain a comprehensive understanding of AI fundamentals and learn effective ways to apply AI applications to their own contexts and products.
By the end of the program, students will gain skills in leveraging AI technologies responsibly, ethically, and effectively within their organizations. They will be equipped to advocate for the responsible use of AI and mitigate risks associated with AI deployment while also understanding the human-centered aspects of AI development and implementation.
