## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
Please see [Cross-Disciplinary Science PhD](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/cross-disciplinary-science-phd/) for further information on PhD programs available. 
Students who ​do not qualify for the PhD Biophysics degree or the PhD Human Behavior and Sustainability Sciences degree, but who have completed ​required coursework with a cumulative GPA of 3.000 or better, may be eligible to receive ​a terminal MS Cross-Disciplinary Science degree. Note that no students will be admitted directly into this degree program to pursue a masterʼs degree.
## Concentration
Complete one of the following concentrations:
  * [Biophysics](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/interdisciplinary/cross-disciplinary-science-ms-bos/index.html#ms-cdsc-bphy)
  * [Human Behavior and Sustainability Sciences](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/interdisciplinary/cross-disciplinary-science-ms-bos/index.html#ms-cdsc-hbss)


### Program Credit/GPA Requirements
31 total semester hours required   
Minimum 3.000 GPA required
