OUR UNIQUE APPROACH
## Deepen your exploration through experience
All our PhD programs offer unique opportunities to take time outside your research group to work in industry, government, or academia. These experiences help shape your own research, deepen your exploration, and test ideas in the real world. You’ll also expand your career network.
### Industry PhD
Through this first-of-its-kind program co-developed by Northeastern and employers, pursue your PhD as you continue to work full time, while gaining deep expertise, cutting-edge skills, and mentorship.
### LEADERs
In this certificate program, you’ll earn placements at employer partner sites, where you’ll gain the cultural agility, collaboration, and communication skills to be effective researchers and leaders.
### Experiential PhD Fellowship
With the sponsorship of partner organizations, conduct research and get training in an area of your interest alongside experienced professionals across industries and sectors.
### Experiential Postdoc
With the sponsorship of partner organizations, conduct research and get training in an area of your interest alongside experienced professionals across industries and sectors.
### Global PhD
Split your time between multiple campuses in Northeastern’s global system, or between Northeastern and a partnering international university, and receive a dual doctoral degree.
