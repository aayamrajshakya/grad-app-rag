# PhD Programs at Khoury College
Innovate and lead as you pursue your PhD 
Khoury College of Computer Sciences — [ranked #12 by CSRankings](https://csrankings.org/#/index?all&us) — provides you with a variety of options to pursue a PhD, collaborating with top researchers in the field at one of the country’s leading technology hubs. Plus, our emphasis on interdisciplinary education across multiple Northeastern colleges provides opportunities not available anywhere else. Khoury College PhD programs include:
  * [PhD in Computer Science](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/computer-science-phd/)
  * [PhD in Cybersecurity](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/cybersecurity-phd/)
  * [PhD in Network Science](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/network-science-phd/)
  * [PhD in Personal Health Informatics](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/personal-health-informatics-phd/)

## Collaborate with top researchers in state-of-the art facilities
Khoury College research is pushing the boundaries of computer technology, applying research discoveries to real-world challenges. For example, our faculty are conducting cutting-edge research in areas like AI-powered disease diagnosis, developing secure network systems for the internet of things, and exploring the mathematical foundations of computer science — tackling the profound question of computational limits on what computers can do.
Researchers at Khoury College make transformative contributions to many fields within computer science, collaborating, publishing, leading national and international projects, and participating in top conferences. There is a strong emphasis on real-world applications of CS at Khoury College — combined with an ethos of interdisciplinary work both within the college and beyond — that puts you at the center of [collaborative research that makes a difference.](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/research/)
## Career prospects across industry and academia
Our computer science PhD graduates have found prestigious positions in both industry and academia:
  * Tenure-track faculty (e.g., Michigan, Indiana, UC Irvine, UMass Amherst)
  * Postdoc research scientists (Harvard, UCLA, University of Paris)
  * Senior software engineers and industry leaders (Akamai, Google, Microsoft, Mozilla)
