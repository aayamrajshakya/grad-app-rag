## Lead financial strategy in fast-moving business environments 
In today's global and digital business world, companies need leaders who understand global markets and the impact of technological innovation on financial outcomes. Your core coursework covers financial management essentials like budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis so you can identify opportunities and develop strategies that align with your organization's goals. 
Our Corporate Finance concentration equips you with essential skills in capital allocation, equity and fixed-income markets, and financial asset valuation. You'll master stock and bond valuation, understand macroeconomic impacts, and learn advanced techniques for analyzing financial assets. 
Dive into practical applications with cash-flow models and modern finance theory to make strategic capital decisions. Personalize your learning with electives like mergers and acquisitions, business turnarounds, or real estate investing. Explore entrepreneurial finance, including venture capital and startup valuation, to broaden your expertise.

## 360 Huntington Fund
### Gain hands-on financial experience
At D'Amore-McKim, you can apply to join the management team of the 360 Huntington Fund— a real mutual fund that is part of Northeastern's endowment. You can join as an analyst, where you'll research, analyze, and present investment choices to your peers, or apply for a manager role, where you'll handle mutual fund management tasks like preparing accounting statements, creating asset allocation plans, and monitoring compliance. No matter what your experience level with managing stocks, you'll learn invaluable skills in an environment that no simulated fund exercise can match. 
[Learn more about the fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/360-huntington-fund/)
