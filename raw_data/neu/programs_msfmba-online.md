Program overview 
##  Become a natural leader 
Benefit from this unique opportunity to earn a combined degree from anywhere in the world and become a leader at the intersection of business and finance. Our online MS in Finance/MBA program is 100% online, with four finance courses added to the online MBA program. This program includes experience-powered learning opportunities, which is the hallmark of a Northeastern education. You’ll be embedded in project-based work where you can apply your knowledge right away at your current position or as you pursue a new role. Join the next generation of business leaders who are equipped to drive change in the digital era.

Unique features 
  * Complete the online MBA’s required core and elective courses
  * Earn an MS in Finance by completing an additional four courses
  * Select one of two finance tracks—Corporate or Investment Finance
  * Complete your coursework 100% online


Gain real-world experience 
Sharpen the skills you learn in the classroom with real-world experiences, including:
  * In-Class Case Studies—Professors integrate case studies and exercises into the classroom to bring a real-world perspective and relevance to their teaching.
  * Dual Degree Option—Students can consider our dual degree option–online Master of Science in Finance/MBA–to deepen business knowledge and finance expertise.
  * Optional Residencies—Students can participate in their program’s optional domestic and international residency courses.

Admissions requirements 
  * Application form
  * Application fee ($100)
  * A personal statement (400-800 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores (for more information, review our [admissions policies](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/))
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (for international students only, view more information and waiver eligibility in our [admissions policies](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/))
