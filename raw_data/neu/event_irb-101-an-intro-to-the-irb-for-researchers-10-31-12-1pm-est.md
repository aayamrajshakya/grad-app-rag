IRB 101 is designed for researchers new to human subjects research or for seasoned researchers new to NU. The presentation will include:
  * An introduction to our office and its functions
  * A brief history of human subjects research violations and the development of IRBs
  * When IRB review is and is not required
  * The fundamentals of IRB review, including the ethical and regulatory frameworks we use
  * Our submission, review, and approval processes


The presentation will take roughly 30-40 minutes with plenty of time for audience questions. Participants are eligible to earn 1 hour of RCR credit to satisfy [NU-RES requirements](https://nu-res.compliance.northeastern.edu/responsible-conduct-of-research-program/).
