## Biography
Chris Bolick, Associate Dean Graduate Programs 
Academic Background and Areas of Research   
With EdD, Organizational Leadership Studies, Northeastern University , Chris Bolick’s research focuses on project management and process improvement.
Professional Background
Before joining Northeastern, Chris worked in the retail, construction, and manufacturing industries, serving in management, operations, procurement, process improvement, and project management roles. 
Lowes Companies Inc., 2005-2017
Action Sign Company, 2000-2005
