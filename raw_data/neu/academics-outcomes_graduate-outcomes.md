##  Accelerate your impact 
As a student or alum, you’ll have access to collaborative organizations designed to help you accelerate your career and achieve your professional goals.
[The Center for Research Innovation (CRI)](https://cri.northeastern.edu/)
The CRI serves as the nexus of inspiration and invention, empowering Northeastern innovators and entrepreneurs to transform their boldest ideas into societal impact. Explore how the CRI translates the university’s most dynamic research into real-world results.
[Northeastern's Innovation Ecosystem](https://alumni.northeastern.edu/opportunities/entrepreneurship/)
Northeastern is home to 26 entrepreneurship programs to empower our diverse community of innovators. Whether you’re planning a new idea, looking to accelerate the growth of an existing venture, or seeking expertise from thought leaders and idea generators, you can rely on our robust innovation ecosystem to help you achieve what’s next.
[The McCarthy(s) Venture Mentoring Network (VNM)](https://vmn.sites.northeastern.edu/)
This university-wide resource is available to all Northeastern students, faculty, and alumni ventures. The goal of the VMN is to pair Northeastern entrepreneurs with high-quality, experienced mentors who can help address business challenges and make our ventures more successful.
[Women Who Empower](https://womenwhoempower.advancement.northeastern.edu/)
Women Who Empower aims to inspire and enable future generations to be creative, innovative, and agile as they lead change and pursue impact in the fields of their choice. They advance this mission through entrepreneurship initiatives, financial support, networking events, mentorship, and more.
