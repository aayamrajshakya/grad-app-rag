## Mechanical Engineering Coursework
Students pursuing a concentration in [General Mechanical Engineering](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/mechanical-industrial/mechanical-engineering-concentration-general-msme/#programrequirementstext), [Mechanics and Design](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/mechanical-industrial/mechanical-engineering-concentration-mechanics-design-msme/#programrequirementstext) or [Material Science](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/mechanical-industrial/mechanical-engineering-concentration-material-science-msme/#programrequirementstext) will complete 4 courses (16 semester-hours) of concentration-specific core coursework.
Students pursuing a concentration in [Mechatronics](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/mechanical-industrial/mechanical-engineering-concentration-mechatronics-msme/#programrequirementstext) or [Thermofluids](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/mechanical-industrial/mechanical-engineering-concentration-thermofluids-msme/#programrequirementstext) will complete 5 courses (20 semester-hours) of concentration-specific core coursework.
