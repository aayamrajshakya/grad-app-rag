When Northeastern graduate Camille Martin and associate professor [Leila Deravi](https://cos.northeastern.edu/people/leila-deravi/) co-founded Seaspire, a skincare ingredients company inspired by pigment in octopus and squid, their goal was to create a product that is good for your skin and the environment.
New research shows that they are on the right track.
