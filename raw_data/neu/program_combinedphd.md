Northeastern’s Connected Science Community PhD programs provide students with advanced departmental curriculum, ground-breaking research opportunities, and career and core skills development – all within a supportive network of peers and mentors.
Whether you have realized your post-graduate path or are just beginning to explore one, the PhD programs in the College of Science will prepare you for extraordinary career opportunities.
