#  Graduate Certificate inDigital Humanities
The digital “Atlas of Southern Memory” created by Caroline Klibanoff for the DH certificate in 2018.
## Current requirements: 12 semester hours
Current Northeastern MA and PhD students can signal their intention to pursue the Certificate by enrolling in the NULab Project Seminar (INSH 7910). Students who wish to pursue the standalone Certificate option will find information on how to apply [here](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/dh-certificate/apply/).
