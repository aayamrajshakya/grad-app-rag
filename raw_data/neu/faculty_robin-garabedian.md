#  RobinGarabedian
Visiting Teaching Professor in English 
Robin earned her PhD in Composition & Rhetoric at the University of Massachusetts – Amherst, and teaches courses on scientific writing and technical writing. Her research considers how the neoliberal age requires updated understandings of how writing functions in the lives of our students.
