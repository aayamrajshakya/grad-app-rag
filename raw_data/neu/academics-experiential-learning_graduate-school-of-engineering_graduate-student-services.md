#  Graduate Student Services 
##  Overview 
Graduate Student Services is an academic resource in the College of Engineering that, in conjunction with university faculty and staff, helps with the following concerns:
  * Course Registration
  * Graduation clearance
  * Academic probation
  * Academic review of Office of Global Studies (OGS) forms
  * Administration of funding


Graduate Student Services recognizes the critical role our advising team plays in the transitional process students go through at Northeastern University and we are committed to meeting a diverse set of needs to assist in students’ success and achievements.
We have a variety of advisors to help make your graduate career a success, so use the table below to determine which one will help you the best.
### Introducing the New COE Advising Bot!
Get instant answers to common academic questions about policies, procedures, and resources. Perfect for quick information when you need it, wherever you are in the world.
On the [COE Advising Bot](https://coehusky.vercel.app/) homepage, look for the chat icon in the bottom right corner of the screen, select your student level (undergrad or grad), and start exploring!
