Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Higher Education Administration is designed to empower you with an overall understanding of managerial concepts as well as the operational and strategic concepts that lead to effective academic administration.
This program is best suited for individuals seeking mid- to senior-level administrative roles and individuals interested in transitioning from other fields into academia.
Through the Northeastern Graduate School of Education’s commitment to practitioner preparation and experiential learning, this certificate program brings core theoretical knowledge to current challenges, preparing students to navigate the fast-changing world of higher education.
With an emphasis on social justice and inclusion, we’ll help you identify and explore local, national, and global impacts on higher education policy and practice while you develop the tools to succeed through practical, experience-based learning.
The certificate program also allows you to connect with diverse professionals around the world, so you can expand your network as you collaborate to solve common challenges.
This graduate certificate program is flexible — 100% online—so it is ideal for working professionals.
### Program objectives
  * Analyze law and policy regulations within a higher education context
  * Analyze, generate, and propose innovative solutions to contemporary issues in higher education, driven by data and applied theory
  * Integrate principles of social justice and inclusion for concrete actions in higher education
  * Articulate methods to continuously hone and upgrade professional skills in key higher education competencies
  * Effectively present ideas through multiple mediums for targeted audiences in higher education administration


### Unique Features
  1. Program suited for individuals seeking mid- to senior-level administrative roles and individuals interested in transitioning into academia
  2. Curriculum designed by senior leaders in higher education who provided input around in-demand skills they look for in new hires, providing our program industry validation and credibility
  3. Credits can be applied toward the Master of Education in Higher Education Administration
