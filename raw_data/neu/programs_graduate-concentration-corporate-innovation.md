## Become an innovative leader for growth and change 
As technology upends industries, companies must innovate or risk being left behind. There is an urgent need for leaders who embrace this challenge and know how to evaluate business practices, promote new ideas, and lead change. 
In the Corporate Innovation concentration, you'll study critical topics like design thinking, digital transformation, creative product development, emerging technologies, and corporate transformation with leading researchers and practitioners who are passionate about disrupting industries and creating new markets. By exploring case studies, you'll learn the principles of innovation from multiple perspectives, including product and service development, financing innovation, go-to-market strategies, lean development, managing high-performance teams, and more.
