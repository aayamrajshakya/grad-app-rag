## Concentration or Electives Option
A concentration is not required.Students may complete the electives option in lieu of a concentration.
  * [Cell and Molecular Biology](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/biology/biology-ms/index.html#Cell%20and%20Molecular%20Biology%20Concentration)
  * [Molecular Microbiology](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/biology/biology-ms/index.html#Molecular%20Microbiology%20Concentration)
  * [Electives Option](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/science/biology/biology-ms/index.html#Electives%20Option)


## Program Credit/GPA Requirements
30 total semester hours required (20 semester hours must be taken for a letter grade)
Minimum 3.000 GPA required
