## Developing globally minded leaders
When you combine Northeastern’s focus on research and experience-based learning with London’s large community of international professionals and businesses, you get a one-of-a-kind campus experience. Here, you’ll immerse yourself in the history and culture of an ancient city while learning to expand your skills and perspectives, allowing you to create global change in your industry.
##  What makes London unique 
Our campus in London offers quiet study spaces, tech-infused collaboration zones, and access to all the city has to offer.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://www.nulondon.ac.uk/)


Location, location 
Devon House, the centerpiece of our campus, sits on the doorstep of London’s bustling Central Business District and is just a few minutes away from iconic landmarks like the Tower of London and Tower Bridge.
Modern facilities 
Every campus building features the latest learning technologies, and students benefit from small class sizes and [high levels of support](https://www.nulondon.ac.uk/student-life/life-in-london/student-support/) throughout their time at Northeastern.
## Fast facts: London's job market
London's economy is powered by international businesses and scrappy startups alike, and Northeastern students can easily find their next career move here.

##  London Students' Union 
Our active [Students’ Union](https://www.nusu.org.uk/) serves as a voice for students and a home for events, student groups, and campus news. The union can help resolve issues and connect you with academic and mental health resources, among others.
##  London campus highlights 
Our London campus is the gateway to a vibrant city—and to a world of opportunity for students seeking to add an international slant to their careers.
  * [ Learn more about studying in London](https://www.nulondon.ac.uk/student-life/campus/)


Employer partners 
Partnerships with organisations of any size, from startups to multinational businesses, provide opportunities for networking, site visits, work experience, and other real-world learning projects.
World-class facilities 
Our campus spans 72,000 square feet of collaboration space, modern classrooms, and study and lounge areas in St Katharine Docks. Students can also access the British Library and Senate House Library.
Lively location 
Northeastern’s campus buildings overlook the River Thames in the thriving St Katharine Docks neighbourhood. Financial and business districts are just outside the campus as well.
