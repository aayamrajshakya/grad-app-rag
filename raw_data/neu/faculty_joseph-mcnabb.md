## Biography
Joe McNabb is a full-time faculty member in the Graduate School of Education. Prior to joining the faculty, he completed an 11-year term as president of Labour√© College. This position followed a 17-year tenure as professor and dean of faculty. He served as a commissioner on the New England Commission of Higher Education (NECHE). He chaired, or served as site visitor, for many accreditation teams throughout the New England region and Europe. He has also served on numerous boards, including teaching hospitals, community educational agencies, and several non-profit organizations.
As part of the founding group, he served on the College of Professional Studies-Faculty Academic Council for five years, ending his term in 2019. He serves on various committees within the Graduate School of Education and chairs the College of Professional Studies Faculty Fund Committee. Additionally, he has served on the Northeastern University Faculty Senate as senator, member of the Senate Agenda Committee, and member of the Financial Affairs Committee.  
  
His professional service includes his work as the federal case reporter for the Education Law UpdateImpacting Education-Journal of Transforming Professional PracticeJournal of First-Generation College Success
