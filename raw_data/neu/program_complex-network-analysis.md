#  Master of ScienceComplex Network Analysis
## Advance in a Networked World
Network science has revolutionized many fields, including public health, sociology, business, and finance. Northeastern University’s Master of Science in Complex Network Analysis gives students the practical skills and experience they need to analyze, draw meaning from, and act upon massive datasets in their chosen specialization.
You’ll build a strong foundation in complex network analysis, supplemented by domain-specific coursework. You can choose to study the structure and dynamic processes of epidemiology and public health, online social networks and interactions, or economic networks with one of three unique learning tracks. Each specialization is taught by expert faculty with real-world experience in each field.
## Program Objectives
  * Measure and collect data
  * Optimize and design networks
  * Gain insights of complex structures
  * Explain your findings
