## Developing talent in Arlington
Northeastern’s experience-focused approach to education is the perfect addition to the region’s tech, policy, and security sectors. Partnerships with nonprofits, startups, government agencies, and global enterprises allow students to expand their skills through co-ops and research projects with industry leaders. Northeastern’s network of experts fits seamlessly with Arlington’s thriving tech community, large federal workforce, and strong ties to veterans and the military.
##  What makes Arlington unique 
From startups to federal departments, Arlington’s mix of public and private sector organizations offers students a vast range of learning opportunities.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://arlington.northeastern.edu/)


An accessible location 
Our campus sits in the heart of Rosslyn, a vibrant and walkable neighborhood just two miles away from the Department of Defense headquarters and accessible to Washington, D.C., via public transit.
Impactful partnerships 
Partnerships with local nonprofits and with federal entities like the Office of Personnel Management and Naval Surface Warfare Center provide important networking opportunities for students.
Experience-based learning 
Students can take on co-ops, class projects, and research opportunities with some of the most recognizable employers in the public and private sectors, thanks to the region’s high concentration of federal agencies.
Federal and military support 
Northeastern offers scholarships and reduced tuition for federal employees, servicemembers, and veterans in select programs.
