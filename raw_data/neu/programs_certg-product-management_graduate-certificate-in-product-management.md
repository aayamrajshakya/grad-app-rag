Program overview 
##  Become a product leader 
Northeastern University’s Graduate Certificate in Product Management is designed for early to mid-career professionals seeking practical, industry-ready skills in a competitive and rapidly growing field. This interdisciplinary certificate combines foundational knowledge with hands-on experiential learning, equipping you to lead product initiatives from ideation to launch.
The program focuses on building data-driven decision-making and user-centric design skills with a curriculum that stands out by offering flexible yet targeted learning that aligns with your career goals. Earning this certificate uniquely positions you to excel in product management, advance your career, and succeed in today’s evolving business landscape.

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
The program delivers a strong foundation and in-demand skills that prepare you to excel in diverse professional contexts through industry-aligned, practice-based coursework.
Skill development for all experience levels 
This certificate equips you to excel in technical and nontechnical product management roles. It is designed for learners of all experience levels, regardless of your starting point.
Customizable, industry-aligned, and professionally relevant 
Gain industry-aligned skills designed for career advancement. Select from four one-credit experiential courses built to draw on cross-functional expertise.
Accelerate your career and build product management skills 
The certificate can be completed in as little as four months. Quickly amplify your existing career or transition into product management roles.
Flexible learning modalities 
If you are looking to build new skills with immediate career return, this certificate program is for you. Add the concentration to your current master’s degree, or earn it as a standalone.
