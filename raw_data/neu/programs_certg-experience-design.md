Program overview 
##  Create unforgettable experiences 
Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Experience Design immerses you in research-driven design thinking, preparing you to be a key contributor and leader in experience design teams. This program goes beyond traditional design principles, focusing on enhancing the quality of human experiences in various settings.
Through four targeted courses, you’ll develop skills in integrating technology, creativity, and empathy to solve real-world challenges in industries like healthcare, travel, and entertainment. Open to professionals and recent graduates from diverse fields, this certificate equips you with the mindset and tools to innovate in a rapidly evolving design landscape.

Program highlights 
  * Complete the certificate in as little as two semesters.
  * Gain skills applicable across multiple sectors, including healthcare, technology, and public services.
  * Engage with a vibrant cohort of students and participate in events, exhibitions, and guest lectures.
  * Specialize your learning with an elective tailored to your career goals.


Career outlook 
Experience design is increasingly vital across many industries. Graduates with expertise in designing meaningful interactions can excel in roles within design agencies, tech companies, healthcare, and public institutions–enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.
Experiential learning 
Northeastern’s signature experiential learning model places you at the forefront of real-world design challenges. Participate in workshops, collaborate with faculty on projects, and join a network of creative professionals. This dynamic experience prepares you to make an immediate impact in the evolving field of experience design.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Experience Design](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-experience-design/master-of-science-in-experience-design/)
  * [Master of Fine Arts in Experience Design](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mfa-experience-design/mfa-in-experience-design/)
