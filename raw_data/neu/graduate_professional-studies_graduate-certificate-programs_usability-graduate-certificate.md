## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
The Graduate Certificate in Usability is a practical, in-demand, career-focused graduate certificate. The certificate stresses both a broad, theory-based introduction to the field, as well as the ability to choose from focused electives, with an emphasis on practical assignments. This certificate highlights the key skills and tools used by usability generalists, working in a broad variety of fields.
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
