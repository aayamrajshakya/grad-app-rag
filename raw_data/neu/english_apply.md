Join us for Welcome Day.
Our Graduate Programs Welcome Day events include an overview of the College of Social Sciences and Humanities and its Experiential Liberal Arts mission, as well as a themed panel discussion, featuring faculty and students from our various graduate programs. Following a college overview, participants will have time to speak with faculty, staff, and students from their particular program of interest. Participants receive an application fee waiver for attending. [RSVP today](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/apply/welcome-day/)!
