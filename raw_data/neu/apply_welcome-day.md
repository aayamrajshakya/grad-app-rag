# Graduate Programs Welcome Day
Join us for Welcome Day! Prospective graduate students, current applicants and admitted students are invited to attend our Virtual Welcome Day to hear from current students and faculty. Register now for one of our upcoming events.
## Attend a Virtual Welcome Day
Welcome Day events include an overview of the College of Social Sciences and Humanities (CSSH) and its Experiential Liberal Arts mission, and program-specific breakout sessions, featuring faculty and students from each of our graduate programs. RSVP today!
Please note: if you are interested in the Master of Public Administration (MPA), Master of Public Policy (MPP) or Master of Science in Criminology and Criminal Justice, you can find program specific info sessions here: [College of Social Sciences and Humanities Events – Public Affairs Master’s Degree Programs at Northeastern](https://publicaffairs.northeastern.edu/events/)
## Visit CSSH, Get an Application Fee Waiver
If you attend a CSSH Welcome Day, join an Open House session, or meet with a Northeastern University staff or faculty member through another graduate program recruitment event before you apply  It pays to plan ahead as we will not refund fees already paid.
