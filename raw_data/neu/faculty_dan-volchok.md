## Biography
Dan Volchok has nearly 40 years of Higher Education experience on both the Student Affairs and Academic Affairs side of the house. He is currently Associate Dean of the Sackler School of Graduate Biomedical Sciences at Tufts University where he is responsible for all academic and student service administration. He was previously Director of Program and Student Services in the D’Amore-McKim School of Business and most recently Assistant Dean for Graduate Student Life, both at Northeastern. Previously he was a Dean of Students and VP for Student Development, and worked for an online learning start-up. He is a lecturer in Northeastern’s Graduate School of Education’s MEd and EdD programs as well as the Global Studies and International Relations program. He has an MBA from Ohio University and Ed.D. in Higher Education Administration from Teachers College, Columbia University.
