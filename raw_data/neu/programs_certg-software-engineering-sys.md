Program overview 
##  Help design impactful solutions 
The Graduate Certificate in Software Engineering Systems focuses on using engineering tools and considering real-world complexities to design and construct practical and viable software solutions.
This four-course graduate certificate seeks to provide students opportunities to apply the fundamentals of engineering knowledge and skills to software development with attention on enterprise design and integration, secure systems design and creation, and data integration and architecture.
Note:Master of Science in Software Engineering Systems students are not eligible for this graduate certificate.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Information Systems](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msis-information-systems/master-of-science-in-information-systems-boston/)
  * [Master of Science in Data Architecture and Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-data-architecture-and-mgmt/master-of-science-in-data-architecture-and-management/)
  * [Master of Science in Telecommunication Networks](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-telecommunication-networks/master-of-science-in-telecommunication-networks/)
  * [Master of Science in Cyber-Physical Systems](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-cyber-physical-systems/master-of-science-in-cyber-physical-systems-boston/)
