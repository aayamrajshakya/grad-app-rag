#### Location

From Green Line T:
  *     * Exit at Northeastern stop.
    * Cross the street toward Forsyth Street; look for the Cabot Physical Education Center.
    * Walk south on Forsyth Street for 2-3 minutes.
    * The Forsyth Building will be on your left. It is a cement building.
    * There is a red sign on the building for “University Health and Counseling Services.”


From Orange Line T:
  *     * Exit at Ruggles stop.
    * Go up the escalator to the station. Turn left to exit.
    * You will end up on Northeastern’s campus. There will be a traffic circle as you exit the station.
    * Walk straight down Forsyth Steet for 3 minutes.
    * The Forsyth Building will be on your right. It is a cement building.
