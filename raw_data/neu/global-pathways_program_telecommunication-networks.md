# Telecommunication Networks
[« Return to Programs](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/programs/)
The Master of Science in Telecommunication Networks degree is designed for professionals currently in the dynamic, exciting, and thriving telecommunications industry who either wish to enhance their technical skills and credentials or who wish to make a transition to the business side of telecommunications.
If you want to earn your master’s in Telecommunication Networks at Northeastern, Global Pathways can help you succeed through rigorous language training, cultural activities, and support.
[Apply Now »](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/)
### Entry Requirements
To enter this Global Pathways program, you must meet the College of Engineering’s admissions scores. Please note, your English language score will determine the length of your Global Pathways studies.
#### English Language for Global Pathways
English Score for 1 term:  
TOEFL iBT 74 / IELTS 6.5 / PTE 50
English Score for 2 term:  
TOEFL iBT 61 / IELTS 6.0 (no band below 5.5) / PTE 46
