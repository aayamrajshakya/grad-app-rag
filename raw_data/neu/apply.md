Not ready to commit to a degree or certificate program? Apply to be a Special Student!
Non-degree special students are nonmatriculated students who are enrolled on a part-time basis (fewer than 8 semester hours). Special students may earn up to 12 semester hours over time. After completing 12 semester hours, a special student who wants to continue their graduate studies should apply to a degree or certificate program. See the Academic Catalog for more information about the [special student classification](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/social-sciences-humanities/general-regulations/general-information/).
To apply as a special student, select “CSSH Special Student” for the program option and “Special Student/Non-Degree” for the degree option. Applicants should include the CSSH graduate courses that they would like to take should they be admitted within their personal statement.
Ready to apply for special student status? Check out the [Deadlines and Required Materials](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/apply/graduate-admissions/) page.
