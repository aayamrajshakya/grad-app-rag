#  SimonRabinovitch
Stotsky Professor of Jewish Historical and Cultural Studies 
Simon Rabinovitch is the Stotsky Professor of Jewish Historical and Cultural Studies, and he teaches and writes on a range of topics in European, Jewish, Russian, and legal history.
