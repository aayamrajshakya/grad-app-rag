##  Become a global citizen 
At Northeastern, teaching and research are grounded in global engagement. Students participate in experiential learning opportunities on all seven continents, 320,000-plus alumni live and work in 186 countries, and more than 3,800 organizations worldwide partner with us to help open doors and create opportunities.
##  Expand your perspective 
Northeastern believes in a welcoming and inclusive community that invites diverse viewpoints from all. And each year, more than 21,000 international students from 136+ countries call our campuses home.
Our students bring global perspectives and cultural context to learning both inside and outside the classroom, broadening students’ views and enriching our community.
  * [ Learn more about our community](https://belonging.northeastern.edu/)


##  Make the world your campus 
Northeastern brings the world to our campuses and our students to the world. You can choose to learn online or at one of our 13 campuses located in emerging hubs of tech, business, education, and other in-demand industries.
  * [ Explore our campuses ](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/global-campus-network/)


##  Gain international experience 
As a graduate student, you can participate in experience-based learning opportunities wherever makes the most sense for your goals.
Global co-op, research, field study, and Dialogue of Civilization opportunities allow you to positively impact communities while gaining international work experience and lasting professional connections.
  * [ Visit the Global Experience Office](https://geo.northeastern.edu/)
