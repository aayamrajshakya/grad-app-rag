## Your academic advisor: Here to help
Academic advisors provide expert guidance in course registration and academic planning. They are also available to answer any other questions and connect you to university resources. Current students can find their assigned advisor on their student account.  
  
Incoming students receive communications from their campus advising contact until their academic advisor is assigned in the first week of classes. For questions about advising, please reach out to khoury-gradadministration@northeastern.edu.

##    
Get involved with research
Students interested in conducting research can pursue several types of opportunities during their graduate program. Research can be taken for credit or can be a volunteer/paid opportunity with a faculty member. You can also connect with your academic advisor to learn more about current research opportunities at a specific campus location.
[Explore research opportunities](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/graduate-research/)
