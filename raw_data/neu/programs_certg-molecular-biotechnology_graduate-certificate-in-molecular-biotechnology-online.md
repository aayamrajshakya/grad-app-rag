Program overview 
##  Precise science, online 
The Graduate Certificate in Molecular Biotechnology-Online is designed for those seeking advanced knowledge in cutting-edge molecular biology techniques and protein structure analysis. This program is tailored to enhance competency in modern biotechnological practices, allowing professionals to contribute more effectively in their roles.
The Graduate Certificate in Molecular Biotechnology-Online is designed for those seeking advanced knowledge in cutting-edge molecular biology techniques and protein structure analysis. This program is tailored to enhance competency in modern biotechnological practices, allowing professionals to contribute more effectively in their roles.
Program highlights 
  * The certificate consists of four or five courses totaling 12 credits, offering flexibility for busy professionals.
  * This program can serve as a pathway to the MS in Biotechnology; completed credits may be applied toward the master’s degree if accepted.
  * While the coursework is available entirely online, certain lab components are conducted on-ground to provide hands-on experience.
  * Learn from faculty with industry experience, providing insights into real-world biotechnology applications.


Career outlook 
The biotechnology industry is rapidly growing and brimming with opportunities. Professionals equipped with advanced scientific knowledge and practical skills are positioned to drive innovation, tackling critical healthcare and environmental challenges. The ongoing expansion of the biotech sector opens a multitude of career pathways, from large pharmaceutical corporations to entrepreneurial start-ups.
Experiential learning 
Northeastern’s signature experiential learning model combines academics with practical application. The online courses include in-depth case studies and collaborative research projects, preparing you for real-world challenges in biotechnology.

Admissions requirements 
  * Online application
  * Unofficial transcripts (official transcripts required upon acceptance of admissions)
    * The Foreign Credential Evaluation (FCE) is a required assessment of all transcripts and documents from non-U.S. accredited post-secondary education institutions. (Review the [FCE requirements](https://northeastern.my.site.com/GraduateAdmissionsFAQ/s/article/What-documents-are-required-for-a-credential-evaluation) by country.)
  * GPA 3.0 or higher
  * Personal statement
  * English language proficiency:
    * Degree earned or in progress at a U.S. institution
    * Degree earned or in progress at an institution where English is the only medium of instruction
    * Official exam scores from either the TOEFL iBT (institution code is 3682), IELTS, PTE exam, or Duolingo English Test. Scores are valid for 2 years from the test date.
