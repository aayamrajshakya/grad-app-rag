Program overview 
##  Learn alongside XR experts 
Northeastern’s Master of Science in Extended Realities (XR) provides you with a deep understanding of the interdisciplinary skills, theoretical foundations, technologies, and processes essential for creating impactful XR experiences.
The groundbreaking program partners with multiple Northeastern colleges—ensuring that you are exposed to a breadth of theory, technologies, and perspectives.
You’ll have the flexibility to choose a specialized path that matches your goals and interests. Concentration offerings take advantage of expertise across the university, bringing you access to global experts and innovators.

Industry-specific concentrations 
Specialize in one of 10 industry-aligned concentrations offered by:
The College of Arts, Media and Design:
  * XR+design
  * XR+media
  * XR+games


The College of Engineering:
  * Wireless networking


The College of Social Sciences and Humanities:
  * Informatics ethics
  * Public history


Bouvé College of Health Sciences:
  * Human movement science


Khoury College of Computer Sciences:
  * Artificial intelligence
  * Human-computer interaction


D’Amore-McKim School of Business:
  * Marketing


Unique features 
  * The program’s flexibility will prepare you to compete for the most innovative positions in medicine, business, entertainment, architecture, journalism, and other industries leveling XR technologies.
  * Northeastern has several immersive lab spaces dedicated to AR, VR, and XR research and design, including the Immersive Media Labs and Experiential Technologies Lab.
  * A unique curriculum enhanced with a multi-college partnership that connects the College of Arts, Media and Design, Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Bouvé College of Health Sciences, College of Engineering, College of Social Sciences and Humanities, and the D’Amore-McKim School of Business.


Career outlook 
XR technologies have seen explosive growth over the last decade, and professionals with the skills to work in augmented, virtual, and mixed reality are in high demand.
  * 33% compounded annual XR market growth expected from 2022 through 2030 (Xrtoday.com, 2022)
  * 23 million jobs are projected to be enhanced by XR technology by 2030 (Statista.com, 2019)
  * $129,000 estimated median total pay for an XR Developer in the U.S. (Glassdoor.com, 2025)

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
The Master of Science in Extended Realities serves a growing market demand, as XR technologies are projected to see exponential industry growth with increasing career opportunities and high earning potential.
Learn cutting-edge XR skills and tools 
Students gain a deep understanding of XR theory, history, and state-of-the-art hardware and software for augmented, virtual, and mixed reality.
Immerse yourself in XR 
Through critical analysis and hands-on projects, students learn to critique XR experiences, develop domain-specific vocabulary, craft engaging narrative content, and rapidly prototype innovative XR solutions.
