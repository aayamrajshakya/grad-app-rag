# Pharmaceutics and Drug Delivery
[« Return to Programs](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/programs/)
The Master of Science in Pharmaceutics and Drug Delivery seeks to produce specialists in drug development and delivery who use many approaches to create and deliver treatments.
If you want to earn your master’s in Pharmaceutics and Drug Delivery at Northeastern, Global Pathways can help you succeed through rigorous language training, cultural activities, and support.
