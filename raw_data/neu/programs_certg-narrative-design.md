Program overview 
##  Story-driven design 
The Graduate Certificate in Narrative Design provides interdisciplinary training in storytelling, interactive design, and digital media production. This program prepares students for roles in the gaming industry, digital entertainment, and interactive media by teaching them how to create compelling narratives that enhance user engagement and drive commercial success.
Students gain real-world experience through practice-oriented problem-solving approaches and real-world projects with industry collaborations. The certificate offers maximum flexibility for completion and helps establish pathways toward advanced degree programs for continued professional development.

Program features 
  * Flexible learning options: Complete coursework through a combination of on-ground and virtual models built to accommodate working professionals.
  * Global perspective: Learn alongside students from more than 15 countries, engaging with diverse cultural storytelling traditions and building international professional networks.
  * Industry connections: Collaborate on real-world projects with gaming and digital media industry partners, gaining practical experience with immediate professional application.
  * Expert faculty: Learn from renowned faculty with extensive experience in narrative design, game development, and interactive media production.

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
Prepare for success in narrative design by developing storytelling, interactive design, and collaborative skills that meet the growing demand for effective storytellers.
Workforce ready 
Develop the storytelling skills you need to meet the growing demand for effective narrative designers in the game design industry and digital media sectors.
Interactive storytelling 
Explore the process of creating interactive, media-rich stories for digital platforms using industry-standard tools and proven methodologies.
Learning by doing 
Gain practical experience in narrative design through practice-oriented, problem-solving approaches and real-world project collaboration.
Narrative innovation 
Bridge classic storytelling techniques with modern interactive media to create compelling narratives for contemporary digital platforms.
