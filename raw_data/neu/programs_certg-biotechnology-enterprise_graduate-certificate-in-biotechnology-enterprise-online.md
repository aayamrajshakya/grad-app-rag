Program overview 
##  Biotech business, online 
The online Graduate Certificate in Biotechnology Enterprise is designed for professionals with a background in biotechnology who want to enhance their business prowess.
This flexible, one-year program covers key aspects of the biotech industry, focusing on strategic management, regulatory affairs, and business development. By improving your business competency, you’ll be equipped to take on leadership roles, manage teams effectively, and navigate the business side of the biotech world.
Taught by industry-aligned faculty with real-world experience, this program offers practical knowledge you can immediately apply to your career. Plus, the credits you earn can be applied toward Northeastern’s MS in Biotechnology program.

Curriculum highlights 
  * Courses are designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of biotech business practices, covering areas like finance, strategic management, and regulatory affairs.
  * Gain hands-on experience through in-class case studies and exercises that mirror real-world biotech scenarios.
  * The program consists of four to five courses, totaling 12 semester hours, allowing you to tailor your studies to your career goals.

Admissions requirements 
  * Online application
  * Unofficial transcripts (official transcripts required upon acceptance of admissions)
    * The Foreign Credential Evaluation (FCE) is a required assessment of all transcripts and documents from non-U.S. accredited post-secondary education institutions. (Review the [FCE requirements](https://northeastern.my.site.com/GraduateAdmissionsFAQ/s/article/What-documents-are-required-for-a-credential-evaluation) by country.)
  * GPA 3.0 or higher
  * Personal statement
  * English language proficiency:
    * Degree earned or in progress at a U.S. institution
    * Degree earned or in progress at an institution where English is the only medium of instruction
    * Official exam scores from either the TOEFL iBT (institution code is 3682), IELTS, PTE exam, or Duolingo English Test. Scores are valid for 2 years from the test date.
