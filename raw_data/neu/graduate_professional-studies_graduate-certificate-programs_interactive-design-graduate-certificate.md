## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
Digital media plays an increasingly significant role in the global culture and economy. The Graduate Certificate in Interactive Design offers an overview of courses in the creative process of storytelling and communicating through visuals and sound. Students have an opportunity to gain expertise in time-based design and interface and experience design through a practice-oriented problem-solving approach.
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
