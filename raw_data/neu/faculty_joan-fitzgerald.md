#  JoanFitzgerald
Professor of Public Policy and Urban Affairs 
Joan Fitzgerald is a Professor of Urban and Public Policy. She focuses on urban climate action and strategies for linking it to equity, economic development, and innovation. In her fourth book, Greenovation: Urban Leadership on Climate ChangeCities and the Struggle for Climate Justice[Planetizen](https://www.planetizen.com/user/9637%22%20/t%20%22_blank). She teaches The 21st Century City, Cities, Sustainability & Climate Change, and Intro Environmental Science & Policy.
