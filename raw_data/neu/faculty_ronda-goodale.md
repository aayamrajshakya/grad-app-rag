# Ronda Goodale
Dr.Goodale is psychologist and educator who has extensive experience in urban schools at all levels.
* * *
Contact
[[email protected]](https://cps.northeastern.edu/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#56247831393932373a3316383924223e3337252233243878333223)
## Biography
Dr. Goodale Has worked for more than 30 years in urban schools as a teacher , administrator and psychologist, and consultant. Her areas of expertise include: diversity, students with disabilities, assessment, classroom management, early childhood education, second language learning, literacy, math and litigation. She currently is the chair of the early education and care department, Cambridge College, senior lecturer at University of Mass.-Boston, adjunct faculty member at William James College, as well as a consultant to school systems and parents.
