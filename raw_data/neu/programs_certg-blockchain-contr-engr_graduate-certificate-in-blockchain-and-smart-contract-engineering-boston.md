Program overview 
##  Shape the future of blockchain 
The Graduate Certificate in Blockchain and Smart Contract Engineering delves into the transformative field of blockchain technology. Designed for professionals across industries, this program provides in-depth knowledge of platforms like Ethereum, which facilitate transparency and trust in multiparty relationships.
Explore the impact of blockchain on digital currencies, secure transactions, and legally binding contracts. Additionally, you’ll master cryptoengineering techniques to create digital trust fabrics that can safely handle all kinds of transactions. With hands-on learning in the heart of Boston, you’ll be well-positioned to advance or shift your career in this rapidly evolving space.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Information Systems](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msis-information-systems/)
