#  AnnCai
Teaching Professor of Chinese 
Dr. Ann Cai, Full Teaching Professor of the College of Social Science and Humanities at Northeastern University (NU). Ed.D. in Educational Psychology. Dr. Cai has extensive teaching experience at NU, Harvard University & MIT. She has received distinction teaching awards from Harvard University. Research and teaching areas: AI-Assisted Bilingual Scriptwriting; AI Chatbot, Bilingual Guided Meditation, Rap Songs, & Comparative studies of Quantum Physics, Evolutionary Psychology, & Buddhism, etc.
