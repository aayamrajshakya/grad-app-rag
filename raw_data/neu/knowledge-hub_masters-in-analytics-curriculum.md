Northeastern’s [Master of Professional Studies in Analytics](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/program/master-of-professional-studies-in-analytics-online-14942/) program gives students the skills they need to succeed in today’s data-driven world. Developed by experienced thought leaders from across the analytics industry, the program incorporates a range of employer-sponsored projects, from data extraction to the translation and implementation of data analysis.
Our program is interwoven with rigorous academics and experiential learning opportunities that provide you with professional work experiences across various industries, including financial technology, healthcare, biotechnology, business, and pharma.
The analytics program at Northeastern will strengthen your knowledge of the latest analytics technologies, applications, and practices that are reshaping today’s businesses. Graduates from the program will be able to confidently:
  * Create a portfolio that demonstrates the competencies mastered and key projects completed.
  * Gather and interpret big data.
  * Gain a strong foundation of a core analytical skillset to build upon more technical skillset or industry-specific applications.
  * Develop a working relationship with industry leaders and other analytic professionals that’ll help them now and in the future.
  * Choose from several flexible programming options led by faculty experts and industry guest speakers.
  * Contribute and lead the way for the future direction of data analytics.


Data is increasingly shaping the world, and there is a growing demand for people with this skill set. Below, we provide you with a comprehensive look at how our program helps you make your mark anywhere.
## Core analytics courses
In the MPS program, students must complete six core analytics classes. Each core course is designed to broaden and deepen your understanding of analytics to prepare you for a successful career. The [required courses](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/professional-studies/masters-degree-programs/analytics-mps/#programrequirementstext) include:
  1. Introduction to Analytics
  2. Probability Theory and Introductory Statistics
  3. Intermediate Analytics
  4. Introduction to Enterprise Analytics
  5. Communication and Visualization for Data Analytics
  6. Database Management Systems


You’ll benefit from small class sizes, collaborating with renowned faculty members, and working with leading companies from around the world. A cumulative 3.0 GPA and 45 total quarter hours are required to complete the program.
## Analytics concentrations
Northeastern’s MPS students choose one of three specialization tracks that prepare them to work in an in-demand role within the industry.
These concentrations include:
  * [Applied Machine Intelligence](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/professional-studies/masters-degree-programs/analytics-mps/#Applied%20Machine%20Intelligence)
  * [Evidence-Based Management](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/professional-studies/masters-degree-programs/analytics-mps/#Evidence-Based%20Management)
  * [Statistical Modeling](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/professional-studies/masters-degree-programs/analytics-mps/#Statistical%20Modeling)


## Analytics electives to help set you apart
Northeastern’s master’s in analytics curriculum offers an array of elective courses to help you shape a unique learning experience. Some popular electives include:
  * Applications of Artificial Intelligence
  * Predictive Analytics
  * Leadership in Analytics
  * Risk Management for Analytics
  * Data Management and Big Data


Students take a minimum of two electives that help broaden their knowledge and explore different career interests.
## Analytics capstone project
The capstone practicum is critical to the MPS experience and provides a high-level analytical application of real-world data. You’ll partner with a local organization to solve an analytics problem integral to the company’s operation. The MPS capstone bridges theory and practice, giving you the real-world skills you need to succeed. At the end of the project, you’ll present your analytical insights and recommendations to successfully implement your project.
## Integrated experiential learning
Experience is at the heart of our analytics master’s program. Powered by our signature co-op program, [experiential learning](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/why-northeastern/about-our-academics/experiential-learning/) enables you to explore your interests, find your passion, and acquire the skills and knowledge you need for future success.
The program also incorporates a practicum in the development and delivery of predictive data analysis for strategic decision-making in organizations. You’ll get the opportunity to apply the principles and tools of analytics to real-world problems in business organizations and to develop and present analytical insights and recommendations for successful implementation of your capstone project.
## The future you want starts here
In today’s increasingly technology-driven environment, the ability to manage and analyze large volumes of data is critical to growth, innovation, and productivity in every field.
From finding cures for cancer to plotting more efficient service delivery routes, industries need people with curiosity, creativity, knowledge, and skills to analyze and model complex datasets and draw actionable insights.
Enter Northeastern’s [Master of Professional Studies in Analytics](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/program/master-of-professional-studies-in-analytics-boston-14859/), a program aligned with industry trends. No matter what stage you’re at in your career, we’ll provide you with the experiences and support you need to advance your career.
