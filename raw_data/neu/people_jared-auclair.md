Vice Provost for Research Economic Development, and Director of Bioinnovation
# Jared Auclair
## Biography
Jared R. Auclair is currently the interim dean of the College of Professional Studies, vice provost for research economic development, and director of bioinnovation in the Office of the Provost. As vice provost for research economic development, Auclair works to strengthen the bonds between our education and research missions by strengthening the integration of work-integrated credentialed learning and use-inspired research, co-creating with communities and partners while expanding our global mindset. As director of bioinnovation, he works to leverage important university activities around biotechnology, bringing together experts from a wide range of disciplines and backgrounds to advance the expansion of Northeastern’s life sciences programs. In addition to these roles, Auclair holds a faculty appointment in the Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology, where he collaborates with academic researchers, industry, and government in the area of biopharmaceutical development and analysis.
