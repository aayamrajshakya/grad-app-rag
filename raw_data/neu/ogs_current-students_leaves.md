#  Leaves of Absence 
There are many reasons why you may have to make the difficult decision to take a leave of absence. Whatever the reason may be, the Office of Global Services is here to support you. Learn what steps you must take to protect your visa status.

###  [ University Health & Counseling Services ](https://www.northeastern.edu/uhcs/forms/medical-leave-of-absence/)
Need a medical leave of absence? Students need to first apply through University Health & Counseling Services (UHCS) and once approved, OGS will authorize the medical leave in SEVIS and issue an updated I-20.

###  [ Academic Skills Advising ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/student-support/academic-services/academic-skills-advising/)
If you're considering a leave of absence for academic reasons, an Academic Skills Advising appointment can help you evaluate your options and offer concrete strategies for improving your performance in your classes if you decide to stay at Northeastern for the semester.
[ Learn More ](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/student-support/academic-services/academic-skills-advising/)
