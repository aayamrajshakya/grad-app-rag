#  MixieWholey
Postdoctoral Teaching Associate, English 
Mixie Wholey (she/they) is a Boston-based writer, educator and drag performer. She has a PhD in Literature from Northeastern University, writing extensively on queer internet culture and performance art as a form of queer activism. Her work has been featured in HuffPost, Xtra, and Salty.
