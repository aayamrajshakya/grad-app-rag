Program overview 
##  Help improve people's lives 
Northeastern University’s Master of Science in Human Movement and Rehabilitation Sciences prepares students for practice with an intensive study of the revolutionary breakthroughs that allow our bodies to work better. This one-year program is focused on innovative rehabilitation solutions through research and education covering:
  * Biomechanics
  * Control of movement
  * Health and well-being through movement and design
  * Human-cybernetic system interactions
  * Neurorehabilitation of movement and function
  * Motor learning
  * Musculoskeletal disorders


You’ll engage in a curriculum developed by industry professionals that brings you an understanding of state-of-the-art technology and offers you a robust choice of electives to personalize your knowledge and expertise.

Admissions requirements 
  * Academic transcript(s)
  * Statement of purpose
  * Resumé/CV
  * Two letters of reference
