Committee Signature Page:
Begin the Committee Signature Page process only after successfully defending and making all required revisions.
Once revisions are complete, use the appropriate “Initiate Your Signature Page” link below to start the signature collection process. The Graduate School of Engineering will notify you once all signatures are obtained and when you may submit your final document to ProQuest.
For questions, please contact coe-gradadvising@northeastern.edu, and your advisor will assist.
