#  CombinedEnglish Majors
The Department of English offers combined majors for students wishing to broaden and enrich their studies. View the Undergraduate Catalog for the requirements of each of the English combined major:
  * [English and Communication Studies, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/english/english-communication-studies-ba/)
  * [English and Cultural Anthropology, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/english/english-cultural-anthropology-ba/)
  * [English and Graphic and Information Design, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/english/english-graphic-information-design-ba/)
  * [English and Philosophy, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/english/english-philosophy-ba/)
  * [English and Theatre, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/english/english-theatre-ba/)
  * [Architecture and English, BS](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/arts-media-design/architecture/architecture-english-bs/)
  * [Computer Science and English, BS](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/computer-information-science/computer-information-science-combined-majors/computer-science-english-bs/)
  * [History and English, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/history/history-english-ba/)
  * [Journalism and English, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/arts-media-design/journalism/journalism-english-ba/)
  * [Linguistics and English, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/science/linguistics/linguistics-english-ba/)
  * [Media and Screen Studies and English, BA](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/arts-media-design/communication-studies/media-screen-studies-english-ba/)
  * [Biology and English, BS](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/science/biology/biology-english-bs/)


Students can also create a new English combined major with almost any other program. Contact Professor Patrick Mullen, Undergraduate Program Director, for details.
