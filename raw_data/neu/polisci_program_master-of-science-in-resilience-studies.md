#  Master of Science Resilience Studies
## Who Could Have Seen This Coming? You Did.
As a resilience professional, your city, organization, or country looks to you to prepare for the next emergency. Design coastal wetlands plans to safeguard cities from rising tides. Develop communication strategies to combat political radicalization. Lead global organizations through cyber threats. With our MS Resilience Studies, you’ll apply strategic planning to protect the communities you care about. 
## Paid Experience, Real-World Impact
Build a portfolio of impressive resilience projects through an optional, paid, full-time, 6-month co-op. Connect with key employers or gain research experience at the forefront of the field. Past co-op partners have included leading organizations like Climate XChange, Boston City Hall, Interpol, Oxfam America,
