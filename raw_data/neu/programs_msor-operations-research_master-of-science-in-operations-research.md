Program overview 
##  Bring a scientific eye to decision making 
Operations research deals with the application of scientific methods to decision-making. Students learn how to develop and solve mathematical and computer models of systems using optimization and statistical methods.
Our program is offered jointly by the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department and the Mathematics Department, thus achieving a unique balance of theory and application. Graduates work in a wide variety of fields, including transportation, supply chain operations, communications and computer operations, manufacturing, finance, and health care.
The MS in Operations Research can be combined with a [Gordon Engineering Leadership certificate](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/certg-engineering-leadership/graduate-certificate-in-engineering-leadership/). Learn more about the benefits of this unique program.
