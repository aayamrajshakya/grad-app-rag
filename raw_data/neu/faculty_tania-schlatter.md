## Biography
Tania Schlatter is a UX & UI designer, author, and educator. Tania works with designers, researchers, and developers to help decide what to design, and how to design it using a system-aware and user-centered approach. She leads UX & UI research and design at the Institute for Quantitative Social Science at Harvard University and teaches undergraduate and graduate classes in interaction and information design at Northeastern University.
## Education
MDes from Illinois Institute of Technology (1999)
## Area of Expertise
Graphic Design, Human-Computer Interaction, usability, User Experience Design, user research
