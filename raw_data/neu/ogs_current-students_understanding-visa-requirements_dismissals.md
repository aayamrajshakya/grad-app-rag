#  Students Studying in The United States 
If you are preparing to leave Northeastern University, it is important to understand all of the steps that you must take in order to maintain your non-immigrant status. 
##  I am finishing my program of study and graduating 
Congratulations on graduating! 
Students who have completed a program of study at Northeastern University 
> _Transcript[request transcripts here.](https://registrar.northeastern.edu/group/transcripts/)_
> _Diploma[questions about your diploma directly to the Registrar.](https://service.northeastern.edu/registrar)_
> _Commencement[[email protected]](https://international.northeastern.edu/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection). Please include your NUID._
F-1 Students are granted a 60-day grace period during which they may:
  * Remain in the U.S. and prepare to depart
  * [Request a Change of Degree Level (CDL) I-20](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/current-students/understanding-visa-requirements/change-of-degree-level/) to begin a new program of study at Northeastern University
  * Request a SEVIS transfer to another SEVP-certified institution in the U.S.
  * [Apply for Post-OPT](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/employment/off-campus-employment/applying-for-post-completion-opt-f-1/) (if eligible after concluding your degree program)
  * Change to another immigration status


J-1 Students are granted a 30-day grace period during which they may:
  * Remain in the U.S. and prepare to depart
  * All other requests and changes ([CDL](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/current-students/understanding-visa-requirements/change-of-degree-level/), SEVIS transfers, [Academic Training](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/employment/off-campus-employment/j-1-academic-training/)) must be completed prior to the program end date.


Please note that if you have withdrawn prior to completion, been academically dismissed, or have been found in violation of your F/J student status you are not eligible for a grace period. Please consult directly with OGS if you have any questions. 
##  I am withdrawing or taking a leave of absence 
If you are withdrawing from Northeastern or taking a Leave of Absence (medical or non-medical), you must communicate with OGS before you drop or withdraw from your classes
To notify OGS that you plan to Withdraw from Northeastern 
  * [Notification to OGS of University Withdrawal – officeofglobalservices (northeastern.edu)](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/forms-and-guides/notification-to-ogs-of-university-withdrawal/)
    * An Designated School Official (DSO) or Alternate Responsible Officer (ARO) will follow up with you regarding your next required steps


To notify OGS of your intent to take a Leave of Absence
  * [Medical Leave Of Absence – officeofglobalservices (northeastern.edu)](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/current-students/leaves-of-absence/medical-leave-of-absence/)
  * [Non-Medical Leave of Absence – officeofglobalservices (northeastern.edu)](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/current-students/leaves-of-absence/non-medical-leave-of-absence/)


##  I have been academically dismissed 
If you have been academically dismissed from Northeastern, you will no longer be eligible for enrollment. OGS will contact you directly to discuss your next steps.
If you are anticipating that you may be academically dismissed, please schedule an appointment with a Designated School Official/DSO (F-1 Students) or Alternate Responsible Officer/ARO (J-1 Students) to proactively discuss your options by calling +1-617-373-2310 or visiting the front desk at 354 Richards Hall in Boston.

Returning to Northeastern after an Academic Dismissal

##  I am transferring to another SEVP-Certified school in the U.S. 
If you are leaving/completing your current degree program, or ending a period of approved Post-OPT/STEM OPT, you may be eligible to request that your SEVIS record be transferred to another SEVP-certified school in the U.S.
To learn about the SEVIS Transfer process, please review the following pages:
  * [Transferring Out – officeofglobalservices (northeastern.edu)](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/current-students/understanding-visa-requirements/transferring-out/)
  * [Transfer out request e-form](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/forms-and-guides/transfer-out-request/)


##  I am Discontinuing Post-OPT/STEM OPT 
If you plan to depart the U.S. and discontinue your Post-OPT/STEM OPT extension, you will need to report this to OGS via one of the following e-forms:
  * Students on Post-OPT:ubmit the [Post-OPT Central](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/forms-and-guides/ogs-post-completion-opt-central/)
  * Students on the STEM Extension:[Request to Complete STEM OPT and SEVIS Record](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/forms-and-guides/request-to-complete-stem-opt-sevis-record/)


If your Post-OPT/STEM Extension will be discontinued due to a change of status to H-1B, you will need to report this to OGS.
  * Students should submit the [H-1B Status Change Reporting e-form](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/forms-and-guides/h-1b-status-change-reporting/)
