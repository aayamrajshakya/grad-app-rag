# Inside the March 2025 Silicon Valley Immersion: Tech Leaders, Innovation, and Experiential Learning
Mar 25, 2025[Artificial Intelligence (AI)](https://online.northeastern.edu/resources/categories/ai/), [Engineering](https://online.northeastern.edu/resources/categories/engineering/), [Information Systems & IT](https://online.northeastern.edu/resources/categories/information-systems-it/), [Technology](https://online.northeastern.edu/resources/categories/technology/)
On March 27–28, learners from Northeastern University’s [Online MBA](https://online.northeastern.edu/business/onlinemba/), [Online MS in Information Systems](https://online.northeastern.edu/engineering/ms-information-systems/), and [Online MS in Data Analytics Engineering](https://online.northeastern.edu/engineering/ms-data-analytics-engineering/), and [Online Grad Cert in AI Applications](https://online.northeastern.edu/certificates/ai-applications/) programs participated in a place-based immersion in Silicon Valley
### A visit to Coursera headquarters
The two-day experience kicked off with a visit to Coursera’s global headquarters
Special thanks to:
  * Maanie Hamzaee
  * Angela Huwe, MBA


Both speakers offered real-world perspectives from the front lines of digital education and business growth.
### Silicon Valley panels and collaboration
On Friday, the group gathered at Northeastern’s Silicon Valley campus
  * Arash Molavi
  * Sam Sinai
  * Khushboo Singh
  * Steven Aldrich
  * Rebecca Li


Panelists shared lessons on leadership, adaptability, and tech trends shaping the future of business and AI.
