## Flexible Pathways to Success
At Northeastern, you will define your future through a powerful education that integrates learning inside and outside of the classroom.
### Education in Action
Through experiential learning and our signature co-op program, you’ll bring your academics to life, and your life experiences back to your classroom. 
### Go Global
Live, learn, and innovate without boundaries. Each of our 13 campuses offers unique experiences, opportunities, and connections that enrich and inform your learning and research.
### High-Impact Research
Resources that spark breakthroughs and collaboration. We offer leading-edge labs, tools, and technologies for our researchers and partners to tackle large-scale problems from all angles.
## Your military experience gets you further
Military servicemembers and veterans may be eligible to receive transfer credit toward their bachelor’s degree in the College of Professional Studies or their master’s degree in one of Northeastern’s ten colleges.
