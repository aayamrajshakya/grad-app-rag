### Step Two: Upload your documents
Please have a copy of the following documents ready to successfully complete your application:
Proof of English Language Proficiency 
  * If you are submitting a TOEFL score[ ](https://s3-eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/s3-gnspiww-prod/bef42c71-ab05-4cfb-9df4-da7257dd6c59/Northeastern-University-How-To-Send-TOEFL-Scores-Guide.pdf)Northeastern University through your [TOEFL iBT account](https://v2.ereg.ets.org/ereg/public/jump?_p=TEL) (code 4999) before your application can be reviewed.
  * If you are submitting an IELTS score, results are delivered to our Electronic Download account by the IELTS test center. An institution code for IELTS is NOT required. Please contact the test center where you took the IELTS test directly, and request that your test scores be sent to the account name: Northeastern University College of Professional Studies.


Unofficial College Transcripts 
Please ensure that the scanned documents are readable and include all courses, grades (i.e., marks, scores, etc.), and credits completed to date. The scanned documents should also include information about the institution’s grading scale. If admitted, you will need to provide your official transcript. For a transcript to be “official,” it must be in a sealed envelope from the transferring college or university.
Other Documents
  * Statement of purpose – 500 words minimum
  * Letters of recommendation (2 or 3 for graduate programs)
  * Curriculum vitae (CV)/résumé


## When should I apply to Global Pathways at Northeastern?
Since each program is different, you are advised to apply as early as possible, and at least 3 months in advance
Check the[ fees and dates page](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/fees-dates/) for application and start dates.

QUALIFICATIONS FOR OFFICIAL ACADEMIC DOCUMENTS:
Foreign Transcripts must include original English translation and be mailed from institution in a stamped and sealed envelope or e-mailed through E-Script, National Student Clearinghouse, Docufide, or another secure electronic transcript transmission service. If transcripts are not issued from the university in the English language, an official or notarized translation must be sent directly from a certified translation agency, along with the original transcript. Foreign transcripts must show degree completion equivalent to a bachelor’s degree in the United States. We encourage you to submit a course-by-course evaluation of your diploma(s) and transcript(s).
2. Once you have accepted your offer, you will be advised on how to pay your deposit
3. Finally, once you have received your I-20 form, you will be eligible to apply for your student visa
