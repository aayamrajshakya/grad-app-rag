  * Work with faculty and experienced entrepreneurs on projects and case studies that directly apply to business challenges.

## Program options
The Graduate Certificate in Leading People and Organizations offers the flexibility to complete the program at a pace that suits your personal and professional life. In just 12 credits—with the option to expand up to 15 credits to deepen your knowledge base—you'll gain the skills and knowledge needed to confidently take on leadership roles in your career. These sample timelines show how your plan might look, whether you attend full-time or part-time. Actual schedules may vary, so you'll work closely with your advisor to plan your courses. 
Full-time study 
The full-time format is designed for students who can commit to eight months of full-time study. Evening classes run Monday through Thursday, and you can choose from 5:20 p.m. or 7:30 p.m. class times, completing the program in just two semesters. 
The full-time format is F-1 and J-1 visa compliant for international students. This may make you eligible to apply for apply for [Optional Practical Training](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Finternational.northeastern.edu%2Fogs%2Femployment%2Foff-campus-employment%2Fapplying-for-post-completion-opt-f-1%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cc.negron%40northeastern.edu%7Cdc8b31fcd59b4464370c08ddc56bcbde%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C638883788970732150%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=p1JxGM6TC8oWOPS4WnbbA4vgB%2B9DvYWeA3aEVJODbLQ%3D&reserved=0) (F-1 students) or [Academic Training](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Finternational.northeastern.edu%2Fogs%2Femployment%2Foff-campus-employment%2Fj-1-academic-training%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cc.negron%40northeastern.edu%7Cdc8b31fcd59b4464370c08ddc56bcbde%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C638883788970745688%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=ckZWekbWOyvSYsl1pq2ZuZF0Hf2v2YD74oIWURVNxlw%3D&reserved=0) (J-1 students), allowing you to work in the United States for up to 12-months (F-1 students) or 8-months (J-1 students) after your studies are complete. 
Part-time study 
The part-time format is designed for working professionals who would prefer to set their own pace to work with their life and other responsibilities. Evening classes run Monday through Thursday, and you can choose from 5:20 p.m. or 7:30 p.m. class times. You can finish in as little as one year, or if you need to go slower in order to accommodate your work and family commitments, you can finish your certificate over a longer period of up to three years. 
Requirements & electives 
In addition to the required course in managing people and organizations, you'll choose three or four electives that deepen your knowledge in specific areas and prepare you to reach your unique goals.

## Applicant FAQs
Are graduate business certificates worth it? 
Graduate certificates can be a worthwhile investment as they can enhance specific skills, boost career advancement opportunities, and offer flexible, cost-effective education. These programs are ideal for professionals seeking targeted knowledge without the commitment of a full degree, providing networking opportunities and the potential to stand out to employers in a short-term timeframe.
[Learn more about certificates](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/resources/are-graduate-business-certificates-worth-it/)
Graduate certificate vs. master's degree: What business credential is right for me? 
Exploring graduate education options is an important step towards advancing your career, gaining specialized knowledge, and enhancing your professional qualifications. If you're contemplating graduate-level education, you might be weighing the benefits of pursuing a graduate certificate versus a master's degree. Learn more about these distinct types of graduate credentials, the key differences between a graduate certificate and a master's degree, and the pros and cons of each option.
[Learn more](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/resources/graduate-certificate-vs-masters-degree-what-business-credential-is-right-for-you/)

Is English language proficiency required? 
Yes, English proficiency—both written and verbal—is necessary for success in D'Amore-McKim classrooms. If you are not a native English speaker, we require a standardized test of English language proficiency unless you completed a bachelor's or master's degree at an accredited institution where English is the language of instruction, or you have worked full-time for two years in the United States or other native English-speaking environment.
[Learn more on our FAQ page](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/international-graduate-students/)
Is an interview required for admission? 
Interviews are rarely required for admission to the Graduate Certificate in Leading People & Organizations program. However, the Admissions team may request one if necessary.
Are there other application requirements for international students? 
The D'Amore-McKim School of Business is a vibrant, diverse community of learners from all over the world. Our [international graduate students page](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/international-graduate-students/) provides more information about applying and the benefits of choosing D'Amore-McKim.
Is the GMAT or GRE required for admission? 
Graduate certificate programs do not require GRE or GMAT scores for admissions.
