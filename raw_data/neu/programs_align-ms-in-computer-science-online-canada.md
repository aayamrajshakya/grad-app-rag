## Career prospects and top employers
Canada’s tech sector is expanding across artificial intelligence, cloud, gaming, visual effects, and clean tech. There’s a huge demand for highly skilled computer scientists. More than half of Canadian IT departments are grappling with skills gaps in AI, ML, and automation. Meanwhile, AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity skills are driving 28–35% salary increases in Canadian tech jobs (Robert Half, Canada Tech and IT Salary Trends, 2025). You can learn these in-demand skills in the Align program.
Align graduates move into roles like software engineer, full-stack developer, data scientist, and cloud/security engineer. Khoury College aligns coursework with industry needs and connects students to a network of 800+ employer partners across North America, including Canadian organizations such as Amazon, Microsoft Canada Inc., TELUS, SAP, EA, RBC, Clio, and more. Our students bring a unique background, and Align enables them to use it to make a real-world impact.

## Flexibility and connections that work for you
Keep work and life on track. Courses are online and designed for busy professionals. Residencies are short and purposeful, focused on collaboration, practical tools, and industry-relevant projects. Faculty at Khoury College are experienced educators who love teaching computer science to beginners and connecting your learning to opportunities.

## Learning outcomes
The Align MS in Computer Science Online – Canada will equip you with technical knowledge and career preparation. As a graduate, you will:
  * Exhibit proficiency in the design, implementation, and testing of software
  * Demonstrate skills and experience working in small teams
  * Apply algorithmic and theoretical computer-science principles
  * Demonstrate the ability to learn and develop competencies in specialized or emerging computer science fields
