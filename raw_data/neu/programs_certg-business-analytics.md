Program overview 
##  Navigate the future of business 
In today’s competitive business landscape, data analytics is key to strategic decision-making. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics equips you with essential techniques for collecting, analyzing, and leveraging data to guide business strategy.
Through four or five master’s-level courses, you’ll gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools like Python, R, SQL, and Tableau. You’ll also study real-world cases alongside experienced practitioners and thought leaders in analytics.
Whether you choose to focus on data visualization, machine learning, or data wrangling, the program is designed to provide a flexible, tailored experience. Plus, with the opportunity to apply your credits toward a master’s degree, you’ll lay the groundwork for continued growth and advancement.

Career outlook 
As Big Data and artificial intelligence transform global business, learning new data skills-and refreshing existing ones-will be critical to advancing your career. Earning your Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics equips you with the specialized, up-to-the-moment knowledge that companies value and demonstrates your commitment to professional development. Your new background in analytics can lead you toward a role as an analyst, specialist, or manager, depending on your work experience.
Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your courses for the Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics will center around case studies and applied project work, allowing you to learn from the experience of real companies and hone your skills in a team setting. You can also strengthen your data science knowledge outside the classroom through unique applied opportunities:

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)
