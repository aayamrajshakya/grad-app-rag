## Biography
Greg spent the majority of his career in executive leadership, general management, and marketing roles which he leverages extensively in the classroom. His experience has been primarily in the technology sector (semiconductors, software, and cybersecurity) including 14 years at Kronos, one of Massachusettsâ€™s most well-respected companies.
Ten years ago Greg transferred his experience and skills to the nonprofit sector through Board leadership positions and obtaining an MS in Nonprofit Management. Today he is actively involved as a nonprofit marketing and strategy consultant with Curtis Strategy and the North Shore nonprofit ecosystem as current Board Chair for Amirah Inc., and Vice Chair at Pathways for Children. Greg also has an MBA from Endicott College and a BS in Business Administration from California State University.
