# Professor Chris Barney’s Innovative Pattern Language
[View GDC Video](https://gdcvault.com/play/1027874/Game-Design)
Game Science and Design Professor Chris Barney’s acclaimed book, “Pattern Language for Game Design,” extends the visionary ideas of architect Christopher Alexander, offering an authoritative guide to creating Design Patterns that empower designers to create meaningful, immersive experiences. His work isn’t just instructional—it transforms how designers think about and approach game creation, providing invaluable insights for GSND students, educators, and industry professionals alike. Professor Barney’s pattern language has reached an international audience, with him sharing his expertise at leading conferences such as GDC, DevCom, ECGC, and DiGRA. The Library of Design Patterns, curated by Barney, has grown into the world’s largest collection, boasting over 3,000 patterns.
