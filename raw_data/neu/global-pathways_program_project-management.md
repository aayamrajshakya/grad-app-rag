# Project Management
[« Return to Programs](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/programs/)
The Master of Science in Project Management is designed to provide you with the practical skills and theoretical concepts you need to lead complex projects.
If you want to earn your master’s in Project Management at Northeastern, Global Pathways can help you succeed through rigorous language training, cultural activities, and support.
[Apply Now »](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/)
