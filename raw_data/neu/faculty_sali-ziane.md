#  SaliZiane
Teaching Professor of French; Academic Coordinator of the French Program 
Sali Ziane is a Teaching Professor and the Coordinator of the French Program at the WLC. Her thesis focuses on the study of medical terminology. Her areas of teaching are French Language, Linguistics, and Culture. She has taught in France, North Africa, and the United States. Dr. Ziane has received many certificates in France and England in both Linguistics and Didactics. She has extensive experience teaching French at all levels as well as expertise in translation.
