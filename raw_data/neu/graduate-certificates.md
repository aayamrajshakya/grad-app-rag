**Learn it today, use it tomorrow**
For working professionals in the program, these flexible, focused certificates offer education with immediate impact. Take the lessons you learn in the classroom and apply them to your career the very next day. Your graduate certificate will give you the tools and skills you need to overcome current and future business challenges.
**Courses tailored to your goals**
Choose from a broad range of electives to supplement your required courses and build the knowledge and skills you need to position yourself for professional growth. Fill in gaps in your business acumen or strengthen key areas to expand your career potential.
**Build toward something more**
When you are ready to pursue a graduate degree at Northeastern University, the credits you earn during your graduate certificate program may be applied to eligible D'Amore-McKim master's programs, including our Part-Time or Online MBA. This is a valuable opportunity to turn your career-enhancing graduate certificate into a more advanced professional credential.
****Optional Practical Training (OPT)****
Nine of our graduate certificates are compliant with F-1 visa regulations. For international students studying full-time on one of our U.S. campuses, you may become eligible to apply for Optional Practical Training (OPT), allowing you to apply to work for up to 12 months in the U.S.

Tuition Cost and Financial Aid 
### Tuition
Northeastern Student Financial Services publishes the [tuition rates](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/billing-payments/tuition-and-fees/) for graduate programs. Under the “Graduate Programs” section, the cost per credit appears next to “D'Amore-McKim School of Business Programs.”
### Financial Aid
Northeastern's Student Financial Services team will guide you through options to help you finance your graduate education. You can learn more about [graduate financial aid options](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/applying-for-aid/graduate/) on their website.
Once you become a student, your financial aid advisor will work with you to answer your questions, and provide valuable insight as you navigate the financial commitments of your program.
### Scholarships
D'Amore-McKim and Northeastern University offer a variety of [scholarships](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/tuition-aid/). Campus-based applicants will automatically receive consideration for a merit scholarship. You may also qualify for funding support as an [alum](https://www.northeastern.edu/graduate/double-husky-scholarship/), [U.S. servicemember](https://military.northeastern.edu/fund-your-education/), or [veteran](https://military.northeastern.edu/fund-your-education/).
### Application Fee Waivers
