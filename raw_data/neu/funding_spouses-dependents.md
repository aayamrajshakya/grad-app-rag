# Spouses and Dependents
  * **The Post 9/11 GI Bill® (Ch. 33)**


[The Post 9/11 GI Bill](https://www.va.gov/education/about-gi-bill-benefits/post-9-11/)® 
If you served 36 months** on active duty, you could be entitled to funding for:
Tuition and fees
  * Up to $28,937.09 for tuition and fees (this amount is set annually by the VA)
  * Students at the 100% level are automatically enrolled in the Yellow Ribbon Program which covers any additional tuition and fee charges to go beyond the annual amount set by the VA.


Books & Supplies
  * Up to $1,000.00/ year paid out at $41.67 per credit.


Monthly Housing Allowance
  * Rates vary by campus and full time status
  * *Only available to members of the Reserves and National Guard****** All payments are prorated based on eligibility % More information can be found [here](https://www.va.gov/education/benefit-rates/post-9-11-gi-bill-rates/)


[Learn more about the Post 9/11 GI Bill®](https://www.va.gov/education/about-gi-bill-benefits/post-9-11/)

  * ******Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry Scholarship******


Spouses and children of servicemembers who passed away while serving may be eligible for the[ Fry Scholarship](https://www.va.gov/family-and-caregiver-benefits/education-and-careers/fry-scholarship/). Recipients of the Fry Scholarship are entitled to the same benefits available under the Post 9/11 GI Bill® at the 100% level, including Yellow Ribbon.
[Explore the GySgt Fry Scholarship](https://www.va.gov/family-and-caregiver-benefits/education-and-careers/fry-scholarship/)

  * ******Survivors’ and Dependents’ Educational Assistance DEA (CH 35)******


[DEA](https://northeastern-my.sharepoint.com/personal/mjspahn_northeastern_edu/Documents/Desktop/\(https:/www.va.gov/family-and-caregiver-benefits/education-and-careers/dependents-education-assistance/\)) is an educational assistance program available to children and spouses of veterans that are permanently and totally disabled, have passed away due to service-connected disability, or are captured/missing in action. This program pays a monthly stipend based on the number of courses you are taking directly to you. The [current](https://www.va.gov/family-and-caregiver-benefits/education-and-careers/dependents-education-assistance/rates/#2024-to-2025-chapter-35-benefi) maximum amount for a full-time student is $1,535.00/mo.
