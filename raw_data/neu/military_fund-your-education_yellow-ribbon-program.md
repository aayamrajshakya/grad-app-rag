# Strengthening Skills. Forging Futures.
Build on your military experience and open new career pathways with an experience-powered degree from Northeastern University.
[Get Connected](https://military.northeastern.edu/contact-us/)
Northeastern University is deeply committed to supporting military veterans and servicemembers with powerful educational experiences in every part of the world.
### Experiential learning opportunities ideal for military students
Northeastern University is a recognized global leader in experiential learning, with a renowned co-op program that allows students to gain invaluable professional experience and connect with industry-leading companies in their field of interest.
[Explore Programs](https://military.northeastern.edu/find-your-program/)
## Military-specific financial aid opportunities
Northeastern is proud to bring military students closer to their degree through several financial aid mechanisms, including Tuition Assistance (TA) matching, the Yellow Ribbon Program, and military-specific tuition rates for select programs.
