#  HarlowRobinson
Matthews Distinguished University Professor Emeritus of History 
Dr. Harlow Robinson is an author, lecturer and Matthews Distinguished University Professor of History, Emeritus, at Northeastern University. His books include Russians in Hollywood, Hollywood’s Russians;Sergei Prokofiev: A Biography; Selected Letters of Sergei Prokofiev;The Last Impresario: The Life, Times and Legacy of Sol Hurok;Lewis Milestone: Life and Films.The New York Times, Boston Globe, Los Angeles Times, The Nation, Opera News, Musical America, Cineaste, San Francisco Chronicle, Stagebill
