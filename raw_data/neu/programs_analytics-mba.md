## This concentration is ideal for
Business students who want to use data to drive decision-making
Emerging leaders interested in jobs like business intelligence manager or senior data analyst
Students looking to build an advanced toolset to use data to form actionable insights
## Turn data into actionable insights
In today's business world, data drives almost every decision. Employers need leaders who can turn data into meaningful information and act on insights. By developing expertise in analytics, you'll immediately add value to your workplace and unlock opportunities to advance your career. 
You'll learn from experienced faculty and collaborate with fellow students, exploring real-world applications of advanced data mining, visual dashboards, and AI and machine learning. Plus, you'll get hands-on experience with tools like Python, R, SQL, and Tableau.
