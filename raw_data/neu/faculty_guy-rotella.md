#  GuyRotella
Professor Emeritus of English 
Guy Rotella retired from the English department at the end of the 2011-2012 academic year. Professor Rotella began his Northeastern career in 1974. A specialist in twentieth-century American poetry, he has written or edited eight books, including CastingsAmerican LiteratureContemporary LiteratureLiterary Imagination
Selected Publications
  * Castings: Monuments and Monumentality in Poems by Elizabeth Bishop, Robert Lowell, James Merrill, Derek Walcott, and Seamus Heaney
