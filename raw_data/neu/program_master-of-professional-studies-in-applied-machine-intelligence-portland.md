### **Overview**
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are playing an increasingly important role in the private and public sectors worldwide. To prepare the next generation of leaders and innovators, Northeastern University is offering the MPS degree in Applied Machine Intelligence. 
With the support of industry representatives, the targeted curriculum of this STEM-degree program was created by faculty leaders who have a strong commitment to an industry validation process. By soliciting perspectives and opinions from business professionals, the coursework has been aligned to meet industry needs for artificial intelligence, machine learning, data management, data analysis, and data visualization. 
To further strengthen your marketability as an AI professional - you will also be able to specialize your degree even further by choosing from one of several concentrations in today’s most in-demand areas of expertise. 
Following Northeastern’s traditional emphasis on real-world learning, our AMI graduate program offers you many opportunities to problem-solve and collaborate on real-world projects with enterprise partners such as Google, AWS, Deloitte, Samsung, Dell, Uber, and more – demonstrating to employers you have the technical and project management skills to succeed. 
### **Program Objectives**
  * Build technological literacy by evaluating and integrating tools, techniques and conceptual skills necessary for managing complex systems.


  * Utilize data literacy to show the significance of digital and global transformation to create business strategies, make data-informed decisions, drive innovation and create competitive advantage.


  * Develop the human literacy needed for lifelong learning, communication, design and innovation essential for guiding AI systems, 


  * Be able to assess and develop an ethical position, critical thinking and cultural agility as it relates to AI.


### Unique Features
  * Graduate with a portfolio of work samples that demonstrate your range and depth of competencies and skills with machine learning and artificial intelligence. 
  * Modular curriculum allows students to customize their classes, projects, and other experiential learning opportunities based on their unique interests.
  * Select a degree concentration in these fast-growing areas: pharmaceuticals/healthcare, finance, human resources, or business ventures.
