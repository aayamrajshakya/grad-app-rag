## Guide troubled companies back to financial health 
Turning around a troubled company takes a unique set of skills. Professionals who can develop sound financial strategies, tackle challenges with innovative solutions, and create lasting business value can stand out as renewal specialists—an important role in today's fast-paced business world. 
Our Corporate Renewal concentration will equip you with the diverse knowledge you need to reinvent companies from the ground up. You'll gain the skills to analyze distressed companies, develop recovery plans, and implement changes that restore financial health and operational efficiency. 
This concentration is all about choice. Personalize your experience by selecting courses that align with your interests. You can explore essential topics like negotiations, intellectual property, business law, product development, and innovation as you build critical skills that can help you pursue consultant roles or advance into senior management positions.
