#  FiStewart-Taylor
Postdoctoral Teaching Associate, Writing Program 
Fi Stewart-Taylor received a PhD in English from the University of Florida in 2024. They study multimodal, self published, and small press texts, particularly (web)comics and zines. Their recent work considers the relationship between mediums, publishing formats, and community formation.
