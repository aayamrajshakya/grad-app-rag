Program overview 
##  Harness data for decision-making 
The Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics Engineering equips students with essential tools and techniques to extract meaningful information from large datasets, supporting effective decision-making. This four-course program covers key concepts in data storage, retrieval, visualization, and predictive analytics.
You’ll also gain hands-on experience in database design, data pre- and post-processing, data mining, and quantitative analysis to create impactful infographics and uncover valuable insights.
Note: This program is not available for students enrolled in the MS in Data Analytics.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Human Factors](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-human-factors/master-of-science-in-human-factors/)
  * [Master of Science in Robotics](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-robotics/master-of-science-in-robotics/)
  * [Master of Science in Industrial Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msie-industrial-engineering/master-of-science-in-industrial-engineering/)
  * [Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msme-mechanical-engineering/master-of-science-in-mechanical-engineering/)
