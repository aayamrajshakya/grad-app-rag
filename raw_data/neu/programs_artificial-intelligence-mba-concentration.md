## Harness advanced technology to create a competitive edge 
As intelligent machines and AI are redefining what's possible for businesses, employers are seeking leaders with the technical skills and expertise to thrive at the forefront of the digital world. Our MBA x Artificial Intelligence concentration offers an innovative curriculum in partnership with [Northeastern's Khoury College of Computer Sciences](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/). Guided by Khoury's award-winning professors, you'll explore topics like heuristic search, game trees, natural language processing, and machine learning. 
If you have a degree in computer science, you can dive right into the MBA x AI coursework. If your degree is in another field, you'll start with 16 credits of bridge courses taught by Khoury faculty to prepare you for this advanced computer science coursework.
