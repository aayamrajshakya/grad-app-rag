Program overview 
##  Training Communication Leaders 
This certificate trains students to leverage strong communication skills across various groups in a particular environment. Students will learn about effective communications in areas ranging from grassroots outreach to the communications at large public entities like global corporations. With effective skills in cross-cultural communication, professionals can seek opportunities in consultancy, governmental bodies, nonprofit organizations, public relations, sales, social service, and tourism. There are also a vast range of roles within corporate departments, such as communications, customer service, development, human resources, marketing, and sales.

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
This unique certificate allows students to learn traditional communications strategies while increasing their personal cross-cultural awareness.
Address bias and organizational strategy 
Analyze personal cross-cultural awareness and implicit bias, in addition to interpreting organizational cross-cultural communication strategy to develop effective communication processes and activities.
Evaluate audiences 
Evaluate communication audiences from a holistic perspective, thereby constructing effective verbal and nonverbal interactions based on cross-cultural consumption.
Develop more effective strategies 
Formulate enlightened cross-cultural communication and inclusive diversity strategies, processes, and policies.
Gain advanced critical-thinking skills 
Apply critical-thinking skills through research, case analysis, role plays, and experiential learning while employing cross-cultural communication strategies.
