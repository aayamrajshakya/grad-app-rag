Program overview 
##  Shape your economic future 
The Graduate Certificate in Engineering Economic Decision Making equips you with the skills to build decision-making models and make data-driven, financial, and economic-based decisions in engineering contexts.
This four-course program focuses on key aspects such as decision-making models, risk assessment, and handling uncertainty in engineering operations and projects. You’ll gain specialized knowledge in engineering economics, preparing you to tackle real-world challenges in a management setting.

Program highlights 
  * Complete four courses focused on engineering economic decision-making.
  * Learn to create decision-making models that address risk, uncertainty, and financial analysis.
  * Boost your career: Graduate certificate holders earn an average 25% higher salary, according to the Washington Post.
  * Use this certificate as a gateway to further education, with the option to apply credits toward a master’s degree.


Career outlook 
With specialized knowledge in engineering economic decision-making, you can advance or transition your career in engineering operations. The skills gained through this certificate prepare you for roles that require strategic, data-driven financial decisions, enhancing your value in various engineering fields.
Experiential learning highlights 
Northeastern’s experiential learning model integrates academics with hands-on experience. Through the cooperative education program (co-op), graduate students gain professional industry exposure by working four to eight months in organizations ranging from large companies to startups. The program offers support through resumé building, interview preparation, and professional development to ensure success in the co-op experience.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Masters of Science in Advanced and Intelligent Manufacturing](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-advanced-intelligent-mfg/master-of-science-in-advanced-and-intelligent-manufacturing/)
