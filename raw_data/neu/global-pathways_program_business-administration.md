# Business Administration
[« Return to Programs](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/programs/)
Northeastern’s Full-Time MBA program provides the interdisciplinary and global experience you need to prepare for the future work, and equips you with the entrepreneurial and creative mindset to solve business problems. Your challenging curriculum will integrate foundational business knowledge with a deep understanding of the future of technological advancement, and you’ll have the opportunity to build rich perspective through the study of in-demand concentrations and a corporate residency.
If you want to earn your MBA at Northeastern, Global Pathways can help you succeed through rigorous language training, cultural activities, and support.
