# Qianqian Zhang-Wu is selected for Hong Liu China Studies Research Award
## People in this story
  * [ Qianqian Zhang-Wu ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/faculty/qianqian-zhang-wu/)


Assistant Teaching Professor of Multilingual Writing and Multilingual Writing Coordinator Qianqian Zhang-Wu has been awarded a grant from the Northeastern Asian Studies program to support research on the academic writing experiences of Chinese international undergraduate students at Northeastern. Professor Zhang-Wu is one of two recipients of this [competitive award](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/internationalcenter/area-studies/asia/asian-studies-research-award/), which is awarded annually to support research and related activities undertaken by Northeastern University faculty and/or PhD students.
Congratulations, Professor Zhang-Wu!
