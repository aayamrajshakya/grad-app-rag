Program overview 
##  Propel your career in a growing field 
This program prepares students to design and deploy secure, scalable, and intelligent cyber-physical systems that drive innovation across industries. With a curriculum spanning embedded systems, sensor and actuation networks, robotics, and advanced Internet of Things (IoT) protocols, learners gain practical, hands-on experience in building responsive and reliable smart devices. Courses also cover wireless networking with cutting-edge 5G and emerging 6G technologies, emphasizing scalability, interoperability, and cybersecurity. On the application side, students explore AI, machine learning, and big data analytics to develop intelligent systems capable of transforming domains such as smart cities, healthcare, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation.

Industry-aligned courses 
This program positions itself at the forefront of technological innovation, preparing students to lead in a world where intelligent systems seamlessly integrate with the physical environment. Rooted in a curriculum that blends embedded systems, IoT protocols, wireless networking, AI, and data analytics, the program empowers graduates to design and deploy smart solutions across industries.
Career outlook 
Graduates of the program are highly sought after by industry, and their career paths primarily lead to practical applications across a wide range of engineering and technology domains.
Leveraging their expertise in cyber-physical systems, IoT, and intelligent applications, alumni often pursue roles such as IoT embedded software engineers, data scientists, systems architects, and robotics engineers. Many also transition into cross-functional positions like sales engineers, technical consultants, and product managers, where their deep technical knowledge and strategic thinking drive innovation and business growth.
Learning outcomes 
  * Identify, formulate, and solve complex engineering problems.
  * Explain and apply engineering design principles, as appropriate to the program’s educational objectives.
  * Produce solutions that meet specified end-user needs with consideration of economic factors, and, where relevant, factors related to public policy, safety, and the environment.
  * Acquire strong working knowledge of the computer languages required to build IoT applications, both on IoT devices and on the network/server side.
  * Gain comprehensive understanding of the range of wireless and application protocols that support different IoT application domains.
  * Work in a team environment to design and develop a complete IoT application using both high-level and low-level computer languages and a range of software configuration tools.
