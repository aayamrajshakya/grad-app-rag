### Customize your STEM MBA for impact
Earning your MBA at D'Amore-McKim means mastering both the human and technical sides of leadership. In this one-year, STEM-designated program, you'll complete a rigorous core curriculum in analytics, innovation, and business strategy—and shape your learning through a wide range of elective courses aligned with your career goals. The result: a career-ready, tech-fluent MBA designed for the pace of modern business.

## Applicant FAQs
How do I apply and what will I need? 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You'll need to submit:
  * Application form 
  * A personal statement (<=500 words) 
  * Resumé 
  * Two letters of recommendation 
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended 
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only) 
  * GMAT/GRE test scores (GMAT preferred; review our [admissions policies](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdamore-mckim.northeastern.edu%2Fadmissions%2Ffaq%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cr.higgins%40northeastern.edu%7Cd938930e20764198184508dc49d4b7cf%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C638466425164373955%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=OFb9AoXEgoIATlpXPDPL7Kgp%2BKC469SMq7mXORb6SPM%3D&reserved=0)) 
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (for international students only, find more information and waiver eligibility in our [admissions policies](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdamore-mckim.northeastern.edu%2Fadmissions%2Ffaq%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cr.higgins%40northeastern.edu%7Cd938930e20764198184508dc49d4b7cf%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C638466425164384564%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=96cZr3oJe%2BjWEqKOibAeiFBt2VFbQvl%2BmFpIkwJF%2F00%3D&reserved=0))
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Are there other application requirements for international students? 
Yes. International students must meet additional requirements, including proof of English language proficiency, a valid passport, and English translations of academic transcripts. Our [international graduate students page](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/international-graduate-students/) provides more information about applying and the benefits of choosing D'Amore-McKim. 
Are there opportunities for international students to gain work experience in the United States? 
Yes. International students in F-1 immigration status are eligible for a corporate residency using [Curricular Practical Training](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/employment/off-campus-employment/f-1-curricular-practical-training/) authorization. When you complete your program, you may become eligible to apply for [Optional Practical Training](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/employment/off-campus-employment/applying-for-post-completion-opt-f-1/) (OPT), which, if granted, allows you to work in the United States for up to 12 months. We encourage you to work closely with your career advisor and an advisor in the Office of Global Services.
Is the GMAT or GRE required for admission? 
Yes, GMAT is preferred.
Can non-business majors earn an MBA? 
You don't need an undergraduate business degree to pursue a Master of Business Administration (MBA) or advanced business degrees like our [MS in Accounting](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-accounting/) (MSA). While having one can strengthen your application, most MBA programs welcome applicants from diverse academic backgrounds.
[Learn more about these programs for non-business majors](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/resources/can-non-business-majors-earn-an-mba-or-ms-in-accounting/)
