## Creating tomorrow’s tech leaders
Seattle has long been a leading technology center. Home to tech giants like Microsoft, Amazon, and Expedia, the city is constantly buzzing with new digital developments and innovations. It’s also the headquarters of major global corporations in other industries, from Starbucks to Boeing. Northeastern students can easily connect with potential employers at these companies and more, helping them advance their careers alongside renowned organizations that are shaping how the world does business.
##  What makes Seattle unique 
Seattle has a distinctly modern, energetic feel, and students can immerse themselves in a range of dynamic learning opportunities.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://seattle.northeastern.edu/)


Strong co-op placements 
With 1,088 co-op and internship placements to date, our Seattle campus is the perfect launchpad for students seeking to gain deep experience in their industries.
Centrally located 
Our campus sits in the vibrant South Lake Union neighborhood, right next to Amazon’s headquarters, and continues to expand.
Community partnerships 
Northeastern maintains partnerships with more than 20 community organizations and is committed to remaining involved with the neighborhood’s well-being.
Groundbreaking research 
Faculty and students conduct research in areas of emerging tech, such as artificial intelligence, in labs outfitted with the latest equipment.
