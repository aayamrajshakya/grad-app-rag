## Deliver business value through sustainable practices 
In today's world, consumers care more than ever about the environmental, social, and economic impacts of business practices. Organizations need leaders who can make smart, strategic business decisions that not only create value but also reduce the organization's environmental footprint, champion social justice, and ensure transparent governance. 
Our Sustainability and Business concentration equips you with the knowledge and skills to do just that. You'll dive into the essentials of sustainable business, covering topics like global competition, public policy, social entrepreneurship, and sustainable supply chain management. This concentration empowers you to make a meaningful impact on the world while driving business success.
