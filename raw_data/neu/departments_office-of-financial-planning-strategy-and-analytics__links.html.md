# Financial Planning, Strategy, and Analytics Office
The Office of Financial Planning, Strategy, and Analytics uses data and analytics to inform day-to-day decision-making, plan for the future, and identify new efficiencies across the university. We apply advanced analytics to convert financial, treasury, human resources, and facilities data into actionable strategic insights.
Looking for support? Visit our knowledge and service portal, [AskFin Assist](https://service.northeastern.edu/finance).
