#  Working After Graduation 
Discover the post-graduation employment options available to F-1 and J-1 international students — options that allow students to gain practical work experience related to their program of study. 
##  F-1 Post-Completion Optional Practical Training 
F-1 students may apply to United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) for Post-Completion Optional Practical Training (Post-OPT) work authorization, which helps them apply their classroom learning to the real-world.

##  F-1 STEM EXTENSIONS FOR POST-COMPLETION OPTIONAL PRACTICAL TRAINING 
F-1 students who receive Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) degrees and meet certain requirements are eligible to apply for a 24-month extension of their Post-Completion Optional Practical Training (OPT).
Not sure if you’re eligible for STEM OPT? Review the [Applying for STEM OPT page](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/employment/working-after-graduation/24-stem-opt/) and check the [STEM-designated CIP code list](https://studyinthestates.dhs.gov/stem-opt-hub). If you have additional questions please [contact OGS](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/student-support/resources/contact/).
