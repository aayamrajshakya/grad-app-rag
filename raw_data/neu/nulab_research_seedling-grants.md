# Seedling, Community Collaboration, and Travel Grants
A visualization for the [Black Architects Archive](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/black-architects-archive/), a project led by NULab core faculty member Jay Cephas, assistant professor of Architecture and Urbanism.
Since 2014, the NULab has offered funding for seedling grants, travel, and co-sponsorship of events related to computational social science and digital humanities.
