Program overview 
##  Advance your biotech career 
The Graduate Certificate in Molecular Biotechnology at Northeastern responds to the growing need for expertise in molecular biology and protein analysis. This program covers the principles and practices of state-of-the-art molecular techniques, providing a deeper understanding of this rapidly evolving field.
Designed to be flexible, the certificate includes four or five courses that fit into your schedule and can be completed on-ground in Boston. The credits earned may serve as a gateway to the MS in Biotechnology program, allowing a seamless transition if you choose to further your education.
The Graduate Certificate in Molecular Biotechnology at Northeastern responds to the growing need for expertise in molecular biology and protein analysis. This program covers the principles and practices of state-of-the-art molecular techniques, providing a deeper understanding of this rapidly evolving field.
Designed to be flexible, the certificate includes four or five courses that fit into your schedule and can be completed on-ground in Boston. The credits earned may serve as a gateway to the MS in Biotechnology program, allowing a seamless transition if you choose to further your education.
Program highlights 
  * Choose from four or five courses, totaling 12 credits, for a tailored learning experience.
  * Stack your certificate toward the MS in Biotechnology, transferring all earned credits if accepted.
  * Engage in hands-on lab courses conducted on the Boston campus.
  * Study with industry-aligned faculty bringing real-world insights to the classroom.
  * Access both on-campus and online course options for added flexibility.


Career potential 
The multi-billion-dollar biotech industry is booming, offering a range of career paths in pharmaceuticals, biotech startups, and more. A background in molecular biotechnology positions you for innovation in healthcare and environmental sectors, where growth and new opportunities continue to expand.
Experiential learning 
Northeastern’s experiential learning model integrates academics with professional practice. In-class case studies and collaborative research offer firsthand exposure to the biotech field’s challenges. You’ll have opportunities to engage in practical lab work, guided by faculty experienced in tackling health, security, and sustainability issues.

Admissions requirements 
  * Online application
  * Unofficial transcripts (official transcripts required upon acceptance of admissions)
    * The Foreign Credential Evaluation (FCE) is a required assessment of all transcripts and documents from non-U.S. accredited post-secondary education institutions. (Review the [FCE requirements](https://northeastern.my.site.com/GraduateAdmissionsFAQ/s/article/What-documents-are-required-for-a-credential-evaluation) by country.)
  * GPA 3.0 or higher
  * Personal statement
  * English language proficiency:
    * Degree earned or in progress at a U.S. institution
    * Degree earned or in progress at an institution where English is the only medium of instruction
    * Official exam scores from either the TOEFL iBT (institution code is 3682), IELTS, PTE exam, or Duolingo English Test. Scores are valid for 2 years from the test date.
