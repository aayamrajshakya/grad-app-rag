## Lead supply chain strategy, optimize logistics, capitalize on opportunities
As advancing technology and globalization have transformed supply chain management, organizations need leaders with modern skills to manage logistics, drive innovation, and establish market differentiation. 
Our Operations and Supply Chain Management concentration—ranked one of the top 25 supply chain graduate programs in North America by Gartner, Inc.—will prepare you with the skills and knowledge you need for careers in the field. In this program, you'll learn from experienced faculty and visiting experts as you explore topics like supply chain strategy, logistics and transportation, sourcing, analytics, and more—all from the perspective of digital and global commerce.
