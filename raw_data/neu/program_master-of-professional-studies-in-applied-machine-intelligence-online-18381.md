Program overview 
##  Become a leader in AI 
The flexible, online MPS in Applied AI program will prepare you to thoughtfully navigate the changing AI landscape and its challenges.
The online program focuses on building and implementing AI and machine learning technologies, with an emphasis on collaborative learning, interdisciplinary innovation, and ethics. You’ll gain functional government and industry expertise, challenge your creativity, and learn to lead through real-world experiences, all on your schedule.
This comprehensive approach, along with the opportunity to specialize your learning through concentrations and electives, ensures that you are equipped with the skills to transform your workplace through AI.

Program concentrations 
Conversational AI and Chatbots 
Understand how linguistics, tech, and design impact conversational AI and enhance user experiences and business operations. Expertise here opens diverse career avenues, from global enterprises to local businesses.
Machine Learning for Cybersecurity
Machine learning (ML) enhances cybersecurity by detecting threats, automating responses, scaling operations, spotting sophisticated threats in vast datasets, and predicting future risks. As cyber threats evolve, ML’s role in defense and job relevance grows, offering novel research opportunities and applications.
AI for 3D Imaging 
Understand how AI can offer improved accuracy, faster workflows, data augmentation, real-time analysis, image segmentation, noise reduction, and cost efficiency in a variety of industries, from healthcare to game design.

Special admissions information 
For the 2025–2026 academic year, MPS Applied AI and MPS Applied AI Connect are being offered as a collaboration between the College of Professional Studies (CPS) and the Office of the Provost. During this period, students will follow Northeastern University’s [Graduate (GR) Academic Calendar](https://registrar.northeastern.edu/article/academic-calendar/) and be governed by the academic policies and procedures documented in the university’s [Graduate Catalog.](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/academic-policies-procedures/) CPS will continue to provide each student with full academic and student support services (e.g., academic advising services, co-op and career advising services, International Tutoring Center, etc.).
Beginning in the 2026–2027 academic year, students enrolled in MPS Applied AI and MPS Applied AI Connect will transition to the College of Professional Studies, and their official college designation will be updated accordingly in the academic system of record. At that time, students will be governed by CPS’ academic policies and procedures within the university’s [Graduate Catalog](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/professional-studies/).

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
In this program, you’ll build a comprehensive foundation in machine learning and AI concepts, methodologies, and applications and be prepared for careers as a developer, engineer, or data scientist.
Analytical competencies 
Evaluate the efficacy of and employ advanced data analysis methods, data management technologies, and modern AI analytical techniques for public and private sector challenges.
Human-focused solutions 
Integrate the theory and practice of human-computer interaction and usability to analyze and design solutions to key management and ethical issues throughout the data lifecycle.
Real-world applications 
Apply strategic and effective analysis, problem-solving, decision-making, communication, and leadership skills to implement ML and AI solutions in real-world situations.
Communication skills 
Communicate complex quantitative information to different audiences using exploratory data visualization, dashboard design, scorecard design, and spatial data representation.
