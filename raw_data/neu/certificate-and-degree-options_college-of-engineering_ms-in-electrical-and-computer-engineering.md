## Electrical and Computer Engineering Coursework
Students pursuing an [MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/electrical-computer/#programstext) must complete 8 semester-hours of fundamental coursework, 8 semester-hours following the coursework option for their chosen concentration, and 8 semester-hours of elective courses either from concentration or breadth courses.
The above plans of study are for current students only. These plans are samples only- actual curriculum sequences may deviate from these samples. Programs of study are the responsibility of the student. The University Catalog provides a listing of degree requirements for each program, and the DARS system provides a degree audit utility for students.
