# Teaching Resources
A presentation on [Our Marathon](https://marathon.library.northeastern.edu/) for an Advanced Writing in the Disciplines course
These are the latest versions of the handouts and slides the DITI offers students during in-class visits, now available for professors and educators to use in their own classes. All of the handouts and slides are in non-editable Google Docs, but you can copy (File, Make a Copy) or download (File, Download) them to make edits tailored to your course.
