Program overview 
##  Build business accumen 
In today’s digital and global business landscape, skilled professionals who can adapt and lead are in high demand. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Business Administration equips you with the essential business skills to navigate this evolving ecosystem.
In just four to six master’s-level courses, you’ll learn from renowned faculty, study real-world cases, and gain a well-rounded foundation in accounting, marketing, management, marketing, analysis, and entrepreneurship.
Personalize your curriculum by choosing from three tailored options: the Part-Time MBA Path, Build-Your-Own Curriculum, or the 8-month International Student Cohort. Throughout your program, Northeastern’s unique emphasis on experience will enrich your perspective and build your confidence.

Program highlights 
  * Choose from three tailored curriculum options: Part-Time MBA Path, Build-Your-Own Curriculum, or 8-month International Student Cohort
  * Learn through applied project work and real-world case studies
  * Access expert career guidance from the D’Amore-McKim Graduate Career Center
  * Explore experiential opportunities like DATA Club or the 360 Huntington Fund
  * Lay the groundwork for a future MBA—you may be able to apply completed credits toward an eligible master’s degree at Northeastern
  * Study in person on our beautiful campus in Boston—a global business hub with a diverse and vibrant culture
  * Qualify to apply for Optional Practical Training (OPT) as an international student through the eight-month full-time curriculum option


Career outlook 
In a global and digital business world, learning new business skills—and refreshing existing ones—will be critical to advancing your career. Earning your Graduate Certificate in Business Administration equips you with the specialized, up-to-the-moment knowledge that companies value and demonstrates your commitment to professional development. Your new skill set can serve as a stepping stone to a more significant role, depending on your goals and work experience.

Experience is at the core of Northeastern’s education model. Courses in the Graduate Certificate in Business Administration focus on case studies and applied project work, allowing you to learn from top companies. Enhance your business knowledge through:

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)


Contact an enrollment counselor to learn more about eligibility requirements.
