# Resources for Prospective Master’s Students
A rigorous, research-driven degree
At Khoury College of Computer Sciences, our mission — computer science for everyone — influences everything we do. For our master’s programs, that means combining academic rigor with research excellence, collaborating across disciplines and around the globe, and bringing together diverse talent to meet the demands of industry and society. That’s how we create the problem solvers of today, the thought leaders of tomorrow, and the engaged experts ready to tackle whatever challenge comes next. Let’s get started.

## Support at every step
Khoury College is proud to have a culture based on collaboration, community, and respect. With the backing of academic advisors, co-op coordinators, teaching assistants, and numerous other support staff, Khoury students can plan and pursue their optimal path. As well, Northeastern University offers undergraduates a wide range of support services including WeCare (support for students experiencing unexpected changes), health and counseling services, mental health support, and much more.

## No tech experience? No problem
Our Align Master’s Program provides a direct path to a master’s degree for non-CS majors. Regardless of your background or bachelor’s degree, you’ll gain the knowledge needed for a new career in tech — in just 2.5 years.
[Explore the Align program](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/align-programs/)
## Fast-track your future with a graduate certificate
Acquire a strong foundation in a growing field to boost your career or get ready for a master’s degree — in as few as 16 semester hours. Graduate certificate programs include:
  * Inclusive Computer Science Education graduate certificate
  * Cybersecurity Graduate Certificate


  * Data Analytics Graduate Certificate
  * Health Informatics Management and Exchange graduate certificate


[Apply to a graduate certificate program](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/apply/how-to-apply-graduate-certificate/)
## Graduate co-ops
Khoury College’s graduate co-op program is built to enrich your master’s-level coursework and elevate your technical expertise through hands-on learning. Combining invaluable networking opportunities and the chance to apply newly acquired skills in an industry setting, co-ops will give you significant professional experience – and a distinct competitive for post-graduate job opportunities. Along the way, you’ll further develop the knowledge, perspective, and confidence you need to take your career to the next level. 
[Explore graduate co-ops](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/graduate-co-ops/)
## Master’s clubs
Khoury College is home to numerous graduate student clubs and organizations that offer students the chance to bond over shared interests, test their know-how, and much more — all while providing a sense of place and belonging.
[Explore graduate clubs](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/graduate-clubs-and-organizations/)
## Master’s research
Khoury College master’s students participate in a full spectrum of research across the Northeastern Global Campus system, from Boston to Vancouver. Project-based courses and master’s theses provide a curriculum-based format, and some campuses include an industry-partnered research capstone as part of the program, such as Vancouver.
[Explore master’s research opportunities](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/graduate-research/)
## Campus locations
Having locations at nine of Northeastern University’s 13 network campuses places Khoury College in tech hubs around the world and brings students closer to our industry partners. Our undergraduate degrees are offered full-time in Boston and Oakland — with semester learning opportunities in Seattle, London, and Oakland. In addition, there are a host of global learning opportunities.
[Visit our campus locations](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/global-campuses/)
