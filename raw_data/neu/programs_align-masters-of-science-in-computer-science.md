# Align MS in Computer Science
[Apply now](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/apply/align-how-to-apply/)
[Request info](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/align-masters-of-science-in-computer-science/#have-questions)
The Align MS in Computer Science prepares you to break into tech, without a computer science background. Study with a supportive cohort of your peers as you spend the first two semesters building a solid base of fundamental knowledge before diving into emerging areas like AI, human-computer interaction, game design, cybersecurity, and more. When you graduate, you’ll be ready for your future in the diverse, ever-growing field of computer science.

## Learning outcomes
The Align MS in Computer Science will equip you with technical knowledge and career preparation. As a graduate, you will:
  * Exhibit proficiency in the design, implementation, and testing
  * Demonstrate skills and experience working in small teams
  * Apply algorithmic and theoretical computer-science principles
  * Demonstrate the ability to learn and develop competencies
