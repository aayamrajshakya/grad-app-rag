## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
The Graduate Certificate in Software Engineering Systems focuses on the sociotechnical approach to software engineering with attention on using engineering tools and considering real-world complexities to design and construct practical and viable software solutions.
This four-course graduate certificate is designed to provide students with opportunities to apply the fundamentals of engineering knowledge and skills to software development with attention on enterprise design and integration, secure systems design and creation, and data integration and architecture.
Note:
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
