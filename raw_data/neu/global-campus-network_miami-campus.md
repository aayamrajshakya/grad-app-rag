## Get ahead in a growing economy
Miami has long been a gateway between the U.S. and Latin America. Now, it’s becoming a gateway to fintech, startups, and global technology enterprises. Northeastern is here to help students like you transform alongside the city. With experience-based learning options, flexible courses, and in-demand programs, our students become leaders ready to create change and move their communities forward. Our Miami campus is perfectly situated within the city’s dynamic Wynwood neighborhood, a cultural and creative hub that’s also home to Spotify, LiveNation, PwC, and more.
##  What makes Miami unique 
There’s always something new happening in Miami, and Northeastern students live, work, and learn in the heart of it all thanks to our strong partnerships and unique learning model.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://miami.northeastern.edu/)


A focus on entrepreneurship 
Our Miami campus regularly hosts entrepreneurship events, like the eight-week Miami Innovation Academy fellowship and the FinTech Student Trek, so students can learn from both academic and business leaders.
A commitment to community 
Northeastern is committed to making technology more accessible to underrepresented learners through collaborations with organizations like Kode with Klossy and the Mark Cuban Foundation.
