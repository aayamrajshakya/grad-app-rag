Program overview 
##  The future of accounting 
Northeastern University’s MS in Accounting program teaches students critical skills in data mining, analysis, and innovative problem-solving. Beginning with essential accounting knowledge, the program cultivates your ability to drive strategic decisions and enhance organizational success in today’s dynamic economy. Students learn to harness the power of Big Data tools and artificial intelligence to redefine financial analysis and audits.  
Designed for undergraduate accounting majors, our program integrates advanced analytics into core courses, preparing you to leverage data for informed business decisions. Gain practical experience with cutting-edge tools essential for modern accounting practices and be ready to excel in the rapidly evolving accounting field.

Career outlook 
The accounting industry is undergoing a digital transformation, and there are more opportunities for accounting experts who understand Big Data. Employers seek tech-savvy leaders who can navigate change and think independently. With that in mind, we designed the MS in Accounting program to prepare you to meet the demands of the industry today and in the future.
In just seven months, you’ll deepen your accounting expertise, integrate advanced analytics into your skill set, and be ready to sit for the CPA exam before the busy tax season. You’ll emerge well-prepared to meet current industry demands and highly sought after by leading companies globally, spanning from agile startups to established multinational corporations.

Admissions requirements 
Admissions prerequisites
  * Foundational coursework in accounting and business from a U.S. undergraduate institution, including:
    * Financial & Managerial Accounting
    * Intermediate Accounting I & II (with U.S. GAAP coverage)
    * Auditing
    * U.S. Federal Income Tax
    * U.S. Business Law
  * General business coursework sufficient to meet CPA licensure requirements in the state where you plan to pursue licensure and employment


To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)


Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
Northeastern’s MS in Accounting program is designed to propel students beyond traditional educational boundaries, offering a dynamic and personalized approach to mastering the complexities of modern accounting practices.
Customized learning paths 
Embrace flexibility with our program that allows you to tailor your educational journey. Choose from specialized courses in audit or taxation and select from various electives to match your career aspirations.
Accelerated timeline 
In just seven months, you’ll enhance your accounting expertise by focusing on audit or tax, completing your program before the key hiring period for the busy season.
Integration of Big Data analytics 
Gain proficiency in data mining, analysis, and visualization, essential skills sought after by employers navigating the digital transformation of the accounting profession.
Career readiness and development 
Prepare for the CPA exam and licensure requirements. Benefit from strong industry connections, leading to high job placement rates and opportunities for advancement in prestigious accounting roles worldwide.
