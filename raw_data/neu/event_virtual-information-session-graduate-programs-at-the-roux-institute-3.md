Build Your Rewarding Career in Tech
Northeastern University’s Roux Institute is a hub of graduate education and innovation in scenic Portland, Maine. We invite you to join an upcoming Virtual Information Session hosted by our Graduate Admissions Team for the Portland, Maine campus.
When you enroll at Northeastern University’s Roux Institute, you will:
– Join our thriving campus community of more than 500 graduate learners from around the world;
– Learn from Northeastern’s world-renowned faculty and researchers in Portland, Maine;
– Discover the joys and convenience of life in the oceanfront city of Portland, Maine, named as the 8th-best city to live in the United States (U.S. News & World Report, 2022);
– Gain access to the powerful Northeastern University network, with the flagship Boston campus conveniently located less than 2 hours away via car, bus, or train
We can’t wait to see!
