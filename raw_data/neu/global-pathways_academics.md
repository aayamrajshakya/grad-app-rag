#  Academics 
The Global Pathways curriculum at Northeastern is designed to prepare international students for success in their degree programs by strengthening their academic English, research, and critical thinking skills. 
##  One Semester Students 
  * ESLG0520: Advanced Listening and Speaking for Graduate School
  * ESLG0550: Research & Writing for Graduate School
  * ESLG0510: Advanced Reading for Graduate School
  * ESLG0080: Career Exploration


+ one content course in destination discipline  
+ credits vary (2.0-4.0)
##  Two Semester Students 
#### Semester One
  * ESLG0234: Culture & Communication Skills for Graduate School
  * ESLG0045: Reading in the Field of Study
  * ESLG0230: Writing for Graduate School
  * ESLG0224: Core Structure and Vocabulary


#### Semester Two
  * ESLG0520: Advanced Listening and Speaking for Graduate School
  * ESLG0550: Research & Writing for Graduate School
  * ESLG0510: Advanced Reading for Graduate School
  * ESLG0080: Career Exploration


+ one content course in destination discipline (3.0)  
+ credits vary (2.0-4.0)
