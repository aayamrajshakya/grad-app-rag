Program overview 
##  Improve sustainability efforts 
Learn how top organizations develop, execute, and measure their sustainability efforts and build a competitive advantage with a Graduate Certificate in Sustainability and Business. In just four or five master’s-level courses, you can position yourself as a leader and decision-maker in this critical area of business.
You’ll learn from renowned faculty who are experienced practitioners and thought leaders in sustainability, studying real-world cases that bring your coursework to life. You’ll study the competitive implications of sustainability and learn effective frameworks for decision-making and analysis. Throughout your program, Northeastern’s unique emphasis on experience will enrich your perspective and build your confidence.

Accreditations

Program features 
  * Personalize your curriculum with three or four electives that prepare you for your unique career goals, such as social entrepreneurship, sustainability leadership, or sustainable supply chains.
  * Build confidence and skill through applied project work and real-world case studies-an experience-oriented approach that makes Northeastern University a global leader in education
  * Study in person on our beautiful campus in Boston-a global business hub with a diverse and vibrant culture
  * Receive expert guidance from the D’Amore-McKim Graduate Career Center
  * Amplify your learning with experiential opportunities outside the classroom, such as DATA Club or IDEA
  * Lay the groundwork for a future MBA-you may be able to apply completed credits toward an eligible master’s degree at Northeastern


Career outlook 
As artificial intelligence and technology speed up the pace of business, learning new skills-and refreshing existing ones-will be critical to advancing your career. Earning your Graduate Certificate in Sustainability and Business equips you with the specialized, up-to-the-moment knowledge that companies value and demonstrates your commitment to professional development. You’ll hone your ability to make strategic decisions about practices that benefit the community and the bottom line, potentially preparing you to pursue a sustainability-centered role, depending on your work experience.
Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your courses for the Graduate Certificate in Brand Management will center around case studies and applied project work, allowing you to learn from the experience of real companies and hone your skills in a team setting. Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)


Contact an enrollment counselor to learn more about eligibility requirements.
