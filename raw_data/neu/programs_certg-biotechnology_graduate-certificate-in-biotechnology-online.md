Program overview 
##  Break into biotech 
The online Graduate Certificate in Biotechnology provides individuals without a biotechnology background the essential knowledge and skills to thrive in this growing industry.
This one-year program includes four to five courses, covering key concepts and hands-on skills necessary for a successful transition into biotechnology-related fields. Delivered entirely online, it offers the flexibility needed for working professionals. Industry-aligned faculty guide you through real-world case studies and projects, ensuring you gain practical experience.
The certificate serves as a gateway to the MS in Biotechnology program, allowing students to apply all completed credits toward the master’s degree.

Program highlights 
  * 100% online format for maximum flexibility.
  * Stackable credentials: Apply all credits toward the MS in Biotechnology.
  * Taught by industry-aligned faculty with real-world experience.
  * All programs can be performed on campus in Boston. Some can be performed entirely online.
  * Lab courses are delivered on campus.


Experiential learning 
  * Engage in case studies that mirror real-world biotechnology challenges.
  * Collaborate with faculty on research projects in health, security, and sustainability.
  * Practical skills you gain can be immediately applied in your current role.

Admissions requirements 
  * Online application
  * Unofficial transcripts (official transcripts required upon acceptance of admissions)
    * The Foreign Credential Evaluation (FCE) is a required assessment of all transcripts and documents from non-U.S. accredited post-secondary education institutions. (Review the [FCE requirements](https://northeastern.my.site.com/GraduateAdmissionsFAQ/s/article/What-documents-are-required-for-a-credential-evaluation) by country.)
  * GPA 3.0 or higher
  * Personal statement
  * English language proficiency:
    * Degree earned or in progress at a U.S. institution
    * Degree earned or in progress at an institution where English is the only medium of instruction
    * Official exam scores from either the TOEFL iBT (institution code is 3682), IELTS, PTE exam, or Duolingo English Test. Scores are valid for 2 years from the test date.

Learn real-world applications through case studies and research, enhancing your ability to contribute to biotechnology projects.
