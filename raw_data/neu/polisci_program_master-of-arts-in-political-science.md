#  Master of Arts Political Science
### Overview
The Master of Arts program focuses on the core scholarly Areas of Political Science. Courses in the MA program serve as foundation for work in doctoral program or as preparation for careers in government, nonprofit organizations, or related work in the private sector. Students specialize in one of the five concentration areas.
#### The concentrations available in the MA program include:
  * American Government and Politics
  * Comparative Politics
  * International Relations
  * Public Policy
  * Security Studies


For more information on curricular requirements for the MA program see the [Course Catalog](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/social-sciences-humanities/political-science/political-science-ma/).
