##  Find your path to success 
Northeastern is dedicated to helping our international students advance their careers and strengthen their professional networks. Prepare to stand out and thrive in the workforce with support from application to graduation and beyond.
F-1 compliant graduate programs 
Northeastern offers more than 200 F-1 visa-compliant programs—including almost 100 STEM-eligible programs—across 10 international campuses in the U.S., U.K., and Canada.
  * [ Explore our programs](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/?visa_eligible=1)


International application requirements 
Applying to a U.S. university doesn’t have to feel daunting. Our dedicated admissions counselors are ready to answer your questions and help you navigate the process with ease.
  * [ See how to apply](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/international-students/apply-as-an-international-student/)


Global Pathways 
Global Pathways provides a pathway to a master’s degree for academically eligible students. The program helps strengthen your English skills and transition to North American academics and culture, preparing you for success in the classroom and beyond.
  * [ Learn more about Global Pathways](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/international-students/guidance-support/global-pathways-program/)


Student resources 
Northeastern is home to nearly 21,000 students from more than 136 countries—the second-largest international student community in the U.S. As you face the unique challenges of learning abroad, know that we are committed to providing the tools, support, and services you need to achieve your goals.
  * [ Discover student support resources](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/international-students/guidance-support/)
