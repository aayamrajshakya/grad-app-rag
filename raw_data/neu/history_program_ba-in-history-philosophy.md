#  Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)in History & Philosophy
The Department of Philosophy and Religion and the Department of History offer an interdisciplinary combined major in philosophy and history. 
Students interested in the combined major integrate the study of philosophy and ethics with the study and analysis of human history.
For more information, visit the [program requirements](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/history/history-philosophy-ba/#programrequirementstext).
