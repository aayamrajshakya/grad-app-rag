#  Master of Arts International Affairs
## Work Toward Global Equity
Examine issues that cross borders—and how they affect social groups defined by race, ethnicity, class, gender, sexuality, national belonging, and more. Human rights. Global governance. Diplomacy. Development. International public policy. Sustainability. 
## Focus on the Issues You Care About
Select from an incredibly wide range of electives for 5 of your 9 courses 
[Discover Focus Areas ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/policyschool/program/ma-in-international-affairs/degree-requirements/)
## Small Classes. Leading Experts.
Class sizes in the MA International Affairs program average 20 students. 
[Meet Our Faculty ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/policyschool/people/faculty/)
## Build a Career with Meaning
The MA International Affairs prepares you for influential positions with NGOs, nonprofits, international organizations, governments, and private companies.
[See Career Outcomes](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/internationalaffairs/program/master-of-arts-in-international-affairs/career-outcomes/)
## Top-Ranked for Co-op and Research
The MA International Affairs offers an optional, paid, full-time, 6-month co-op
[Explore Co-op Stories](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/internationalaffairs/program/master-of-arts-in-international-affairs/experiential-opportunities/)
## Rich Resources. Diverse Community.
Find engaging research, lectures, and opportunities for collaboration at the [Center for International Affairs and World Cultures](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/internationalcenter/), the [Dukakis Center for Urban and Regional Policy](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/dukakiscenter/), the [Boston Area Research Initiative](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/bari/) (BARI), and the [Global Resilience Institute](https://globalresilience.northeastern.edu/). Make the international city of Boston your base. Work with diverse peers and faculty.
## Take a Flexible Path to Success 
The average annual pay for an international affairs role in the U.S. is over $100,000. Professionals with a master’s degree have a 20% higher salary on average ([Indeed, 2023](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/pay-salary/how-much-does-degree-increase-earnings)). Core courses are typically scheduled at 4:45 p.m. or after, with in-person, hybrid, full-time, and part-time options.
