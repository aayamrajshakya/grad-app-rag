Program overview 
##  An MBA program for the 21st century 
Emerging technology has redefined how we do business—creating demand for adaptable, curious leaders who can turn challenges into growth. When you earn your Full-Time MBA at D’Amore-McKim School of Business, you’ll develop the business skills and entrepreneurial mindset to excel in this dynamic environment.
Building on a strong foundation of core business concepts, you’ll begin customizing your coursework in your second semester to create the unique blend of expertise that best serves your career goals. You’ll study with faculty who are thought leaders and entrepreneurs, exploring real-world case studies that bring your learnings to life. Throughout your program, you’ll have transformational experiences that amplify your learning—including Northeastern’s signature corporate residency, where you can earn a salary at a leading firm as a valued team member.

Accreditations 
Accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business International—the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide.
Program benefits 
  * Take a paid position with a leading firm or startup in our intensive, long-term corporate residency.
  * Participate in our wide range of experiential learning opportunities, including our Global Consulting Project, 360 Huntington Fund, and IDEA, our student-led venture accelerator.
  * Study with your peers in person on our beautiful campus in Boston—a global business hub and cultural center.
  * Choose two of our 25+ in-demand concentrations, including our unique MBA x concentrations in areas such as artificial intelligence or cybersecurity.
  * Customize two-thirds of your curriculum with elective credits to build a rich background for decision-making.
  * Map your career plan with your dedicated advisor at the D’Amore-McKim Graduate Career Center.
  * Amplify your learning with experiential opportunities where you’ll apply what you’ve learned in actual business settings—and see it make an impact.
  * Qualify for Optional Practical Training (OPT) as an international student with our F-1 visa-compliant curriculum.


Career outlook 
The D’Amore-McKim Full-Time MBA program can prepare you for a leadership role in any industry, depending on your work experience. Through classroom learning and confidence—building experiences, you’ll cultivate the indispensable qualities of a decision-maker—deep business expertise, a curious mindset, and the ability to innovate to solve complex problems. Companies of all sizes heavily recruit our graduates, from startups to multinational organizations.
Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your Full-Time MBA courses will center on case studies and applied project work, allowing you to gain insight from the experience of real companies and build skills for collaborating to solve problems. You’ll also have opportunities to strengthen your business knowledge through unique experience-based activities, some of which can include travel:
  * [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/)
  * [Bloomberg Business Lab](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/bloomberg-business-lab/)

  * [Global Field Study](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/global-field-study/)
  * [IDEA](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/idea/)

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)
