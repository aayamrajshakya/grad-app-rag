#  MegHeckman
Faculty Affiliate in Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies; Assistant Professor of Journalism 
College of Arts, Media, and Design 
Meg Heckman is a journalist, author and educator focused on building a news ecosystem that is robust, diverse and equipped to serve all segments of society. She is an assistant professor of journalism and a faculty affiliate of the [NULab for Digital Humanities and Computational Social Science](https://nam05.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.northeastern.edu%2Fnulab%2F&data=02%7C01%7Cb.farrell%40northeastern.edu%7C8ed5c4f1a92047a22bd808d78edbb658%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C637134946632324839&sdata=OMXXLLSTecGTclMZk%2FJ6ao5ALuhg%2B6oENSNvq47hQdA%3D&reserved=0) and the Women’s, Gender, and Sexuality Studies Program where she serves on the Executive Committee.
