## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
The Certificate in Postsecondary Teaching prepares graduate students to be effective teachers and educators both inside and outside the classroom. It focuses on both didactic training in best practices and pedagogy around traditional teaching as well as online teaching. Finally, it is anchored by a teaching practicum where students put into practice what they have learned.
