# Early Black Boston Digital Almanac
**_**Partially supported by a NULab Seedling Grant** **and an American Rescue Plan award.**_**
Inspired by Benjamin Franklin’s “Poor Richard’s Almanack,” the [Early Black Boston Digital Almanac](https://ebbda.northeastern.edu/) is a collection of interesting and edifying archival material and digital exhibits about the vibrant Black communities of Boston during the 18thth
