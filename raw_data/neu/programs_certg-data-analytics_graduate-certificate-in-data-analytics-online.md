Program overview 
##  Master data analytics online 
Northeastern’s Online Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics provides you with the foundational knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly expanding field of data science. This interdisciplinary program covers key areas such as data management, machine learning, data mining, statistics, and data visualization, equipping you to make data-driven decisions in any sector.
Through a blend of academic coursework and real-world application, you’ll explore how to collect, store, and analyze large datasets. The four-course program, designed through a collaboration between the Khoury College of Computer Sciences, the College of Social Sciences and Humanities, and the D’Amore-McKim School of Business, offers flexibility and a pathway to pursue advanced master’s programs in related fields.

Program highlights 
  * Learn essential data analytics skills, including machine learning and data mining.
  * Flexible, fully online format tailored for working professionals.
  * Engage in hands-on learning through real-world case studies and practical exercises.
  * Gain insights into managing and analyzing both structured and unstructured data.
  * Continue your education and pursue a Master of Science in the following programs: 
    * Master of Science in Health Informatics
    * Master of Science in Health Data Analytics
    * Master of Science in Bioinformatics
    * Master of Science in Urban Informatics


Career outlook 
In today’s increasingly competitive, technology-driven global environment, where business data across all companies is estimated to double every 1.2 years, and the public sector is building enormous data infrastructures, the ability to manage and analyze large volumes of data is critical to growth, innovation, and productivity in every field.
From finding cures for cancer to plotting more efficient service delivery routes, from predicting crime hot spots in our cities to pinpointing marketing efforts, industries need individuals with the curiosity, creativity, knowledge, and skills to analyze and model complex datasets and draw actionable insights. The rapid evolution in the way businesses, research institutions, and governments produce, process, and analyze data has created a shortage of talent.
This certificate equips you with the skills employers need and provides a pathway to roles in fields such as health data analysis, bioinformatics, and urban planning.

Admissions requirements

  * Undergraduate GPA of 3.0

  * Transcripts from all institutions attended 
  * Statement of purpose, including description of relevant work experience
  * Recent professional resume listing detailed position responsibilities
  * Reference letter from an individual who knows your academic or professional record and/or potential for graduate study
  * Recommended TOEFL (90 minimum) or IELTS (6.5 minimum) examination scores (international students only)
