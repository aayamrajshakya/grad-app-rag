#  Student Experience 
## Learn what it’s like to be a Global Pathways student
Global Pathways offers students the rare opportunity to pursue a degree from one of the country’s top-ranked universities.
Built around the specific needs of international students, Global Pathways is a college preparatory program that combines university-level academic courses with intensive English instruction—preparing you to succeed at Northeastern and beyond.
