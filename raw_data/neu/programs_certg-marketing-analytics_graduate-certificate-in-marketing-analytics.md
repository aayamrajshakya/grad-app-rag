Program overview 
##  Up-to-the-minute data skills 
Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Marketing Analytics equips you with the latest skills to turn data into intelligent marketing strategies. In just four or five courses, you’ll learn how to link insights to customer needs using predictive analytics, market research, and digital marketing tactics.
Under the guidance of expert faculty, you’ll build proficiency in SPSS, Python, or R for marketing analysis and tackle real-world cases that prepare you to excel in today’s data-centric marketplace. Personalize your curriculum to match your career goals and leverage your new analytical expertise to drive impactful marketing decisions.

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

##  Stack your certificate
