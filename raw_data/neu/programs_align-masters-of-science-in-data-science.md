# Align MS in Data Science
[Apply now](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/apply/align-how-to-apply/)
[Request info](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/programs/align-masters-of-science-in-data-science/#have-questions)
Today, all companies are driven by data and the demand for expertise in data science has never been higher. The Align MS in Data Science will teach you the skills to harness the power of data, even without a tech background. Study with a supportive cohort of your peers as you spend the first two semesters building a solid CS foundation. Then, you’ll dive deep into the world of data, learning to use it to solve some of the world’s most complex organizational problems.
[Explore program requirements in the course catalog](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/computer-information-science/computer-science/data-science-ms-align/index.html#programrequirementstext)
