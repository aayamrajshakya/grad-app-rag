## Weekly Enrollment Counseling Sessions
Our global team offers weekly counseling sessions in Mandarin, Taiwanese Mandarin, Spanish, and English. 
During these sessions, you’ll learn more about the university, next steps after admission, how to get your I-20, tuition, scholarships, campus life, and much more.
Drop in quickly to ask a question, or join the group discussion for the full 60 minutes. Simply select the session link for your region below and mark your calendar.
