## Project Description
Transfeminine people are broadly defined as those who were assigned the gender identity of “male” at birth, but who now hold a different gender identity. Transfeminine disposability is the phenomenon of transfeminine people commonly being the targets of attacks on social media that serve to isolate them by turning online communities against them. These attacks often use technical features of social media, such as taking screenshots, in harmful ways. The result is that the victim is cut off from critical support networks pushed out ofwhy  ways that social media enables and facilitates it.transfeminine resistance
