Program overview 
##  Shape the future of finance 
The fast-paced, global business world demands strategic financial expertise. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Corporate Finance provides you with the tools to thrive in high-risk, high-reward situations.
In just four or five master’s-level courses, you’ll delve into the fundamentals of financial strategy, gaining a detailed understanding of how companies increase profitability and shareholder value. Customize your curriculum with electives like mergers and acquisitions or business turnarounds to align with your career goals.
You’ll also have the chance to explore the Bloomberg Business Lab or join the student management team of the 360 Huntington Fund, enhancing your hands-on finance experience.

Career outlook 
In the high-speed world of corporate finance, learning new business skills-and refreshing existing ones-will be critical to advancing your career. Earning your Graduate Certificate in Corporate Finance equips you with the specialized, up-to-the-moment knowledge that companies value and demonstrates your commitment to professional development.
Your new skill set can lead you toward a role as an analyst or manager, depending on your work experience. You can be confident that your credential from D’Amore-McKim carries weight in the business community-employers appreciate our focus on the digital economy, which is one reason our graduates are highly recruited by top firms, multinational businesses, and startups.
Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your courses for the Graduate Certificate in Corporate Finance will center around case studies and applied project work, allowing you to learn from the experience of real companies and hone your skills in a team setting. You can also strengthen your financial knowledge outside the classroom through unique applied opportunities:

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)
