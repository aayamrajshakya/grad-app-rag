#  Stacey KatzBourns
Professor of French; Chair, World Languages and Cultures Department 
Stacey Katz Bourns teaches courses in French language, linguistics, and culture. Her research focuses on the grammar of spoken vs. written French, syntax, pragmatics, applied linguistics, and curriculum development. She currently holds the post of Assistant Editor (Linguistics) for the French Review
