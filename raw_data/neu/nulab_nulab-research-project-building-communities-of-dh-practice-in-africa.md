# Building Communities of DH Practice in Africa
## People in this story
  * [ Jessica Parr ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/faculty/jess-parr/)


**Partially supported by a NULab Community Collaboration Grant.**
Professor Jessica Parr is traveling to Porto Novo, Benin as part of a consortium to teach digital public history methodologies, including digital preservation, LiDAR scanning, mapping, and archival practice. The project team will be running a series of workshops on the campus of L’Ecole du Patrimoine African in December 2024.
