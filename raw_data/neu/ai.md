##  Foundations of Responsible AI badge 
Northeastern makes AI a priority for every learner. That’s why our graduate students* get access to Foundations of Responsible AI, a non-credit badge course, even before classes begin. Completing it allows you to earn an official AI badge, giving you critical skills to navigate today’s AI-driven workplace and a credential you can share with employers right away.
*Not offered to School of Law, Office of the Provost, or Mills College graduate programs
  * [ Learn more](https://online.northeastern.edu/badges/ai/foundations-of-responsible-ai/)
