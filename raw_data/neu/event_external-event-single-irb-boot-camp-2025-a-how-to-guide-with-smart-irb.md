# Single IRB Boot Camp 2025: A How-to Guide with SMART IRB
Description
New to single IRB? Wondering how to build and execute a successful reliance program at your institution? Interested in learning how changes in SMART IRB Agreement V3.0 may affect your reliance process? Join us for a free online boot camp for IRB/HRPP personnel. Whether you primarily serve as a reviewing IRB or relying institution (or a little of both), we will demonstrate how you can leverage SMART IRB resources to successfully implement single IRB arrangements. Day 1: October 29, 2025 (3 hours, see times below) Reliance Requests Using SMART IRB Agreement V3.0 What Institutions Need in Place for Single IRB Review Reliance System Overview Day 2: October 30, 2025 (3 hours, see times below) Communication Training Study Teams Harmonization Guidance Review Resources Recap and FAQs Who should attend? IRB/HRPP personnel who have beginner-to-intermediate level experience with the Single IRB review process. Experienced personnel interested in learning more about integrating changes in SMART IRB Agreement V3.0 into your reliance process may also wish to attend. For more advanced offerings, stay tuned for our annual Single IRB Symposium. Please Note: This program will cover similar information as prior Bootcamp sessions with updates to reflect SMART IRB Agreement V3.0 and the new Reliance System. SMART IRB’s Single IRB Boot Camp will include two half-day sessions; your registration will secure your spot for both days.
