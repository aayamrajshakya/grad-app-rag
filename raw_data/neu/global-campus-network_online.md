## An innovative online learning experience
Northeastern’s online programs are more than just on-ground classes in a digital format. They’re online learning experiences created by instructional designers to help students fully immerse themselves in their studies. With the same expert faculty, learning outcomes, and experience-based opportunities as students on campus, you’ll earn a degree that prepares you for the next phase of your career—on your terms.
##  What makes our online programs unique 
Our online students get the same high-quality, experience-packed education as those studying in person.
Optimized environment 
Each course provides interactive learning features, cutting-edge tools, and customizable modules to make the most of your experience.
Experiential focus 
Like their on-campus counterparts, online students can take on co-ops and internships, engage in Experiential Network projects, conduct research, attend workshops, and more.
High-quality outcomes 
Your online degree is the same as that of your on-campus peers, with the same expert professors and learning objectives.
Flexible scheduling 
Many of our courses do not have set meeting times, allowing you to study whenever and wherever works best for you. Others offer evening scheduling for working professionals.
