Program overview 
##  Reimagine management leadership 
In today’s data-driven economy, how and where we do business is changing. Employers increasingly seek nimble managers with broad business skill sets and specialized knowledge in their field. You need the freedom of choice to select the courses that will help you to achieve your goals. D’Amore-McKim has created a flexible and innovative Master of Science in Management degree-we call it the MS x.
In this highly flexible program, you’ll acquire specialized expertise through your concentration. In your personalizable core coursework, you’ll attain the competencies employers seek-the basics of accounting and finance, data presentation & analysis, and a customer orientation through all parts of business-through courses that align with your experience, skills, and goals.

Unique features 
  * Build the business skills employers seek in new hires by personalizing your curriculum to match your goals.
  * Specialize your studies in one of 15 in-demand concentrations.
  * Apply your business knowledge and skills to real business challenges through our project-based curriculum.
  * Complete your degree in as few as 12 months with full-time study.
  * Apply your credits toward a future MBA at Northeastern once your master’s degree is complete.


Career outlook

You’ll graduate equipped with the core competencies employers seek-the basics of accounting and finance, data presentation and analysis, customer orientation through all parts of business, and more.
Your business knowledge and deep expertise in your concentration area can help you launch or accelerate your career to a significant role in just about any industry you choose. Employers especially value the signature D’Amore-McKim emphasis on the role of data and technology in the global marketplace, which is one reason D’Amore-McKim graduates are highly recruited by top firms, multinational businesses, and startups.

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)
