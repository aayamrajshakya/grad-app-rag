## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
This certificate was designed in response to a need in the biotechnology industry for individuals, in particular regulators, to obtain a strong foundation in the science behind good regulatory practice today, specifically in relation to biopharmaceuticals.
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
## Core Requirements
Course List Code | Title | Hours  
---|---|---  
[BIOT 5330](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?P=BIOT%205330 "BIOT 5330") | Drug Safety and Immunogenicity | 3  
[BIOT 5500](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?P=BIOT%205500 "BIOT 5500") | Concepts in Regulatory Science | 3  
[BIOT 5621](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?P=BIOT%205621 "BIOT 5621") | Protein Principles in Biotechnology | 3  
[BIOT 6320](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?P=BIOT%206320 "BIOT 6320") | Design and Development of Biopharmaceuticals | 3  
or [BIOT 5340](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?P=BIOT%205340 "BIOT 5340") |  Introduction to Biotherapeutic Approvals
