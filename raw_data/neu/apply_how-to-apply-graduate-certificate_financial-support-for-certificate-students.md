# Financial Support for Certificate Students
Khoury College graduate certificate programs are built to accelerate your computer science education and career. Eligible students may be considered for financial aid through the university’s Student Financial Services. They can help you navigate the forms, processes, and deadlines to apply for and receive financial aid.
[Visit Student Financial Services](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/)
## Tuition and fees
Khoury College’s certificate program costs are determined by the number of credits each program requires. For the most up-to-date cost per credit hour and information on student fees, refer to the Student Financial Services website. Total credit hours required for each program can be found in the [Academic Catalog](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/computer-information-science/). Total tuition costs are an estimate and will be determined by the number of credit hours you elect throughout the course of your program.
[Information on tuition and fees](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/billing-payments/tuition-and-fees/#collapse-3-item-7)
