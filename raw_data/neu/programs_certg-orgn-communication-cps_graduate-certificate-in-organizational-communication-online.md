Program overview 
##  Become a stronger communicator 
The online Graduate Certificate in Organizational Communication online program focuses on the dynamics of communication and how to become an effective communicator in complex organizations. Whether this involves meetings, professional presentations, crisis communication, or intercultural exchanges, the message is: effective communication is essential to career success. Related topics include ethical issues in organizational communication and strategies and techniques for assessment, negotiation, and information management.
The online program prepares students to communicate clearly in an increasingly digital environment. The format also makes it easier for working professionals to balance coursework with professional and personal commitments.

Experiential learning 
Our signature experiential learning model combines academics with professional practice to help students gain relevant, real-world skills they can apply to their desired industry. Northeastern offers a variety of ways for students to add experience to their resumé, including:
Experiential Learning at Work – Working professionals collaborate with their employer to develop a project that addresses a key business need their company has in an area they want familiarity with.
Experiential Network (XN) – Students work virtually with a sponsoring organization on a short-term project over a six-week period.
Full-Time Co-op Opportunities – Students on co-op work in a paid position in their field of study for three to six months.
