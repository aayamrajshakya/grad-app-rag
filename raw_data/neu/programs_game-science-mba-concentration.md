## Develop engaging games through data-driven design
Rapid technological advancements and constant innovation keep the gaming industry growing and evolving. From social and mobile games to augmented and virtual reality, and the gamification of health, education, and science, there are endless opportunities. 
Our innovative MBA x Game Science concentration, with courses taught by faculty from [Northeastern's College of Arts, Media and Design](https://camd.northeastern.edu/), will prepare you to bring business best practices, data analytics, and scientific methods to the gaming industry. You'll learn to blend skills in game design, psychology, sociology, anthropology, media studies, data analytics, and biometrics to create truly engaging and immersive gaming experiences. 
You don't need a background in game science. This concentration is open to anyone interested in developing games using a scientific approach.
