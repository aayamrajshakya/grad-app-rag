Program overview 
##  Address climate challenges 
The Graduate Certificate in Climate and Engineering provides students with essential knowledge to tackle climate change’s impact on engineered systems. This three-course program delves into decarbonization, carbon sequestration, climate engineering, and adaptation strategies.
Students will learn how to mitigate emissions, re-engineer systems, and assess the safety and societal impact of climate solutions. Open to both engineering and non-engineering graduates, the program emphasizes the analytical skills needed to create effective, sustainable designs.

Program details 
  * Open to students with non-engineering undergraduate degrees.
  * Graduate certificates in the College of Engineering can be taken alongside a master’s degree in the College of Engineering or pursued separately. Students not pursuing a master’s degree who have completed the graduate certificate can apply the certificate credits toward an eligible master’s degree in the College of Engineering.


Career outlook 
The demand for climate-savvy engineers is growing across sectors, from government agencies to private firms. This certificate prepares you for roles in municipalities, NGOs, engineering companies, and organizations developing climate action plans.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Civil Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-civil-engr-environmental/master-of-science-in-civil-engineering/)
  * [Master of Science in Engineering and Public Policy](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-engineering-public-policy/master-of-science-in-engineering-and-public-policy/)
  * [Master of Science in Environmental Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msenve-environmental-engineer/master-of-science-in-environmental-engineering/)
  * [Master of Science in Sustainable Building Systems](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mssbs-sustainable-bldg-sys/master-of-science-in-sustainable-building-systems/)
