Program overview 
##  Transform your future 
Fifteen months and you’re in business. The MS in Accounting/MBA prepares you for a rewarding career—even if your undergraduate degree wasn’t in accounting or you studied accounting outside the U.S. This combined degree program—developed in collaboration with top accounting firms—offers a rigorous curriculum that equips you with essential business knowledge, comprehensive accounting experience, and critical analytics skills for success in a stable and dynamic field.
Immerse yourself in a paid three-month corporate residency at a leading firm, where you’ll apply classroom knowledge to associate-level work during accounting’s busy season.

Career outlook 
Employers highly value the intense preparation our graduates receive. Completing an MS in Accounting/MBA program sets you up for a rewarding career in the accounting industry by doubling your expertise. The MBA adds essential management skills, making you versatile and ready for roles like staff accountant, senior accountant, manager, and eventually partner in public accounting. In corporate settings, you could progress from financial analyst to CFO, with specialized career paths in tax consulting, forensic accounting, and internal auditing also available.
As part of the program, you’ll gain real-world experience through a paid three-month residency at a Big Four or other top accounting firm during the busy season. Guided by seasoned mentors, you’ll dive into the fast-paced world of accounting, taking on real responsibilities and solving problems as part of a team. Over the past five years, 99% of our graduates have secured jobs within three months of graduation, with 98% receiving offers from the firms where they completed their residencies.
Experiential learning 
Experience is at the heart of a Northeastern education. As you gear up for a career in business, you’ll have many opportunities for experience-powered learning. In your corporate residency, you’ll apply your classroom knowledge as you take on critical projects and deliver work that makes an impact, forming valuable connections along the way.
Charge your learning with experience outside of the classroom. You’ll be able to take advantage of these opportunities outside the classroom:
  * [Bloomberg Business Lab](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/bloomberg-business-lab/)
  * Commodities Trading Club
  * DATA Club
  * DEI Fellows
  * [IDEA Student-Led Venture Accelerator](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/idea/)
  * Sustainable Finance Club

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)


Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
Combining theory and practice, the MS in Accounting/MBA gives you a well-rounded understanding of accounting and business and prepares you professionally with experience at a top accounting firm.
Targeted curriculum 
Our faculty partners with industry professionals to craft a curriculum that equips you with the skills needed to tackle current and future accounting challenges.
Valuable corporate experience 
You’ll gain real-world experience in a paid three-month residency at a Big Four or other top accounting firm. Guided by seasoned mentors, you’ll learn by doing.
Analytical skills advantage 
With the growing demand for data tools and AI to boost business performance, our analytics concentration enhances your analytical skills and helps you use data to make informed decisions.
A professional edge 
If you’re planning for the CPA exam, the MS in Accounting/MBA curriculum will help you prepare. The program will also set you on a path for advancement and enhance your value to employers.
