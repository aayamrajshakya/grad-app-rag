##### DEFINITIONS
This table lists responsibilities for different research administrator roles.
Team | Role | Definition  
---|---|---  
NU-RES Research Administration – | Proposal Grant & Contract Officer () |  Institutional Review and Submission of the Following:
  * Actions routed through ePAWs (proposals, pre-proposals, specific annual progress reports, etc.) – see page [Actions Routed via ePAWs](https://res.research.northeastern.edu/lifecycle-management/develop-proposal/actions-routed-via-epaws/) for details.
  * Proposal post-submission materials
  * Just-in-Time materials

  
Transaction Grant & Contract Officer () |  Negotiation of Funded Agreements (Awards):
  * Originating from non-profit organizations (e.g., Foundation grants) or under federal grants or cooperative agreements.

Award Compliance and Acceptance Review:
  * All award setups, regardless of funding agency.

Non-Financial Award Management:
  * Review and submission of administrative closeouts and progress reports, regardless of funding agency.
  * Review and submission of prior approval requests (rebudgets, non-cost extensions, change of PI, etc.) for awards originating from non-profit organizations or under federal grants or cooperative agreements.

Note:  
NU-RES Finance Team | Research Finance Analyst (RFA) | Responsibilities include review of expense compliance transactions, e.g. JVs, cost transfers, vendor payments (DPay forms), PO requisitions, financial reporting, and closeouts.  
Research Account Analyst (RAA) | Responsibilities include Invoicing and letter of credit draws, payments and collections, and closeouts.
