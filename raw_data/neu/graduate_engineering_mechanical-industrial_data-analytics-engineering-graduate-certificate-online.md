## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
The Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics Engineering program focuses on fundamental concepts, tools, and techniques to extract information from large datasets in order to support effective decision making. This program is designed to provide opportunities for students to master high-demand data intelligence skills through hands-on experience on data storage, data retrieval, data visualization, and prediction.
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
