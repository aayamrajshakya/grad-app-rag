# Returning College of Professional Studies Students
## Step 1: Apply for Financial Aid for next year
You must complete the FAFSA every year in order to be eligible for financial aid.
  * Generally available October 1. The 2024-2025 FASFA will be released in December 2023.
  * To make the FAFSA experience easier, use the [IRS Data Retrieval Tool](https://studentaid.gov/resources/irs-drt-text)
  * Northeastern’s School code is: 002199


  * A copy of your student aid report (SAR) will be emailed to you by the Department of Education approximately 7-10 business days after you submit your FAFSA
  * SFS will reach out to your university email address with requests for any additional documentation
  * Requests for additional information are also viewable on the [Student Hub](https://northeastern.sharepoint.com/sites/EmployeeHub). Once you have logged in, choose “Resources,” “Financial Services,” then select “My Financial Aid Status,” and “Documents” from the menu options


  * Respond promptly to any request for additional documentation
  * If you have been selected for the [federal verification](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/federal-verification-process/) process or additional documentation is necessary to process your aid award, you must submit these materials before a financial aid award can be prepared

  * Submit requested documents via the [SFS document upload portal](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/forms/financial-aid-document-upload/). If you have not yet created an account in the SFS document upload portal, you must do so before you can upload documents. For instructions for setting up your account, [click here](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/forms/financial-aid-document-upload/)


  * Students who have filed a completed aid application and are making [satisfactory academic progress](https://studentfinance.northeastern.edu/policies-procedures/satisfactory-academic-progress/) will receive an aid package electronically
  * Once awards are available, students will be notified via their Northeastern email account
  * Financial aid packages will be released beginning in the summer for the upcoming academic year
  * Financial aid awards will continue to be prepared on a rolling basis for students who complete the financial aid application process


Please note: At any point in this process, you may be selected for the federal verification process. Your aid award will be finalized when all verification & requested documents are submitted and reviewed
