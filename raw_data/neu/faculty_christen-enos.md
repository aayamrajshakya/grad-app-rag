#  ChristenEnos
Teaching Professor in English 
Christen Enos’ short stories have appeared in Quick Fiction, The New Orleans Review, Natural Bridge, and Phoebe, among other journals, and her nonfiction work has been published by The Tusculum Review and MAKE. She has been teaching at Northeastern since 2005.
Awards & Honors
Pushcart Prize nominee, 2009
Selected Publications
  * Fiction has appeared in The New Orleans ReviewQuick FictionThe Portland ReviewPhoebe The Louisville ReviewNatural Bridge
  * Narrative nonfiction has appeared in The Tusculum ReviewMAKE Magazine
