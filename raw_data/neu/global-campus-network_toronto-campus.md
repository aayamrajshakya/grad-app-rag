## Shaping the future of in-demand industries
Toronto is a thriving centre of research and innovation in the life sciences, technology, and more, offering Northeastern students the perfect opportunities to grow their careers and skill sets. These advantages, combined with our strong ties to local industry leaders, allow students to position themselves as innovative thinkers, globally minded leaders, and change-makers within their industries.
##  What makes Toronto unique 
We’ve designed our Toronto campus to include top-notch facilities, encourage collaboration with partners, and give students easy access to all the city has to offer.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://toronto.northeastern.edu/)


Our partnerships 
Our industry partners include leaders in health, tech, construction, transportation, and finance, and community partnerships include local nonprofits that focus on pressing issues.
Our location 
Our Toronto campus is located in the centre of the city’s downtown, within walking distance of events, shops, restaurants, and entertainment.

##  Toronto campus highlights 
Industry meets experience in this multicultural city. Here, students can immerse themselves in thriving businesses—no matter their academic background.
  * [ Learn more about studying in Toronto](https://toronto.northeastern.edu/the-toronto-campus/)


Pathway to Tech programs 
Specially designed degree programs in analytics and information systems give professionals new to the field the foundation they need to start a new career.
Industry-aligned degrees 
Current trends, research, and industry needs heavily influence our programs, which students can typically undertake on a part-time basis while they continue working.
A national hub 
As Canada’s leading city for finance, technology, and the life sciences, Toronto lets students make valuable connections with industry leaders through campus events, co-ops, and more.
