Program overview 
##  An MBA program for the 21st century 
As technology and data transform the business world, companies need resilient leaders with an innovative mindset and future-forward skills. In D’Amore-McKim’s Part-Time MBA program, you’ll gain the broad business knowledge that employers value, the critical problem-solving and communication skills necessary for leadership, and an entrepreneurial spirit that enables you to adapt in a dynamic environment.
Building on a foundation of cutting-edge core courses, this program is flexible and customizable—with all the benefits of face-to-face study, including personal connections with faculty who have extensive backgrounds as business leaders and innovators.
You can also [amplify your learning by doing](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/part-time-mba/experiences/), whether through applied projects, unique experiential electives, or extracurricular activities such as the [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/).

Career outlook 
The Part-Time MBA program, designed for current professionals in diverse business areas, builds your knowledge of core business concepts and cultivates your agility, resilience, creativity, and entrepreneurial mindset.
Employers especially value the signature D’Amore-McKim emphasis on the role of data and technology in transforming business. Depending on your experience and available opportunities, you may be able to leverage your degree for a more advanced role in your current industry or a new career in a different field.

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)


Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
Learn to lead with powerful skills and confidence, positioning yourself to make an immediate impact at work and fast-track your career growth.
Relevant study and practice 
Students are prepared for the dynamic business world with a timely and innovative curriculum centered on transforming business with data and technology.
Build career networks 
You’ll build a close, collaborative professional network by studying in person on our Boston campus.
Sustained career development and support 
You’ll enjoy lifetime access to our Graduate Career Center’s support and its extensive network of hiring employers.
Flexibility to fit your goals 
You can change your course schedule each semester to balance your studies, work, and life. You can start in September or January, and most Part-Time MBA students finish in three years.
