## Bioengineering Coursework
All Bioengineering students must complete 5 semester-hours of general Bioengineering core course requirements.
Students pursuing a concentration in [Biomechanics and Mechanobiology](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/bioengineering/bioengineering-msbioe/#Biomechanics) and [Molecular, Cell, and Tissue Engineering](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/bioengineering/bioengineering-msbioe/#curriculumtext) must complete 8 semester-hours of core coursework and 4 semester-hours of elective coursework.
Students pursuing a concentration in [Biomedical Devices and Bioimaging](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/bioengineering/bioengineering-msbioe/#curriculumtext) and [Systems, Synthetic, and Computational Bioengineering](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/bioengineering/bioengineering-msbioe/#curriculumtext) will complete 12 semester-hours of core coursework.
The above plans of study are for current students only. These plans are samples only- actual curriculum sequences may deviate from these samples. Programs of study are the responsibility of the student.
