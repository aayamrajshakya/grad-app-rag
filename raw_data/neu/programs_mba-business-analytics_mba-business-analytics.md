Program overview 
##  A tech-savvy leadership MBA 
In today’s fast-paced business world, leadership demands more than technical expertise—it calls for strategic insight, emotional intelligence, and adaptability. Northeastern’s one-year, STEM-designated MBA is built to help you meet that challenge head-on.
You’ll gain the tools to navigate a tech-driven landscape through immersive coursework in data analytics, quantitative methods, and artificial intelligence (AI). At the same time, you’ll develop the human-centered leadership skills that employers value most, empowering you to lead with empathy, vision, and purpose.

Accreditation 
Accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business International—the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide.
Program benefits 
  * One-year, full-time format that fast-tracks your career
  * STEM designation that opens doors for international students
  * Blended curriculum of rigorous, quantitative core classes plus leadership and soft skill electives
  * Access to D’Amore-McKim’s career services and recruiting opportunities
  * Powered by experiential learning and real-world business insight


Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your STEM MBA courses will center on case studies and applied project work, allowing you to gain insight from the experience of real companies and build skills for collaborating to solve problems. You’ll also have opportunities to strengthen your business knowledge through unique experience-based activities, including:
  * [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/)
  * [Bloomberg Business Lab](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/bloomberg-business-lab/)
  * [IDEA](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/idea/)

  * Application form 
  * Personal statement (<500 words) 
  * Resumé
