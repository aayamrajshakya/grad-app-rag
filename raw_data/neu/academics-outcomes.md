## Find your graduate program
If you’re ready to level up your career and become a leader in your field, Northeastern is where you want to be. Here, you can choose from 200-plus tailored certificate, master’s, and professional doctorate programs and join a global community designed to help you succeed and achieve your professional goals.
### Master’s degrees
Learn new skills, deepen your knowledge, and gain valuable experience with a master’s degree. Our programs combine expert-led classroom study with real-world application to help you advance your career and create meaningful impact.
[ Master’s degree programs](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/?degree_types=masters)
### Professional doctorates
Combine academic coursework with applied research projects to gain deep, industry-specific knowledge and develop solutions to real-world problems facing your workplace or community. Gain industry insights from faculty researchers in law and policy, education, nursing, and more.
[ Professional doctorate programs](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/?degree_types=professional-doctorate)
### PhDs
Conduct original research alongside world-class faculty to solve some of the world’s most pressing issues. These programs offer dedicated mentoring, robust resources, and experiential opportunities inside industry, government, nonprofits, and academia.
[ PhD programs](https://phd.northeastern.edu/)
### Graduate certificates
Graduate certificates are a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills to advance your career or build toward a master’s degree. Choose from more than 120 programs that feature four to six focused courses and can be completed in as little as six months.
[ Graduate certificate programs](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/?degree_types=certificate)
