### Bloomberg Experiential Learning Partnership
At Northeastern University, you'll benefit from our exclusive status as a Bloomberg Experiential Learning Partner (ELP). This means you get free access to the Bloomberg Terminal, the world's top financial software system. You may use Bloomberg Terminal exercises in your coursework, making decisions based on real-time company data. This lets you explore the fast-paced global marketplace, blending theory with practice.
Plus, through the [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/), you can manage part of Northeastern's endowment using Bloomberg tools, gaining invaluable real-world finance experience.

### State-of-the-art finance lab
The D'Amore-McKim Bloomberg Business Lab, located in Dodge Hall, is a dynamic space with 20 Bloomberg Terminal stations. It gives Northeastern students, faculty, staff, and alumni a hands-on way to explore the world of finance. Whether you're tracking real-time market data or diving into industry-standard financial tools, the lab offers unique, practical learning experiences that bring classroom concepts to life.
### Earn a Bloomberg certification 
Earning a Bloomberg certification is a great way to dive deeper into finance, and there are several paths. As an undergraduate, you'll earn your BMC certification just by taking Introduction to Business[BUSN 1101](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/search/?search=BUSN+1101)), where it's built right into the course. Graduate students can gain certification through select classes that integrate Bloomberg training into the curriculum. And if you're part of the [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/), you'll typically complete the certification as part of that immersive, real-world investing experience.
> When hiring companies see I have a Bloomberg Market Concept certification, they know I've done my homework and have the skills. This certification is an important asset for any company when hiring a team member, and it put me in a favorable position during my hiring process.”
> [Kavan Pandit, MS in Quantitative Finance'22](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/news/certifications-stand-out-in-a-competitive-finance-job-market/?highlight=Kavan+Pandit)

### Trade live on Bloomberg
Getting hands-on experience with live trading through the Bloomberg Terminal is a game-changer for anyone interested in finance. It gives you a real feel for how markets move, how trades are executed, and how professionals make decisions under pressure. At Northeastern, there are multiple opportunities to engage with this powerful platform—from classroom activities and certification programs to competitions and student-managed funds—each offering a chance to build real-world skills that set you apart.
### [Join the Global Trading Competition](https://portal.bloombergforeducation.com/trading_challenges)
Join a team to compete by managing a hypothetical $10M portfolio over 8 weeks, applying real-time strategies. Top teams present at Bloomberg HQ in NYC, with the highest P&L winning the Global Grand Prize.
### [View the recent Global Trading Competition](https://portal.bloombergforeducation.com/trading_challenges)
In the 2024 Bloomberg Global Trading Challenge, over 13,000 students across 2,500+ teams brought their A-game—logging more than 65,000 trades in a fast-paced, real-world investing showdown.
### [360 Huntington Fund](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/programs/360-huntington-fund/)
Apply to join the management team of a real mutual fund. You'll learn invaluable skills in an environment that no simulated fund exercise can match. Earn 1 credit per semester up to three times.
> I earned my Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC) Certification through my Portfolio Management class. In the financial world, Bloomberg is utilized on a daily basis. I have used it at every job. Very few people though, are actually “certified.” My certification will benefit me throughout my career.”
> [Daria Bolotnikova, MS in Finance/MBA'21](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/news/daria-bolotnikova-ms-in-finance-mba21/)
