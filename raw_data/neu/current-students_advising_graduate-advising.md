# Current Students
Get the resources, tools, information and support you need to be your very best at the D'Amore-McKim School of Business.
###  Graduate Advising 
You'll learn best when you are challenged and supported. That's why Northeastern's academic environment is rigorous, guided, and flexible. The Graduate Advising team dedicates itself to supporting you on your journey through higher education and helping you achieve your academic and professional goals. Your dedicated advisor will be with you each step of the way, from planning your schedule to asking tough questions that will challenge your perceptions to help you build your community. 
#### Our professional academic advisors will support you with questions about: 
  * Program curriculum and degree requirements 
  * Course selection aligned with your professional goals with careful attention to ensure academic progression 
  * Interdisciplinary course registration in other Northeastern colleges, if applicable to your program 
  * Wellness resources 
  * Personal and life issues 
  * Anything else on which you need support 


#### They also support your professional growth and network by: 
  * Managing student clubs 
  * Hosting social events where you'll build relationships with fellow students and faculty 
  * Organizing program orientations and events to ease your transition to campus 


We encourage you to read your weekly newsletter, which will keep you informed about events and happenings and help you get to know our community.
