#  The Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in History & Asian StudiesHistory & Asian Studies
History and Asian studies offer an interdisciplinary combined major. Students interested in the combined major in history and Asian studies integrate the exploration of human history with the rigorous study of Asian cultures, societies, languages, and economies.
