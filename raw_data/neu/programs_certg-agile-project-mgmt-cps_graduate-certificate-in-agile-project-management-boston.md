Program overview 
##  Stay ahead of the agile curve 
The increasingly important role of agile practitioners and managers is becoming clear as major companies adopt agile business development processes because of their high degree of success in achieving improved time to market, reducing costs, and increasing overall customer satisfaction.
This graduate certificate is led by highly credentialed faculty members who are agile practitioners with decades of experience in helping companies successfully implement agile in their organizations.
Through online courses, the curriculum will allow you to develop:
  * A strong framework and understanding of the role of agile management
  * A personal leadership strategy for success as an agile practitioner
  * An agile evaluation plan to measure success
