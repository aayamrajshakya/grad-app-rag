## An opportunity engine in Maine
Northeastern aims to create new opportunities for tech professionals at the Roux Institute in Maine. Our AI-focused programs, support for entrepreneurs, and financial backing for research allow Northeastern students to develop their skills and interests in rapidly evolving industries. And with a strong community of peers and professionals who are invested in the region’s growth, it’s easy to find like-minded partners at every step of your learning journey.
##  What makes Portland's campus unique 
While many think of the West Coast when it comes to startup culture, Portland is a great place to advance your career and business ideas while making your mark on a growing tech ecosystem.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://roux.northeastern.edu/)


Powered by tech and AI 
The Roux’s graduate programs are focused on high-tech fields like AI, machine learning, biotechnology, and computer science, preparing students for careers of the future.
An entrepreneurial edge 
Students get firsthand access to entrepreneurship through co-ops, experience-based learning, and startups that are embedded into the campus.
Impactful partnerships 
Industry partners like L.L. Bean, MaineHealth, and The Jackson Laboratory provide real-world learning opportunities and gain in-demand tech talent.
Generous support 
With more than $50 million in funded research and scholarships to date, the Roux Institute is dedicated to supporting research and learning across four research domains and five institutes.
## Fast facts: Northeastern in Portland
Portland offers a strong network of Northeastern alumni, industry partners, and job opportunities for students and graduates alike. 
129 Maine companies that have hired 580+ Northeastern co-op students
5K+ Northeastern alumni in Maine
95 Portfolio startups with $106 million investment capital raised
##  The Institute for Experiential AI 
Northeastern’s [Institute for Experiential AI](https://ai.northeastern.edu/) focuses on solving real-world challenges, advancing research in human-centric AI, and developing solutions that extend human intelligence.
##  Portland campus highlights 
Studying in Portland gives you access to high-tech facilities, natural beauty, and a vibrant community in one of the East Coast’s best cities.
  * [ Learn more about studying in Portland](https://roux.northeastern.edu/our-campus/)


Modern, collaborative spaces 
With multipurpose lab space and an open-concept plan, Northeastern’s Portland campus allows students, researchers, founders, and staff to easily collaborate across disciplines.
An energized community 
Students hail from 20 different countries and make connections through student interest groups, networking events, professional development workshops, and more.
Coastal campus living 
Our waterfront campus is minutes away from nationally recognized restaurants, a stunning coastline, and opportunities for sailing, skiing, and hiking.
