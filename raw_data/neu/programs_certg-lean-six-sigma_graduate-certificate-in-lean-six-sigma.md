Program overview 
##  Lean into six sigma quality 
Ready to become a champion of efficiency? The Graduate Certificate in Lean Six Sigma at Northeastern is your ticket to mastering lean manufacturing thinking and Six Sigma strategies. In just four targeted courses, you’ll learn how to optimize processes, eliminate waste, and improve overall quality.
This program dives into real-world applications, empowering you to make a measurable impact on any organization’s continuous improvement initiatives. With real-world experience and a focus on data-driven decision-making, you’ll be ready to tackle any challenge that comes your way.

Program highlights 
  * Complete four in-depth courses focused on lean manufacturing and Six Sigma concepts.
  * Learn tools and techniques to identify and solve quality control issues across an enterprise.
  * Use this certificate as a gateway to a master’s degree and explore new career opportunities.
  * Boost your earning potential-certificate holders report an average 25% higher salary (source: Washington Post).


Career potential 
Lean Six Sigma expertise is in high demand. Companies everywhere are looking for pros who can optimize processes and enhance quality. With this certificate, you’ll gain a competitive edge and open doors to roles in quality control, process management, and more.
Experiential learning 
Northeastern’s signature experiential learning model puts you right in the action. Work through real-world challenges and gain professional experience in diverse industries. Our co-op program connects you with companies big and small, from corporate giants to innovative startups, where you’ll apply your Lean Six Sigma skills and make a real impact.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Advanced and Intelligent Manufacturing](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-advanced-intelligent-mfg/master-of-science-in-advanced-and-intelligent-manufacturing/)
  * [Master of Science in Data Analytics Engineering](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-data-analytics-engineering/master-of-science-in-data-analytics-engineering/)
  * [Master of Science in Human Factors](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-human-factors/master-of-science-in-human-factors/)
