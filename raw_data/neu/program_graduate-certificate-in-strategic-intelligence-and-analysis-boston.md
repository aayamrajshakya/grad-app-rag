Northeastern University’s Graduate Certificate in Strategic Intelligence and Analysis prepares you for success in security and related fields. There is an ongoing need for professionals trained in strategic intelligence, intelligence operations, and counterintelligence in both the government and private sectors.
You’ll learn from leaders with years of experience gained at organizations including the FBI, CIA, and military. They bring real-world experience to your studies, having served as constitutional scholars, White House policy advisors, security agents and operatives, and military officers. You’ll also gain invaluable insights into the intricacies of intelligence collection and analysis within the context of a democratic society.
### Unique Benefits
  * Study online or in Boston as a part- or full-time student.
  * Build your skills, knowledge, and resumé.
  * Expand your future opportunities with smaller investments of time and money than are required for an advanced degree program.
  * Stack credits from this certificate program into Northeastern’s [Master of Arts in National Security and Intelligence](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ma-national-security-and-intel/master-of-arts-in-national-security-and-intelligence-boston/) program.
