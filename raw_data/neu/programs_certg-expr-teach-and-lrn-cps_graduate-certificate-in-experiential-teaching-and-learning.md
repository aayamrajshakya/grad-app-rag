Program overview 
##  Innovate in experiential learning 
The Graduate Certificate in Experiential Teaching and Learning explores theoretical foundations, approaches, and strategies for learning through experience and how to apply these competencies to foster equity through reflection, analysis, and action. It is designed for K-12 classroom teachers interested in transforming their methods of instruction and assessment to more experience-based pedagogies designed to meet the needs of diverse learners.
Educators interested in school leadership roles will also benefit, as the certificate covers content and skills needed for leading both student and adult experiential learning.
