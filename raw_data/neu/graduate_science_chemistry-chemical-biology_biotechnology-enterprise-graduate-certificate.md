## [2025-2026 Edition](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/)
The graduate certificate in biotechnology enterprise has been designed in response to a need in the biotechnology industry for individuals with a biotechnology background to obtain a strong foundation in the business aspects of biotechnology. Individuals, particularly those who are working in the field of biotechnology, will improve their business competency enabling them to better manage a team or move into a more business-orientated role. 
Complete all courses and requirements listed below unless otherwise indicated.
