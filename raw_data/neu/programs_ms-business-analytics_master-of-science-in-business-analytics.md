Admissions requirements 
How to apply through Fast App (performance-based admissions)
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [Fast App](https://online.northeastern.edu/business/fast-app/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Fast application form
  * Academic transcript(s) from all colleges/universities attended showing a completed bachelor’s degree
  * Complete and earn a B or better in two courses


How to apply using the Standard App
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Standard application form
  * Application fee
  * A personal statement (400–500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all colleges/universities attended showing a completed bachelor’s degree and a minimum 3.0 GPA from an accredited institution
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)


Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
This program builds your technical skills and strategic mindset to analyze data, make smart decisions, and lead innovation in a data-driven business world.
Transform data into insights 
Analyze and leverage data to make informed business decisions. Learn cutting-edge techniques in AI and machine learning to solve complex workplace challenges.
Master data-driven strategies to excel in dynamic industries 
Build the technical and analytical skills to drive innovation and data-based strategies in fast-changing fields like finance, technology, and healthcare.
Cultivate ethical leadership in data-driven environments 
Learn to make informed, ethical, and responsible decisions and harness Big Data to tackle challenges and seize opportunities in tech-driven industries.
Practical application 
Leverage real company data in case studies to explore and resolve critical issues, such as sales growth, marketing enhancement, and infrastructure improvement.
