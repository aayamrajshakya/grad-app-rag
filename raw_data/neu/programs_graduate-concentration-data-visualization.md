## Combine your analytical and creative skills 
Organizations today need professionals with the analytical and creative skills to interpret data and visually present it in clear, accurate, and actionable ways. 
In our forward-thinking MBA x Data Visualization concentration—offered in partnership with [Northeastern's College of Arts, Media and Design](https://camd.northeastern.edu/) (CAMD)—you'll build in-demand skills in visualization principles, programming languages, visual cognition, and more to use data to help organizations make strategic decisions. 
With real-world examples and hands-on programming taught by CAMD professors, you'll transform your insights into impactful visual stories. Plus, design critique sessions offer invaluable feedback from peers, faculty, and industry guests, helping you refine your projects and presentation skills.
