## Develop entrepreneurial skills that drive business success
The Entrepreneurship concentration will equip you with the expertise and perspectives you need to make new business ventures successful—whether you're launching your own startup or helping founders capitalize on their ideas. In this fully customizable concentration, you'll select 12 credits of electives that prepare you to reach your unique goals, with courses covering everything from competitive strategy design to product development to venture financing to business planning to go-to-market strategies to team management and more. 
If you're an aspiring entrepreneur, intrapreneur, or business leader who wants to drive innovation, this concentration is for you. From identifying the potential of an idea to managing the business you create around it, the D'Amore-McKim faculty will prepare you to turn your forward-thinking concept into a real, thriving enterprise. 
## Course requirements
The Entrepreneurship concentration prepares you as an entrepreneurial thinker who can develop and lead successful strategies for new business ventures. Coursework varies by program of interest.
