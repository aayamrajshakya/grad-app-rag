Program overview 
##  Bridging key industry tools 
The Graduate Certificate in Engineering Management at Northeastern University is designed to bridge the gap between engineering, technology, and business. This four-course program empowers you to apply engineering knowledge within management frameworks, focusing on decision-making models, data analysis, and economic-based strategies.
Ideal for those looking to lead technical teams and projects, this certificate prepares you to plan, organize, and direct resources effectively.

Program highlights 
  * Complete four targeted courses focusing on engineering management principles.
  * Meets F-1 international student status requirements.
  * Apply engineering expertise to management settings, enhancing decision-making and planning skills.
  * Boost your career and earnings potential; certificate holders can earn an average of 25% more, according to The Washington Post.
  * Use this certificate as a gateway to a master’s degree, exploring advanced management roles in engineering.


Future outlook 
Specialized knowledge in engineering management opens pathways to leadership roles in diverse industries. With this certificate, you gain a valuable credential recognized by employers seeking strategic thinkers who can guide engineering projects to success.
Experiential learning highlights 
Northeastern’s experiential learning model prepares students for real-world challenges. Through cooperative education (co-op), students gain professional industry experience, working in diverse roles from four to eight months. Access resources like resumé building, interview preparation, and professional development to ensure a successful career in engineering management.

Admissions requirements 
  * Completed online application form
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Transcripts from all institutions attended
    * The Foreign Credential Evaluation (FCE) is a required assessment of all transcripts and documents from non-U.S. accredited post-secondary education institutions.   * Resumé

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Advanced and Intelligent Manufacturing](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-advanced-intelligent-mfg/master-of-science-in-advanced-and-intelligent-manufacturing/)
