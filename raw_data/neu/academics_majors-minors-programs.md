# Majors, Minors, and Graduate Degrees
CSSH offers 17 majors, 40+ minors, 9 doctoral degree programs, 13 Master’s degree programs, and 10 graduate certificates.
[Undergrad Catalog](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/undergraduate/social-sciences-humanities/) [Graduate Catalog](http://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/social-sciences-humanities/)
Your academic journey will be shaped by your discoveries here. It will integrate many [topics](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/topics) and disciplines. As an undergrad, our programs are flexible enough to allow you to pursue a double major, a major and a minor, a [combined major](https://admissions.northeastern.edu/academics/combined-majors/), or an [independent major](https://registrar.northeastern.edu/article/independent-majors/) in cases where interests and goals aren’t met by existing majors. At the graduate level, our programs and certificates cover a wide variety of fields, with the flexibility to build your own path. You can find all our programs in the [undergraduate ](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/resource/undergraduate-catalog/)and [graduate](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/resource/graduate-catalog/) catalogs.

Sequential or combined bachelor & master’s programs, where you can apply undergraduate credits to your graduate degree, or get an accelerated masters degree.
