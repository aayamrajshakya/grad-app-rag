Program overview 
##  Resolve challenges with analytics 
The Master of Professional Studies in Analytics helps meet demand from employers by teaching the skills analysts need to help make data-driven decisions. These include statistics and math, analytics systems technology, business intelligence, advanced analytics, business process/management, business analytics agility, communicating with data, leadership, and data governance, policy, and ethics.
Students apply the principles, tools, and methods of analytics to a project within a sponsoring organization to successfully assist with the extraction, development, delivery, and/or translation/implementation of data analysis for tactical and/or strategic decision making in organizations.
This program was redesigned in 2021 to reflect new trends in the field, supported by faculty and business partners.
