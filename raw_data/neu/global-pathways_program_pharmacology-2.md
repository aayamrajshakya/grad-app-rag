# Pharmacology
[« Return to Programs](https://international.northeastern.edu/global-pathways/programs/)
Graduate education in pharmacology embodies the principles and mechanisms of drug action in biological systems. Through coursework, seminars, and conferences, students in the MS in Pharmacology Program gain exposure to both classical and recent approaches that have led to the development of current theories of drug action and therapeutic application.
If you want to earn your master’s in Pharmacology at Northeastern, Global Pathways can help you succeed through rigorous language training, cultural activities, and support.
