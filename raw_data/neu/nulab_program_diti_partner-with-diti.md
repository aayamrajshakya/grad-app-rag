#  Digital IntegrationTeaching Initiative
A presentation on [Our Marathon](https://marathon.library.northeastern.edu/) for an Advanced Writing in the Disciplines course
The Digital Integration Teaching Initiative (DITI) aims to collaborate with faculty across the CSSH disciplines to introduce students to digital methods and skills at both the undergraduate and graduate levels.
* * *
### For Instructors Interested in Partnering with DITI
DITI partners with faculty across CSSH to integrate digital proficiencies into their classes. See the information below for sample modules, faculty partnership guidelines, and our call for partnerships.
  * See our [modules](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/diti/modules/) page for links to a full list of modules, module definitions, sample modules, and sample student work.
  * Read our [faculty partnership guidelines](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/diti/partner-with-diti/guidelines/).
  * See our [Call for Partnerships](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/nulab/program/diti/partner-with-diti/cfp/).
