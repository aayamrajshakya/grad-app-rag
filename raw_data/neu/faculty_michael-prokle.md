## Biography
Michael Prokle is a Senior Manager and Senior Data Scientist at Fortune Brands Water Innovations. At Fortune Brands WINN, he leads Data Science for Global Supply Chain and supports the digital transformation efforts. He has been a Lecturer at Northeastern Universityâ€™s Analytics and Enterprise Intelligence Program since 2018 and led R&D health care projects at Philips Research North America resulting in multiple patent applications.
Michael Prokle graduated with a BS and MS in Business Engineering from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany, and holds a MS and PhD in Industrial Engineering & Operations Research from the University of Massachusetts Amherst. He studied Innovation and Entrepreneurship at the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. His research interest is in the practical application of operations research and data science.
