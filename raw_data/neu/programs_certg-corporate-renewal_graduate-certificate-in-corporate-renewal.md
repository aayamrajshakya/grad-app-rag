Program overview 
##  Drive business turnarounds 
In today’s dynamic business environment, turnaround management is a highly sought-after specialty. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Corporate Renewal equips you with the skills to help distressed companies through workout agreements, bankruptcies, and liquidations.
In just four or five master’s-level courses, you’ll study value creation, change management, and marketing strategy—the building blocks of transforming troubled companies.
Customize your curriculum with electives like new product development or negotiations to align with your career goals. Through case studies and applied projects, you’ll gain hands-on experience that will enrich your perspective and build your confidence.

Career outlook 
In a global and digital business world, learning new business skills—and refreshing old ones—will be critical to advancing your career. Earning your Graduate Certificate in Corporate Renewal equips you with the specialized, up-to-the-moment knowledge that companies value and demonstrates your commitment to professional development. Your new skill set can serve as a stepping stone to a more significant role, such as a turnaround specialist or operations manager, depending on your work experience.
Experiential learning 
Experience is the signature feature of a Northeastern education. Your courses for the Graduate Certificate in Corporate Renewal will center around case studies and applied project work, allowing you to learn from the experience of real companies and hone your skills in a team setting. You can also strengthen your business knowledge outside the classroom through unique applied opportunities:

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)
