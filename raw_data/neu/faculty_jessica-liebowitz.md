## Biography
Jessica Liebowitz is a Visiting Research Professor in the Center for the Future of Higher Education and Talent Strategy.
Prior to joining Northeastern/CPS, Jessica Liebowitz served as research scientist in the Department of Computer Science at Brandeis University, where she co-founded the Data Science Internal Internship (DSII) and DSII Lab, focusing on student-administrator partnerships in deployments of data science innovations in higher ed operations. Liebowitzâ€™s background includes varied roles in engaging technological change, as a telecom expert in the U.S. Senate, magazine editor for the Gallup Organization, and adjunct professor at the Columbia University Graduate School of Business. 
In addition to her PhD from Harvard, Jessica Liebowitz holds a B.A. from Yale University and an Honorary Degree from Middlebury College.
