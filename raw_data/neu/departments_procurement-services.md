# Procurement Operations
Procurement Operations develops and implements processes and procedures related to buying goods and services for the university. It identifies suppliers as [Diversity Owned (MBE, DBE, VBE, WBE, and SLBE)](https://finance.northeastern.edu/tasks/choose-or-become-a-vendor-2/learn-more-about-supplier-diversity/), educates users about financial systems such as [myMarketplace](https://finance.northeastern.edu/tools/mymarketplace/) and [Banner Finance](https://finance.northeastern.edu/tools/banner/), and provides information about how to use Northeastern-preferred travel planning tools.
Looking for support? Visit our knowledge and service portal, [AskFin Assist](https://service.northeastern.edu/finance).
