#  Northeastern University: The Evolution of the Online Learning Experience 
##  Same degree. Same faculty. Flexible schedule. 
For more than 100 years, Northeastern has been a world leader in experience-driven education. And since 2004, we’ve developed and refined our online learning programs to deliver the same degree online that you’d find on ground.
In this webinar, you’ll learn about the evolution of our online programs, what it’s like to take a class online, tips for excelling in an online learning environment, and more.
