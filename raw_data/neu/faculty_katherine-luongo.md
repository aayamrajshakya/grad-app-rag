#  KatherineLuongo
Associate Professor of History and International Affairs 
Katherine Luongo is a specialist in the anthropological history of Kenya. She studies legal systems in colonial and contemporary Africa, global legal regimes, and human rights. She is the author of Witchcraft and Colonial Rule in Kenya, 1900-1955Obama and Kenya: Contested Histories of Politics and BelongingAfrican Witchcraft and Global Asylum-Seeking: Border-Crossing BeliefsLaw without Justice: A History of Human Rights in Kenya
