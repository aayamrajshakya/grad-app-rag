Program overview 
##  Tailored business education 
Today’s businesses need professionals who can adapt, lead, and make strategic decisions. The Online Graduate Certificate in Business Administration helps you gain these skills through 12 credits across four graduate-level courses. With the advice of your enrollment advisors, you will tailor your course of study either within a specific business discipline or across disciplines.
You can customize your learning experience by selecting classes in:
  * Finance
  * Marketing
  * Healthcare management
  * Innovation entrepreneurship
  * International management


This flexible online format lets you study at your own pace while building skills to advance your career. Apply your credits toward an eligible master’s program, like the Online MBA, for future growth.

Admissions requirements 
How to apply through Fast App (performance-based admissions)
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [Fast App](https://online.northeastern.edu/business/fast-app/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Fast application form
  * Academic transcript(s) from all colleges/universities attended showing a completed bachelor’s degree
  * Complete and earn a B or better in two courses


How to apply using the Standard App
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Standard application form
  * Application fee
  * A personal statement (400–500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all colleges/universities attended showing a completed bachelor’s degree and a minimum 3.0 GPA from an accredited institution
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)


Contact an enrollment counselor to learn more about eligibility requirements.
