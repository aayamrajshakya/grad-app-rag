#  About the program 
If you are a motivated student looking to enhance your English language skills, the Global Pathways program will help you reach your goal. Global Pathways is an intensive academic program that helps you improve your English while becoming comfortable with the culture of an American university. You will strengthen your English language and professional communication skills in a range of areas, such as critical reading, public speaking, academic writing, research, and critical thinking. You will also take a course in your discipline that can then be transferred to your master’s degree. When you successfully complete the program’s requirements, you will be admitted to the graduate degree program of your choice. Learn more about the Global Pathways curriculum.
