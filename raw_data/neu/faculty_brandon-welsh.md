#  BrandonWelsh
Dean's Professor of Criminology; Director, Cambridge-Somerville Youth Study; Co-Director, Crime Prevention Lab 
Dr. Brandon C. Welsh is the Dean’s Professor of Criminology in the College of Social Sciences and Humanities, School of Criminology and Criminal Justice, at Northeastern University. He serves as Director of the Cambridge-Somerville Youth Study and Co-Director of the Crime Prevention Lab. From 2022 to 2024, he was the Visiting Professor of Global Health and Social Medicine at Harvard Medical School. 
His research and teaching focus on the prevention of delinquency, crime, and interpersonal violence, as well as evidence-based social policy. He is the author or editor of 13 books, including Between Medicine and Criminology: Richard Cabot and the Making of the Cambridge-Somerville Youth Study (Oxford University Press, 2025). 
Dr. Welsh holds a Ph.D. in Criminology from the University of Cambridge.
