Before completing this certificate program, you will also develop a personal leadership strategy and agile evaluation plan to measure your success as an agile practitioner.
The increasingly important role of agile practitioners and managers is growing. Agile business development processes are being adopted by major companies who have discovered they are highly successful in:
  * achieving improved time to market
  * reducing costs
  * increasing overall customer satisfaction


This graduate certificate is led by highly credentialed faculty members who themselves are agile practitioners with decades of experience in helping companies successfully implement agile structures in their organizations.
### Program objectives
  * Build a strong framework and understanding of the role of agile management
  * Understand the agile management processes and methodologies
  * Understand how the agile approach to managing projects can deliver value to the organization
  * Develop a personal leadership strategy
  * Design and agile evaluation plan to measure success as an agile practitioner


### Unique Features
  1. Courses are led by highly credentialed faculty who are agile practitioners
  2. Curriculum focuses on current trends and staying up to date with agile framework
