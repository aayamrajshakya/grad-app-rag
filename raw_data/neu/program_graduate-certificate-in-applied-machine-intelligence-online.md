As data continues to grow dramatically across all sectors of the global economy, leaders will need to understand how to use that data for continuous improvement and analysis. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Applied Machine Intelligence focuses on the knowledge, skills, and tools needed to succeed in AI and data mining. You’ll study a wide range of data applications, strategies, and systems technologies utilized in AI while putting your skills to work through data-driven projects.
#### Program Benefits
  * Gain a competitive edge in the growing AI market, which is expected to grow at a rate of nearly 27% each year until 2031 ([Statista](https://www.statista.com/outlook/tmo/artificial-intelligence/worldwide#:~:text=Artificial%20Intelligence%20-%20Worldwide&text=The%20market%20size%20is%20expected%2cUS$826.70bn%20by%202030.)).
  * Open the door to higher earning potential, with specialized AI salaries often reaching $155,000 ([Glassdoor](https://www.coursera.org/articles/artificial-intelligence-salary)).
  * Courses from this program can be applied to the [Master of Professional Studies in Applied AI](https://cps.northeastern.edu/program/master-of-professional-studies-in-applied-ai-boston/).
