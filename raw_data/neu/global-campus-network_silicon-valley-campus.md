## Energizing the Bay Area’s startup ecosystem
Northeastern’s forward-thinking approach to education and Silicon Valley’s strong history of nurturing innovative technology go hand in hand. Here, students immerse themselves in learning experiences within the world’s largest concentration of startups, unicorn companies, and major tech corporations. They develop their skills as critical thinkers and leaders within their industries, then create human-centered, tech-enabled solutions to some of the world’s most pressing issues.
##  What makes Silicon Valley unique 
There’s always something new happening in Silicon Valley’s tech industry, and Northeastern students are uniquely positioned to advance their careers right in the middle of the action.
  * [ Visit the campus website](https://siliconvalley.northeastern.edu/)


Local industry 
Silicon Valley is home to a fast-moving economy based on tech, entrepreneurship, venture capital, and research and development.
On-campus events 
Take advantage of our active events calendar, and connect with community and industry partners, alumni, and cultural opportunities.
Career support 
Our career services team understands the Bay Area’s specialized economy and helped more than 270 students land co-ops and internships during the 2023–2024 school year.
Bay Area access 
Students can easily reach all that the Bay Area has to offer as one of the most diverse regions in the U.S., from unique living communities to varied career choices.

##  Startup Space @ Silicon Valley 
[Startup Space](https://svstartupspace.sites.northeastern.edu/) is a co-working office for selected ventures led by Northeastern alumni or students, connecting them with university staff and resources.
##  Silicon Valley campus highlights
