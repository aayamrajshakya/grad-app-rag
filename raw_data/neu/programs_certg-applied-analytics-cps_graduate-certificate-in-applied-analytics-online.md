Program overview 
##  Turn data into compelling stories 
In a global environment characterized by digital transformation, rapid change, and high levels of uncertainty, the ability to hire, reskill, and upskill analytic talent is a major driver of organizational performance. The online Graduate Certificate in Applied Analytics in the College of Professional Studies will prepare students to develop analytical skills to support decision-making in an organization’s strategy. The certification focuses on analytical, technological, and humanic literacies-emphasizing experiential learning-required for students to serve as strategic business partners in their organizations.

Program objectives 
##  Prepare for success 
You’ll gain the foundational knowledge and skills critical to pursuing data analytics as a profession in relation to statistics and math.
Confidently communicate data findings 
Articulate and effectively defend the significance and implications of work in data analytics in terms of challenges and trends in a local, national, or global context.
Gain in-demand skills and knowledge 
Apply the principles, tools, and analytics methods to a comprehensive real-world problem or project related to data analyses for tactical and/or strategic decision-making.
Create data-driven insights 
Integrate the major theories, tools, and approaches in data analytics to identify data-driven insights for informed business process management.
Create compelling stories 
Design and deliver presentations, reports, and recommendations that effectively translate technical results and data solutions, and are coherent and persuasive to different audiences.
