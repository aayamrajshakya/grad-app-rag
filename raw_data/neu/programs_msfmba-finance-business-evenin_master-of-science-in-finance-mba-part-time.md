Program overview 
##  Two degrees, big impact 
This program enhances analytical abilities, fosters creativity, and sharpens problem-solving skills, preparing you to become an effective business leader. Under the guidance of industry experts, you’ll learn to minimize risk, maximize ROI, and sustain growth. The program is designed for working professionals and offers the flexibility to balance your commitments.
You’ll earn an MS in Finance and an MBA simultaneously, with the advantage of counting nine credits for both degrees. Through your MBA coursework, you’ll become a responsible business leader capable of navigating and creating in a digital world. Through your MS in finance, you’ll hone your skills as a financial decision-maker who can drive financial strategy, increase shareholder value, and support flexible operating models.

Career outlook 
Expand your career opportunities
The D’Amore-McKim part-time MS in Finance/MBA program prepares you for leadership roles in the financial services industry by giving you a dual degree that doubles your expertise. The essential management abilities you’ll gain by adding an MBA to your finance skills will make you stand out and help you become a versatile finance leader. Depending on your experience, your strong finance skills and business savvy can lead to senior roles like portfolio manager, investment banker, financial consultant, or hedge fund manager—highly sought-after, well-paid positions with great career advancement opportunities. Our graduates are heavily recruited by companies of all sizes, from startups to multinational organizations.
Strengthen your network
You’ll forge a strong Boston-based network while continuing to advance your career. Your classmates will be aspiring finance professionals who span the breadth of the financial services world. This network can provide valuable connections with industry professionals, potential mentors, and peers who can offer career insights and opportunities.

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * Two letters of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Virtual interview (by invitation only)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)
