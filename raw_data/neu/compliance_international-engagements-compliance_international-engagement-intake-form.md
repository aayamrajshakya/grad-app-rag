# International Engagements and Collaborations
[NU-RES Compliance](https://compliance.research.northeastern.edu "Go to NU-RES Compliance.") > International Engagements Disclosures
### University Policy
When entering an international engagement and/or collaboration, it is important to follow the correct steps and procedures for getting the engagement/collaboration cleared. NU-RES Research Compliance will help with any due diligence checks (including restricted party screening) for any international engagements/collaborations you may want to engage in from the individual level to the institutional level. If you have an international engagement that you are considering entering in, please complete the below International Engagement Intake Form.
