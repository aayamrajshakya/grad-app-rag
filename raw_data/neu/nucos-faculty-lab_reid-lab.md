#  Reid Lab 
Led by Dr. Brie Reid, we study how climate stress, environmental stressors, water insecurity, metal exposures, climate-change induced food insecurity can impact human development through prenatal/postnatal stress physiology and nutrient mechanisms.
[Visit Website ](https://reid-lab.com/)
## People
  * ### [Brie Reid](https://cos.northeastern.edu/people/brie-reid/)
Dr. Brie Reid is a developmental psychobiologist studying how early social experiences and nutrition shape human development. Her research investigates how human physiology adapts to social and nutritional contexts across the lifespan, focusing on stress, the immune system, and nutrient metabolism.
