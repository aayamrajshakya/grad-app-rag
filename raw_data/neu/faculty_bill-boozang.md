## Biography
Bill Boozang has worked in education for 25 years, and currently serves as Senior Lecturer and DiP Chair at the College for Professional Studies, in addition to his role as Executive Director of Graduate & Continuing Education at Curry College in Milton, Massachusetts.
Bill has previously served as dissertation chair for Ed.D. programs at Condordia-Portland, University of New England and Gratz College. Having worked in a variety of administrative roles at both small and large institutions, Bill has an interest in creating opportunities for affordable and accessible post-secondary education.
Bill is also Past-President for the [Association for Continuing Higher Education](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.acheinc.org%2F&data=05%7C01%7Cw.boozang%40northeastern.edu%7C1dbabfd24a4e44e9d77b08da857a695d%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C637969058069023678%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=mqJ4hgIb98nmgFRXDM6Xv4BwJXYw2aoNPlqpkpzSa%2FI%3D&reserved=0), served on the Board of Governors for Quincy College (MA), and currently is on the Editorial Board for the [Journal of Continuing Higher Education](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tandfonline.com%2Fjournals%2Fujch20&data=05%7C01%7Cw.boozang%40northeastern.edu%7C1dbabfd24a4e44e9d77b08da857a695d%7Ca8eec281aaa34daeac9b9a398b9215e7%7C0%7C0%7C637969058069023678%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=gq5%2FlT%2FOm8Vu3nAcICExJLTPsazcfPdaz3X%2FoUZxp6o%3D&reserved=0).
