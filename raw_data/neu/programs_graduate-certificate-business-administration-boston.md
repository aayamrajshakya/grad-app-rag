###  Why get a graduate certificate? 
## Flexible, when life can't be
In today's fast-moving business landscape, staying competitive means staying current. D'Amore-McKim's Graduate Certificate in Business Administration gives you the flexibility to choose the courses that match your goals, so you gain the skills you need, when you need them. In just 12 to 15 credits, you'll build essential business knowledge, earn a respected credential, and apply what you learn immediately in your work. It's a smart, customizable path to career growth. 
## Program options
The Graduate Certificate in Business Administration is built for flexibility so you can shape your learning around your goals. Choose any course offered on our Boston campus for which you meet the prerequisites, or follow a curated curriculum designed by our expert faculty. Whether you want to explore a specific field like marketing, supply chain, or entrepreneurship, or mix and match across disciplines, you'll gain relevant skills, real-world experience, and a credential that moves your career forward. Complete your certificate in as little as four months or take your time—this is graduate education that fits your life. 
Build your own 
This flexible option lets you choose from a wide range of graduate business courses offered on your campus. You pick the classes that matter most to you, creating a personalized curriculum that fits your career goals and interests. You can move at your own pace, focus on what's important to you, and gain new skills in areas that inspire you or support your professional growth. 
[Explore Sample Course offerings](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/DMSB_GCBA_CurriculumMap_FNL-page-BYO.pdf)
Are you an international student? 
### Consider our 8-month international student cohort 
Designed exclusively for international students, this full-time, 8-month program offers a structured, cohort-based set of graduate-level courses taught sequentially on Northeastern's Boston campus. This is your chance to experience U.S. business firsthand as you study and live in the U.S. You'll study alongside a group of global peers, gain essential business skills, and immerse yourself in American business culture. 
[Download a sample curriculum](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/8month_international_cohort.pdf)
Do you want to study abroad in the U.S. for one term? 
### Consider our accelerated 4-month option
Ideal for international MBA students, you can study full-time for a single semester in Boston. Study abroad, earn credits, and expand your global perspective. You'll take graduate-level courses alongside MS and MBA students, gain exposure to U.S. business practices, and transfer your credits back to your home institution, making this a powerful way to enrich your education and broaden your career horizons.

## Applicant FAQs
Are graduate business certificates worth it? 
Graduate certificates can be a worthwhile investment as they can enhance specific skills, boost career advancement opportunities, and offer flexible, cost-effective education. These programs are ideal for professionals seeking targeted knowledge without the commitment of a full degree, providing networking opportunities and the potential to stand out to employers in a short-term timeframe.
[Learn more about certificates](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/resources/are-graduate-business-certificates-worth-it/)
Graduate certificate vs. master's degree: What business credential is right for me? 
Exploring graduate education options is an important step towards advancing your career, gaining specialized knowledge, and enhancing your professional qualifications. If you're contemplating graduate-level education, you might be weighing the benefits of pursuing a graduate certificate versus a master's degree. Learn more about these distinct types of graduate credentials, the key differences between a graduate certificate and a master's degree, and the pros and cons of each option.

Is an interview required for admission? 
Interviews are rarely required for admission to the Graduate Certificate in Business Administration program. However, the Admissions team may request one if necessary.
Is the GMAT or GRE required for admission? 
Graduate certificate programs do not require GRE or GMAT scores for admission.
Is English language proficiency required? 
Yes, English proficiency—both written and verbal—is necessary for success in D'Amore-McKim classrooms. If you are not a native English speaker, we require a standardized test of English language proficiency unless you completed a bachelor's or master's degree at an accredited institution where English is the language of instruction, or you have worked full-time for two years in the United States or other native English-speaking environment.
[Learn more on our FAQ page](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/international-graduate-students/)
Are there other application requirements for international students? 
Yes. International students must meet additional requirements, including proof of English language proficiency, a valid passport, and English translations of academic transcripts. Our [international graduate students page](https://damore-mckim.northeastern.edu/admissions/faq/international-graduate-students/) provides more information about applying and the benefits of choosing D'Amore-McKim.
