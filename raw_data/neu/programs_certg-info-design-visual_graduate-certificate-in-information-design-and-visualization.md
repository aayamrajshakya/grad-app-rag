Program overview 
##  Turn data into visual narratives 
Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Information Design and Visualization prepares you to transform data into meaningful visual stories. Through advanced data analytics and design principles, you’ll learn how to create effective information environments that engage stakeholders and communicate research across various fields.
This program is designed for professionals from media, design, communications, and data science, equipping them with the skills to think visually and produce clear, impactful visualizations. Embedded in the MFA program, you’ll have the chance to collaborate, attend guest lectures, and participate in workshops, positioning you to meet the growing demand for data-savvy visual storytellers in today’s information-driven world.

This program is designed for professionals from media, design, communications, and data science, equipping them with the skills to think visually and produce clear, impactful visualizations. Embedded in the MFA program, you’ll have the chance to collaborate, attend guest lectures, and participate in workshops, positioning you to meet the growing demand for data-savvy visual storytellers in today’s information-driven world.
Program highlights 
  * Gain real-world experience with a variety of programming languages, tools, and platforms for creating effective visualizations.
  * Learn to critically assess visualization methods to match specific data sources and objectives.
  * Develop skills with different data formats, including quantitative, qualitative, textual, relational, hierarchical, or spatial data.
  * Access workshops and guest lectures, joining a vibrant community of students in the MFA program.


Career potential 
As the “data revolution” reshapes industries, the demand for professionals skilled in information design and visualization is soaring. Whether in global companies like Google and IBM or public sector institutions, you’ll be equipped to take on roles that translate complex data into engaging, accessible insights.
Experiential learning 
Northeastern’s signature experiential learning model blends academics with professional practice, giving you immediate, actionable skills. Engage in projects, workshops, and collaborations with MFA students to refine your visual communication expertise. This immersive learning prepares you to excel in a fast-paced, data-driven landscape.

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
