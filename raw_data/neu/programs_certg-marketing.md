Program overview 
##  Engage your audience 
In today’s fast-paced digital landscape, marketing is all about staying ahead. Northeastern’s Graduate Certificate in Marketing gives you the tools and strategies to confidently connect with your target audience and position your company for success. In just four or five courses, you’ll learn how to build a comprehensive marketing strategy that resonates in the competitive marketplace.
You’ll be guided by renowned faculty who are not just educators but experienced marketing practitioners and thought leaders. Through real-world case studies, you’ll gain insights into what works and how to apply those strategies effectively. Choose electives tailored to your career goals, such as market research, new product development, or B2B sales, to build a versatile skill set.

Admissions requirements 
To apply, log in—or create an account—on our [application portal](https://enroll.northeastern.edu/apply/), choose your program, and answer a few questions. You’ll need to submit:
  * Application form
  * A personal statement (<=500 words)
  * Resumé
  * One letter of recommendation
  * Academic transcript(s) from all undergraduate and graduate schools attended
  * Kira online interview (by invitation, access provided after submitting your application)
  * Optional GMAT/GRE test scores
  * TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo, or PTE Academic (if applicable)
  * Passport or permanent residency card (if applicable)

Degree building 
##  Stack your certificate 
Earning a graduate certificate is a fast and affordable way to acquire specialized skills while building toward your master’s degree. Upon completion of this certificate program, you may be able to apply your credits to an eligible master’s program, saving time and money on your degree.
Some of the eligible master’s programs for this certificate include:
  * [Master of Science in Management](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/ms-management/master-of-science-in-management/)
  * [Part-time MBA](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/mba-business-admin-evening/part-time-mba/)
  * [Master of Science in Finance/MBA – Part-Time](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msfmba-finance-business-evenin/master-of-science-in-finance-mba-part-time/)


Contact an enrollment counselor to learn more about eligibility requirements.
