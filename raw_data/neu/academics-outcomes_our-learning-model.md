## Education + Experience
The world values education and experience. Get both with a graduate degree from Northeastern, where you’ll combine rigorous coursework with hands-on learning opportunities that give you a competitive edge in the workforce.
Our graduate students put their classroom knowledge to work through co-op and XN projects at Fortune 500 and startup companies, government agencies, nonprofits, and global organizations. They tackle today’s most pressing challenges through innovation and discovery at our world-class research centers and institutes. And throughout it all, they’re supported by a global network of expert faculty, employer partners, and alumni dedicated to their success.
Both inside the classroom and out, you’ll refine technical skills, learn to solve problems in multiple contexts, act nimbly, work with teams, and gain resilience. You’ll collaborate with industry professionals, giving you a foot in the door at leading companies and professional connections for life. Best of all, you’ll gain real professional work experience to add to your resumé—making you a standout to employers.
