#  Guidance & Support 
Throughout Northeastern, you’ll find departments, services, faculty, and students who are dedicated to helping you transition smoothly into the Northeastern community.
Office of Global Services 
Adjust to living and learning in the U.S. with help from the Office of Global Services (OGS). OGS offers a range of support specifically for international students so you can feel safe, confident, and at home.
  * [ Visit the OGS website](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/)


Office of Global Student Success 
Access a variety of services and resources, including tutoring, workshops, and classes, to help you thrive at Northeastern and beyond.
  * [ Visit the GSS website](https://international.northeastern.edu/gss/)


##  On-campus resources 
**[International Tutoring Center](https://international.northeastern.edu/gss/tutoring/)  
**Receive comprehensive English-language and academic support through one-on-one and group sessions with professional tutors.
**[Workshops](https://international.northeastern.edu/gss/workshops/)  
**Polish your resumé, improve your English skills, transition to learning and living in the U.S., and more through Global Student Success workshops.
**[Global Student Mentor Program](https://international.northeastern.edu/ogs/global-student-mentor-program/)  
**Connect with a current student for support as you adjust to college life, find and access resources, develop study skills, and more.
