# Experiential learning
Quick Links
## The leader in global experiential learning 
No other university offers the range of opportunities to earn experience that we do. And we continue to evolve those opportunities through our expanding worldwide network of campuses, partners, and alumni. It’s how we empower you to learn and solve problems with the agility and global mindset our complex world requires.
  * Learn in new contexts and apply your knowledge to real-world problems. 
  * Take part in opportunities that span—and positively impact—the world. 
  * Boost skills, agility, and connections to navigate what’s next for you. 


## Co-op
Discover our renowned global co-op program. Alternate academics with periods of professional work to explore career paths, gain invaluable experience, and make connections.
[Learn more about co-op ](https://www.northeastern.edu/experiential-learning/co-op/)
